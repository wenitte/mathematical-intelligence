Theorem
Let $\cos t$ be the cosine of $t$, where $t \in \R$.

Then $\cos t$ is of exponential order $0$.


Proof
The result follows from Real Cosine Function is Bounded and Bounded Function is of Exponential Order Zero.
$\blacksquare$
