Example of Kaprekar Triple
$45$ is a Kaprekar triple.


Proof













\(\ds 45^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 91 \, 125\)




















\(\ds 45\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 9 + 11 + 25\)









$\blacksquare$
