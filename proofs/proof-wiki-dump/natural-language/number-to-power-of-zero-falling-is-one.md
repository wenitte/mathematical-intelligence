Theorem
Let $x \in \R$ be a real number.

$x^{\underline 0} = 1$
where $x^{\underline 0}$ denotes the falling factorial.


Proof













\(\ds x^{\underline 0}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \prod_{j \mathop = 0}^{-1} \paren {x - j}\)





Definition of Falling Factorial














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)





Product is Vacuous



$\blacksquare$
