Example of Algebraic Number
The golden mean $\phi = \dfrac {1 + \sqrt 5} 2$ is an algebraic number.


Proof
From Golden Mean as Root of Quadratic:

$\phi$ is a root of $x^2 - x - 1 = 0$.
Hence the result by definition of algebraic number.
$\blacksquare$
