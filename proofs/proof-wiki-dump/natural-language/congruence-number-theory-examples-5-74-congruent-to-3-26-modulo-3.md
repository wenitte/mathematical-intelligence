Example of Congruence Modulo an Integer
$5 \cdotp 74 \equiv -3 \cdotp 26 \pmod 3$


Proof













\(\ds 5 \cdotp 74\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 3 - 3 \cdotp 26\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 5 \cdotp 74\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds -3 \cdotp 26\)

\(\ds \pmod 3\)







$\blacksquare$
