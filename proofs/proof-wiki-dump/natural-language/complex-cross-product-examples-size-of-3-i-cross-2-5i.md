Example of Complex Cross Product
Let:

$z_1 = 3 - i$
$z_2 = 2 + 5 i$
Then:

$\size {z_1 \times z_2} = 17$
where:

$\circ$ denotes (complex) cross product
$\size {\, \cdot \,}$ denotes the absolute value function.


Proof













\(\ds z_1 \times z_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 17\)





Complex Cross Product: $3 - i \times 2 + 5 i$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \size {z_1 \times z_2}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \size {17}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 17\)









$\blacksquare$
