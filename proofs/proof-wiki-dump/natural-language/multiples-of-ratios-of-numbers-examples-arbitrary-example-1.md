Examples of Multiples of Ratios of Numbers
We have:

$12 : 15$ is the same ratio as $4 : 5$, and also the same ratio as $24 : 30$.


Proof
We have:














\(\ds 12 : 15\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 \times 4} : \paren {3 \times 5}\)





Multiples of Ratios of Numbers














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {6 \times 4} : \paren {6 \times 5}\)





Multiples of Ratios of Numbers



$\blacksquare$
