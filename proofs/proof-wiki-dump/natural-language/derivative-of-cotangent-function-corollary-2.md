Corollary to Derivative of Cotangent Function
$\dfrac \d {\d x} \cot x = -1 - \cot^2 x$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac \d {\d x} \cot x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc^2 x\)





Derivative of $\cot x$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {\cot^2 x + 1}\)





Difference of Squares of Cosecant and Cotangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -1 - \cot^2 x\)





rearranging



$\blacksquare$
