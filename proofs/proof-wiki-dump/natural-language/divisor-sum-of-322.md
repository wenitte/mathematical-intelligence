Example of Divisor Sum of Square-Free Integer
$\map {\sigma_1} {322} = 576$
where $\sigma_1$ denotes the divisor sum function.


Proof
We have that:

$322 = 2 \times 7 \times 23$

Hence:














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {322}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2 + 1} \paren {7 + 1} \paren {23 + 1}\)





Divisor Sum of Square-Free Integer














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 8 \times 24\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 2^3 \times \paren {2^3 \times 3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^6 \times 3^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2^3 \times 3}^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 24^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 576\)









$\blacksquare$
