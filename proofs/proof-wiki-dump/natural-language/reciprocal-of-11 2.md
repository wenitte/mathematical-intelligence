Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $11$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {11} = 0 \cdotp \dot 0 \dot 9$
This sequence is A010680 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).


Proof
Performing the calculation using long division:

      ...

$\blacksquare$
