Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $361$ and $1178$ is:

$\gcd \set {361, 1178} = 19$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 1178\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 361 + 95\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 361\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 95 + 76\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 95\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 76 + 19\)










\(\text {(4)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 76\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 \times 19\)









Thus:

$\gcd \set {361, 1178} = 19$
$\blacksquare$
