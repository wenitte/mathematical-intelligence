Theorem
$\cos i = \dfrac e 2 + \dfrac 1 {2 e}$


Proof













\(\ds \cos i\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cosh 1\)





Hyperbolic Cosine in terms of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {e^1 + e^{-1} } 2\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac e 2 + \frac 1 {2 e}\)









$\blacksquare$
