Theorem
An integer $N$ expressed in decimal notation is divisible by $6$ if and only if

$N$ is even
and:

$N$ is divisible by $3$.


Proof
We have that:

$6 \divides N \iff 2 \divides N \land 3 \divides N$
The result follows by definition of even integer.
$\blacksquare$
