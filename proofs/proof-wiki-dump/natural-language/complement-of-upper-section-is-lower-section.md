Theorem
Let $\struct {S, \preceq}$ be an ordered set.
Let $L$ be an upper section.

Then $S \setminus L$ is a lower section.


Proof
This follows mutatis mutandis from the proof of Complement of Lower Section is Upper Section.
$\blacksquare$
