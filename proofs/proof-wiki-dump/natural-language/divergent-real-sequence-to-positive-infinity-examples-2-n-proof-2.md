Example of Divergent Real Sequence to Positive Infinity
Let $\sequence {a_n}_{n \mathop \ge 1}$ be the real sequence defined as:

$a_n = 2^n$

Then $\sequence {a_n}$ is divergent to $+\infty$.


Proof
Let $H \in \R_{>0}$ be given.
We have that:

$2^n > n$
so setting $N = H$:

$\exists N \in \N: 2^n > H$
for all $n > N$.
$\blacksquare$
