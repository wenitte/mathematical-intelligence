Theorem
$\sec 315 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {7 \pi} 4 = \sqrt 2$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 315 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 45 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 45 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 2\)





Secant of $45 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
