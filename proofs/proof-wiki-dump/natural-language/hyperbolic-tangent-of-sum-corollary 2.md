Corollary to Hyperbolic Tangent of Sum
$\map \tanh {a - b} = \dfrac {\tanh a - \tanh b} {1 - \tanh a \tanh b}$
where $\tanh$ denotes the hyperbolic tangent.


Proof













\(\ds \map \tanh {a - b}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\tanh a + \map \tanh {-b} } {1 + \tanh a \, \map \tanh {-b} }\)





Hyperbolic Tangent of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\tanh a - \tanh b} {1 - \tanh a \tanh b}\)





Hyperbolic Tangent Function is Odd



$\blacksquare$
