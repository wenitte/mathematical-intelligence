Theorem
$\ds \int \cosh a x \rd x = \frac {\sinh a x} a + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \cosh x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sinh x + C\)





Primitive of $\cosh x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \cosh a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \paren {\sinh a x} + C\)





Primitive of Function of Constant Multiple














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sinh a x} a + C\)





simplifying



$\blacksquare$
