Example of Quadratic Equation
The quadratic equation in $\C$:

$z^2 + \paren {i - 2} z + \paren {3 - i} = 0$
has the solutions:

$z = \begin {cases} 1 + i \\ 1 - 2 i \end {cases}$


Proof
From the Quadratic Formula:














\(\ds z^2 + \paren {i - 2} z + \paren {3 - i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds z\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {-\paren {i - 2} \pm \sqrt {\paren {i - 2}^2 - 4 \times 1 \times \paren {3 - i} } } {2 \times 1}\)





Quadratic Formula: $a = 1$, $b = i - 2$, $c = 3 - i$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {2 - i \pm \sqrt {4 + i^2 - 4 i - 12 + 4 i} } 2\)





simplifying














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {2 - i \pm \sqrt {-9} } 2\)





simplifying














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {2 - i \pm 3 i} 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \begin {cases} 1 - 2 i \\ 1 + i \end {cases}\)









$\blacksquare$
