Theorem
$\map \cos {x + \pi} = -\cos x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \cos {x + \pi}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos x \cos \pi - \sin x \sin \pi\)





Cosine of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos x \cdot \paren {-1} - \sin x \cdot 0\)





Cosine of Straight Angle and Sine of Straight Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cos x\)









$\blacksquare$
