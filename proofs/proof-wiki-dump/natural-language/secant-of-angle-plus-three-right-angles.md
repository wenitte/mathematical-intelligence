Theorem
$\map \sec {x + \dfrac {3 \pi} 2} = \csc x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \sec {x + \frac {3 \pi} 2}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\map \cos {x + \frac {3 \pi} 2} }\)





Secant is Reciprocal of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\sin x}\)





Cosine of Angle plus Three Right Angles














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \csc x\)





Cosecant is Reciprocal of Sine



$\blacksquare$
