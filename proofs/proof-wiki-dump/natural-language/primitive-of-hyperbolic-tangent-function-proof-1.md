Theorem
$\ds \int \tanh x \rd x = \map \ln {\cosh x} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \tanh x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \frac {\sinh x} {\cosh x} \rd x\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \frac {\paren {\cosh x}'} {\cosh x} \rd x\)





Derivative of Hyperbolic Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\cosh x} + C\)





Primitive of Function under its Derivative














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \ln {\cosh x} + C\)





Graph of Hyperbolic Cosine Function: $\cosh x > 0$ for all $x$



$\blacksquare$
