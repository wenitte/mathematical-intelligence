Theorem
$\cos 330 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {11 \pi} 6 = \dfrac {\sqrt 3} 2$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 330 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {360 \degrees - 30 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 30 \degrees\)





Cosine of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sqrt 3} 2\)





Cosine of $30 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
