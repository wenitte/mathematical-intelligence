Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $43$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {43} = 0 \cdotp \dot 02325 \, 58139 \, 53488 \, 37209 \, \dot 3$
This sequence is A021047 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
