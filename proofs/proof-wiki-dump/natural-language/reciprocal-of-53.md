Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $53$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {53} = 0 \cdotp \dot 01886 \, 79245 \, 28 \dot 3$
This sequence is A021057 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
