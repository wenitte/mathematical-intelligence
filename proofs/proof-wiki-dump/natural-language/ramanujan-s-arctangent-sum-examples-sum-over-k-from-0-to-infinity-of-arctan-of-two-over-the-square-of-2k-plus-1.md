Example of Use of Ramanujan's Arctangent Sum













\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 0}^\infty \map \arctan {\dfrac 2 {\paren {2 k + 1}^2} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \arctan 2 + \map \arctan {\dfrac 2 9} + \map \arctan {\dfrac 2 {25} } + \map \arctan {\dfrac 2 {49} } + \cdots\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac \pi 2\)











Proof













\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 0}^\infty \map \arctan {\dfrac 2 {\paren {n + 2 k + 1}^2} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 n}\)





Ramanujan's Arctangent Sum: Corollary








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 0}^\infty \map \arctan {\dfrac 2 {\paren {2 k + 1}^2} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \arctan \infty\)





setting $n$ to $0$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 0}^\infty \map \arctan {\dfrac 2 {\paren {2 k + 1}^2} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac \pi 2\)









$\blacksquare$
