Theorem
$\cot 255 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {17 \pi} {12} = 2 - \sqrt 3$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 255 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cot {360 \degrees - 105 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 105 \degrees\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 - \sqrt 3\)





Cotangent of $105 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
