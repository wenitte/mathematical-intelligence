Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $119$ and $272$ is found to be:

$\gcd \set {119, 272} = 17$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 272\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 119 + 34\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 119\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 34 + 17\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 34\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 17\)










Thus:

$\gcd \set {119, 272} = 17$
$\blacksquare$
