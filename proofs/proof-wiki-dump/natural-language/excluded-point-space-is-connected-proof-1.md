Theorem
Let $T = \left({S, \tau_{\bar p}}\right)$ be an excluded point space.

Then $T^*_{\bar p}$ is a connected space.


Proof
We have:

Excluded Point Topology is Open Extension Topology of Discrete Topology
Open Extension Space is Connected
$\blacksquare$
