Example of Use of Cosine minus Cosine
$\cos 2 A - \cos 4 A = 2 \sin 3 A \sin A$


Proof













\(\ds \cos 2 A - \cos 4 A\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -2 \map \sin {\dfrac {2 A + 4 A} 2} \map \sin {\dfrac {2 A - 4 A} 2}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -2 \sin 3 A \map \sin {-A}\)





evaluating














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \sin 3 A \sin A\)





Sine Function is Odd



$\blacksquare$
