Example of Complex Dot Product
Let:

$z_1 = 2 + 5 i$
$z_2 = 3 - i$
Then:

$\size {z_1 \circ z_2} = 1$
where:

$\circ$ denotes (complex) dot product
$\size {\, \cdot \,}$ denotes the absolute value function.


Proof













\(\ds z_1 \circ z_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)





Complex Dot Product: $2 + 5 i \circ 3 - i$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \size {z_1 \circ z_2}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \size 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
