Theorem
$\dfrac {\map \d {\arctan x} } {\d x} = \dfrac 1 {1 + x^2}$


Proof













\(\ds y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \arctan x\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tan y\)





Definition of Real Arctangent








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {\d x} {\d y}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec^2 y\)





Derivative of Tangent Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + \tan^2 y\)





Difference of Squares of Secant and Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + x^2\)





Definition of $x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {\d y} {\d x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {1 + x^2}\)





Derivative of Inverse Function



$\blacksquare$
