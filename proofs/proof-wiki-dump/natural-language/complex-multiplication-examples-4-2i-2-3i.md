Example of Complex Multiplication
$\paren {4 + 2 i} \paren {2 - 3 i} = 14 - 8 i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {4 + 2 i} \paren {2 - 3 i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {4 \times 2 - 2 \times \paren {-3} } + \paren {2 \times 2 + 4 \times \paren {-3} } i\)





Definition of Complex Multiplication














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {8 + 6} + \paren {4 - 12} i\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 14 - 8 i\)









$\blacksquare$
