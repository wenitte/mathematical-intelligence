Example of Ideal of Ring
The set $2 \Z$ of even integers forms an ideal of the ring of integers.


Proof
Let $x \in 2 \Z$.
Then:

$\forall y \in \Z: x y \in 2 \Z$
and:

$\forall y \in \Z: y x \in 2 \Z$
Hence the result by definition of ideal.
$\blacksquare$
