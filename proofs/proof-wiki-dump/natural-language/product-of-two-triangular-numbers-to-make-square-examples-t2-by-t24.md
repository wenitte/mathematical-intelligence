Example of Product of Two Triangular Numbers to make Square
$T_2 \times T_{24} = 30^2$
where $T_2, T_{24}$ denote the $2$nd and $24$th triangular numbers.


Proof













\(\ds T_2 \times T_{24}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {2 \paren {2 + 1} } 2 \times \dfrac {24 \paren {24 + 1} } 2\)





Closed Form for Triangular Numbers














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 3 \times 12 \times 25\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times \paren {3 \times 2^2} \times 5^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 \times 2 \times 5}^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 30^2\)









$\blacksquare$
