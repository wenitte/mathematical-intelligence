Theorem
$\paren {p \oplus q} \oplus q \dashv \vdash p$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {p \oplus q} \oplus q\)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds p \oplus \paren {q \oplus q}\)





Exclusive Or is Associative














\(\ds \)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds p \oplus \bot\)





Exclusive Or with Itself














\(\ds \)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds p\)





Exclusive Or with Contradiction



$\blacksquare$
