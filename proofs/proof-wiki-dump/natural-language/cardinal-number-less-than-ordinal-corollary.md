Corollary to Cardinal Number Less than Ordinal
Let $x$ be an ordinal.
Let $\card x$ denote the cardinal number of $x$.

Then:

$\card x \le x$


Proof
By Set Equivalence behaves like Equivalence Relation:

$x \sim x$
By Cardinal Number Less than Ordinal:

$\card x \le x$
$\blacksquare$
