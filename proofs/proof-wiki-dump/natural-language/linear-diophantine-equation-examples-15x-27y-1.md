Example of Linear Diophantine Equation
The linear diophantine equation:

$15 x + 27 y = 1$
has no solutions for $x$ and $y$ integers.


Proof
We have that:

$\gcd \set {15, 27} = 3$
which is not a divisor of $1$.
So, from Solution of Linear Diophantine Equation, no solution exists.
$\blacksquare$
