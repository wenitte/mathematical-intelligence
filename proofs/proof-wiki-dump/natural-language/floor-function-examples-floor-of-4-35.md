Theorem
$\floor {4 \cdotp 35} = 4$
where $\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof
We have that:

$4 \le 4 \cdotp 35 < 5$
Hence $4$ is the floor of $4 \cdotp 35$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$


Also see
Ceiling of $4 \cdotp 35$: $\ceiling {4 \cdotp 35} = 5$
