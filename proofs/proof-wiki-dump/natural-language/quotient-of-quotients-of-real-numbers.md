Theorem
$\forall x \in \R, y, w, z \in \R_{\ne 0}: \dfrac {x / y} {w / z} = \dfrac {x \times z} {y \times w}$


Proof













\(\ds \frac {x / y} {w / z}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac x y \times \frac 1 {w / z}\)





Definition of Real Division














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac x y \times \frac z w\)





Reciprocal of Quotient of Real Numbers














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {x \times z} {y \times w}\)





Product of Quotients of Real Numbers



$\blacksquare$
