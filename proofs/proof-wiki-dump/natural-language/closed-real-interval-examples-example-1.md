Examples of Closed Real Intervals
Let $I$ be the closed real interval defined as:

$I := \closedint 1 3$
Then $3 \in I$.


Proof
By definition of open real interval:

$I = \set {x \in \R: 1 \le x \le 3}$
As $3 \le 3$ it follows that $3 \in I$.
$\blacksquare$
