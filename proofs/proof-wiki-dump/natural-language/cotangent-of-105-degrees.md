Theorem
$\cot 105^\circ = \cot \dfrac {7 \pi} {12} = -\left({2 - \sqrt 3}\right)$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 105^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot \left({90^\circ + 15^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds - \tan 15^\circ\)





Cotangent of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds - \left({2 - \sqrt 3}\right)\)





Tangent of 15 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
