Example of Factorial
$14! = 87 \, 178 \, 291 \, 200$


Proof
From $13$ factorial:

$13! = 6 \, 227 \, 020 \, 800$
Then:

 6 227 020 800
x           14
---------------
24 908 083 200
62 270 208 000
--------------
87 178 291 200

$\blacksquare$
