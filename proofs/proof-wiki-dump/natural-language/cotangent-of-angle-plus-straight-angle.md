Theorem
$\map \cot {x + \pi} = \cot x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \cot {x + \pi}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\map \cos {x + \pi} } {\map \sin {x + \pi} }\)





Cotangent is Cosine divided by Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {-\cos x} {-\sin x}\)





Cosine of Angle plus Straight Angle and Sine of Angle plus Straight Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot x\)





Cotangent is Cosine divided by Sine



$\blacksquare$
