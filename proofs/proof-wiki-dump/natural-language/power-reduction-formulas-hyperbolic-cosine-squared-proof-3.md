Theorem
$\cosh^2 x = \dfrac {\cosh 2 x + 1} 2$


Proof













\(\ds \cosh^2 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos^2 i x\)





Hyperbolic Cosine in terms of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\map \cos {2 i x} + 1} 2\)





Square of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cosh 2 x + 1} 2\)





Hyperbolic Cosine in terms of Cosine



$\blacksquare$
