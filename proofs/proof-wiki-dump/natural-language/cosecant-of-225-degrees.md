Theorem
$\csc 225 \degrees = \csc \dfrac {5 \pi} 4 = -\sqrt 2$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 225 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \csc {360 \degrees - 135 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 135 \degrees\)





Cosecant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sqrt 2\)





Cosecant of $135 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
