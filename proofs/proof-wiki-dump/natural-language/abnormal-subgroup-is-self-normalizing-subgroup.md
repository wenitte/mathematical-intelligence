Theorem
Let $G$ be a group.
Let $H$ be an abnormal subgroup of $G$.

Then $H$ is a self-normalizing subgroup of $G$.


Proof
We have:

Abnormal Subgroup is Weakly Abnormal Subgroup
Weakly Abnormal Subgroup is Self-Normalizing Subgroup
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
