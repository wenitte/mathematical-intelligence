Theorem
Let $\struct {R, +, \circ}$ be a division ring with unity $1_R$.
Let $\norm {\,\cdot\,}$ be a norm on $R$.
Let $x \in R$.

Then:

$\norm {-x} = \norm x$


Proof
By Norm of Negative of Unity:

$\norm {-1_R} = 1$

Then:














\(\ds \norm {-x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \norm {-1_R \circ x}\)





Product with Ring Negative














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \norm {-1_R} \norm x\)





Norm Axiom $\text N 2$: Multiplicativity














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \norm x\)





Norm of Negative of Unity



as desired.
$\blacksquare$
