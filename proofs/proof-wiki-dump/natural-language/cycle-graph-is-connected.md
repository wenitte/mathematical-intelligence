Theorem
Let $G = \struct {V, E}$ be a cycle graph.
Then $G$ is connected.


Proof
A cycle graph is defined as a (simple) graph which consists of a single cycle.
So a cycle graph consists of just one component, and hence is connected.
$\blacksquare$
