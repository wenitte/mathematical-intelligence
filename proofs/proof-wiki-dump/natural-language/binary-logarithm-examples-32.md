Example of Binary Logarithm
The binary logarithm of $32$ is:

$\lg 32 = 5$


Proof













\(\ds 2^5\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 32\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \lg 32\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \log_2 2^5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 \log_2 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5\)









$\blacksquare$
