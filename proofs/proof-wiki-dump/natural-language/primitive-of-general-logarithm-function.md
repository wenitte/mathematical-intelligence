Theorem













\(\ds \int \log_a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 {\ln a} \paren {x \ln x - x} + C\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \log_a x - \dfrac x {\ln a}\)











Proof













\(\ds \int \log_a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \dfrac {\ln x} {\ln a} \rd x\)





Change of Base of Logarithm














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 {\ln a} \paren {x \ln x - x} + C\)





Primitive of $\ln x$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {x \ln x} {\ln a}  \dfrac x {\ln a} + C\)





rearranging














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \log_a x - \dfrac x {\ln a} + C\)





Change of Base of Logarithm



$\blacksquare$
