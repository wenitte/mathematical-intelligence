Example of Use of Multiplication by 2 over 3 in Egyptian Fractions
$\dfrac 2 3 \times \dfrac 1 5 = \dfrac 1 {10} + \dfrac 1 {30}$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac 1 {10} + \dfrac 1 {30}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 {2 \times 5} + \dfrac 1 {6 \times 5}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 3 {6 \times 5} + \dfrac 1 {6 \times 5}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 4 {6 \times 5}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 2 {3 \times 5}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 2 3 \times \dfrac 1 5\)









$\blacksquare$
