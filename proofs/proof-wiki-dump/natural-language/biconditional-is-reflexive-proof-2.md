Theorem
$\vdash p \iff p$


Proof
We apply the Method of Truth Tables.
As can be seen by inspection, the truth values under the main connective match for both boolean interpretations.
$\begin{array}{|ccc|} \hline
p & \iff & p \\
\hline
F & T & F \\
T & T & T \\
\hline
\end{array}$
$\blacksquare$
