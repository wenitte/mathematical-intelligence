Example of Addition of Codewords in $V \paren {3, 2}$
In the master code $V \paren {3, 2}$, the codewords $110$ and $011$ are added thus:

$110 + 011 = 101$


Proof
Taking the elements of $110$ and $011$ in pairs, successively, we have:














\(\ds 1 + 0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 1\)

\(\ds \pmod 2\)






















\(\ds 1 + 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 0\)

\(\ds \pmod 2\)






















\(\ds 0 + 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 1\)

\(\ds \pmod 2\)







Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
