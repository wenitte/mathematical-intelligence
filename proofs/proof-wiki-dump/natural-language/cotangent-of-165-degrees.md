Theorem
$\cot 165 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {11 \pi} {12} = -\paren {2 + \sqrt 3}$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 165 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cot {90 \degrees + 75 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\tan 75 \degrees\)





Cotangent of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {2 + \sqrt 3}\)





Tangent of $75 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
