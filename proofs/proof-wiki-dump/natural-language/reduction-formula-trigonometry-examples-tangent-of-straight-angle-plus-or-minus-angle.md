Example of Reduction Formula in context of Trigonometry
$\map \tan {180 \degrees \pm \theta} = \pm \tan \theta$


Proof
From Tangent of Angle plus Straight Angle:

$\map \tan {180 \degrees + \theta} = \tan \theta$
From Tangent of Supplementary Angle:

$\map \tan {180 \degrees - \theta} = -\tan \theta$
$\blacksquare$
