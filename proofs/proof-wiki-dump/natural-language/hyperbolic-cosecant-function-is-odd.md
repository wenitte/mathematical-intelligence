Theorem
Let $\csch: \C \to \C$ be the hyperbolic cosecant function on the set of complex numbers.

Then $\csch$ is odd:

$\map \csch {-x} = -\csch x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \csch {-x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\map \sinh {-x} }\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Cosecant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {-\sinh x}\)





Hyperbolic Sine Function is Odd














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csch x\)









$\blacksquare$
