Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\sinh a x} = \frac 1 a \ln \size {\tanh \frac {a x} 2} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\sinh a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \csch a x \rd x\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Cosecant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \ln \size {\tanh \frac {a x} 2} + C\)





Primitive of $\csch a x$



$\blacksquare$
