Example of Modulo Arithmetic
Let $a$ be an odd integer.
Then:

$a^2 + \paren {a + 2}^2 + \paren {a + 4}^2 + 1 \equiv 0 \pmod {12}$


Proof
Let $a = 2 k + 1$ where $k \in \Z$.
Then:














\(\ds a^2 + \paren {a + 2}^2 + \paren {a + 4}^2 + 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2 k + 1}^2 + \paren {2 k + 3}^2 + \paren {2 k + 5}^2 + 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 k^2 + 4 k + 1 + 4 k^2 + 12 k + 9 + 4 k^2 + 20 k + 25 + 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 12 k^2 + 36 k + 36\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 12 \paren {k^2 + 3 k + 3}\)









Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
