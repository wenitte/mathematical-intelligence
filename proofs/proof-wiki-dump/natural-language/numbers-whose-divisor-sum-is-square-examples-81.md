Example of Number whose Divisor Sum is Square
$\map {\sigma_1} {81} = 121 = 11^2$


Proof
From $\sigma_1$ of $81$:

$\map {\sigma_1} {81} = 121$
which equals $11^2$.
$\blacksquare$
