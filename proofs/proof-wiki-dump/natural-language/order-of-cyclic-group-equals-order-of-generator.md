Theorem
Let $G$ be a finite cyclic group which is generated by $a \in G$.
Then:

$\order a = \order G$
where:

$\order a$ denotes the order of $a$ in $G$
$\order G$ denotes the order of $G$.


Proof
Let $\order a = n$.
From List of Elements in Finite Cyclic Group:

$G = \set {a_0, a_1, \ldots, a_{n - 1} }$
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
