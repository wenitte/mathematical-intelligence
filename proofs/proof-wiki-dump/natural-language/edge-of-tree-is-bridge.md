Theorem
Let $T$ be a tree.
Let $e$ be an edge of $T$.

Then $e$ is a bridge of $T$.


Proof
From Condition for Edge to be Bridge, $e$ is a bridge if and only if $e$ does not lie on any circuit.
Since $T$ is a tree, there are no circuits in $T$.

The result follows.
$\blacksquare$
