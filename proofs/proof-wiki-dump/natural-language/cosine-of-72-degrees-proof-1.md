Theorem
$\cos 72 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {2 \pi} 5 = \dfrac {\sqrt 5 - 1} 4$


Proof













\(\ds \cos 72 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {90 \degrees - 18 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sin 18 \degrees\)





Cosine of Complement equals Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\sqrt 5 - 1} 4\)





Sine of $18 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
