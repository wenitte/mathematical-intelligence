Example of Use of Euler's Formula
$e^{i \pi / 2} = i$


Proof













\(\ds e^{i \pi / 2}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos \frac \pi 2 + i \sin \frac \pi 2\)





Euler's Formula














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0 + i \times 1\)





Cosine of $\dfrac \pi 2$, Sine of $\dfrac \pi 2$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds i\)









$\blacksquare$
