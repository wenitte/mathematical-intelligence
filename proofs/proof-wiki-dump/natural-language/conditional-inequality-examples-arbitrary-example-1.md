Examples of Conditional Inequalities
The inequality:

$2 x + 1 > 11$
is a conditional inequality as it is true for $x > 5$ and false otherwise.


Proof













\(\ds 2 x + 1\)

\(>\)







\(\ds 11\)














\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds 2 x\)

\(>\)







\(\ds 10\)














\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(>\)







\(\ds 5\)









$\blacksquare$
