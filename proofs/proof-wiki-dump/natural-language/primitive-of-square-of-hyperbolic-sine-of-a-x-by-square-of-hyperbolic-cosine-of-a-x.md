Theorem
$\ds \int \sinh^2 a x \cosh^2 a x \rd x = \frac {\sinh 4 a x} {32 a} - \frac x 8 + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \sinh^2 a x \cosh^2 a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \paren {\sinh a x \cosh a x}^2 \rd x\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \paren {\frac {\sinh 2 a x} 2}^2 \rd x\)





Double Angle Formula for Hyperbolic Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 4 \int \sinh^2 2 a x \rd x\)





Primitive of Constant Multiple of Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 4 \paren {\frac {\sinh 2 \paren {2 a x} } {4 \paren {2 a} } - \frac x 2} + C\)





Primitive of $\sinh^2 a x$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sinh 4 a x} {32 a} - \frac x 8 + C\)





simplifying



$\blacksquare$
