Example of Reduction Formula in context of Trigonometry
$\map \cos {90 \degrees \pm \theta} = \mp \sin \theta$


Proof
From Cosine of Angle plus Right Angle:

$\map \cos {90 \degrees + \theta} = -\sin \theta$
From Cosine of Complement equals Sine:

$\map \cos {90 \degrees - \theta} = \sin \theta$
$\blacksquare$
