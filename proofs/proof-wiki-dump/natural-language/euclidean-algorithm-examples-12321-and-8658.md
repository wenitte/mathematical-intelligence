Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $12321$ and $8658$ is:

$\gcd \set {12321, 8658} = 333$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 12321\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 8658 + 3663\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 8653\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 3663 + 1332\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 3663\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 1332 + 999\)










\(\text {(4)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 1332\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 999 + 333\)










\(\text {(5)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 999\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 333\)









Thus:

$\gcd \set {12321, 8658} = 333$
$\blacksquare$
