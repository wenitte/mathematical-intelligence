Theorem
The cube function on the real numbers:

$\forall x \in \R: \map f x = x^3$
is an odd function.


Proof









\(\ds \forall x \in \R: \, \)



\(\ds \paren {-x}^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-1}^3 x^3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -x^3\)









Hence the result by definition of odd function.
$\blacksquare$
