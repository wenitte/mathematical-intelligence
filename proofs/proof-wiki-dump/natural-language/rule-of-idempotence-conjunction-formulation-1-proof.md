Theorem
$p \dashv \vdash p \land p$


Proof
We apply the Method of Truth Tables.
As can be seen by inspection, the truth values under the main connectives match for each boolean interpretations.
$\begin{array} {|c||ccc|} \hline
p & p & \land & p \\
\hline
\T & \T & \T & \T \\
\F & \F & \F & \F \\
\hline
\end{array}$
$\blacksquare$
