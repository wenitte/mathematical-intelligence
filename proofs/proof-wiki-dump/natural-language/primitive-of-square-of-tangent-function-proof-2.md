Theorem
$\ds \int \tan^2 x \rd x = \tan x - x + C$


Proof













\(\ds I_n\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \tan^n x \rd x\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\tan^{n - 1} x} {n - 1} - I_{n - 2}\)





Reduction Formula for Integral of Power of Tangent














\(\ds I_0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \paren {\tan x}^0 \rd x\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \d x\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x + C\)





Primitive of Constant








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds I_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tan x - x + C'\)





setting $n = 2$



$\blacksquare$
