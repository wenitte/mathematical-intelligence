Theorem
$\map \cos {x + \dfrac {3 \pi} 2} = \sin x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \cos {x + \frac {3 \pi} 2}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos x \cos \frac {3 \pi} 2 - \sin x \sin \frac {3 \pi} 2\)





Cosine of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos x \cdot 0 - \sin x \cdot \paren {-1}\)





Cosine of Three Right Angles and Sine of Three Right Angles














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sin x\)









$\blacksquare$
