Theorem
Let $M$ be a locally Euclidean space of some dimension $d$.

Then $M$ is locally connected.


Proof
Follows from:

Locally Euclidean Space is Locally Path-Connected
Locally Path-Connected Space is Locally Connected
$\blacksquare$
