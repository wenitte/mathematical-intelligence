Theorem
$\floor 3 = 3$
where $\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof
We have that $3$ is an integer.
Thus this is a specific example of Real Number is Integer iff equals Floor:
$\floor x = x \iff x \in \Z$
$\blacksquare$
