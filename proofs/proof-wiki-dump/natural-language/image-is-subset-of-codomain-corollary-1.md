Corollary to Image is Subset of Codomain
Let $\RR = S \times T$ be a relation.

The image of $\RR$ is a subset of the codomain of $\RR$:

$\Img \RR \subseteq T$


Proof













\(\ds \Img \RR\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \RR \sqbrk {\Dom \RR}\)





Definition of Image of Relation














\(\ds \Dom \RR\)

\(\subseteq\)







\(\ds \Dom \RR\)





Set is Subset of Itself








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \Img \RR\)

\(\subseteq\)







\(\ds T\)





Image is Subset of Codomain



$\blacksquare$
