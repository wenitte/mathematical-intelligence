Example of Use of Euler $\phi$ Function
$\map \phi {734} = 366$
where $\phi$ denotes the Euler $\phi$ Function.


Proof
We have that:

$734 = 2 \times 367$

Thus:














\(\ds \map \phi {734}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 367 - 1\)





Euler Phi Function of 2 times Odd Prime














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 366\)









$\blacksquare$
