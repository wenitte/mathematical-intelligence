Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\sech a x} = \frac {\sinh a x} a + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\sech a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \cosh a x \rd x\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Secant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sinh a x} a + C\)





Primitive of $\cosh a x$



$\blacksquare$
