Example of Consecutive Integers with Same Divisor Sum
Let $\sigma_1: \Z_{>0} \to \Z_{>0}$ denote the divisor sum function.
Then:

$\map {\sigma_1} {206} = \map {\sigma_1} {207} = 312$


Proof
From $\sigma_1$ of $206$:

$\map {\sigma_1} {206} = 312$
From $\sigma_1$ of $207$:

$\map {\sigma_1} {207} = 312$
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
