Theorem
$\csc 270 \degrees = \csc \dfrac {3 \pi} 2 = -1$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 270 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \csc {360 \degrees - 90 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 90 \degrees\)





Cosecant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -1\)





Cosecant of Right Angle



$\blacksquare$
