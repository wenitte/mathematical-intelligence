Example of Cosine of Supplementary Angle
$\map \cos {\theta - 180 \degrees} = -\cos \theta$


Proof













\(\ds \map \cos {\theta - 180 \degrees}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {180 \degrees - \theta}\)





Cosine Function is Even














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cos \theta\)





Cosine of Supplementary Angle



$\blacksquare$
