Example of Euler Phi Function of 2 times Odd Prime
$\map \phi {146} = 72$
where $\phi$ denotes the Euler $\phi$ Function.


Proof
We have that:

$146 = 2 \times 73$

Thus:














\(\ds \map \phi {146}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 73 - 1\)





Euler Phi Function of 2 times Odd Prime














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 72\)









$\blacksquare$
