Theorem
$\sec 135 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {3 \pi} 4 = -\sqrt 2$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 135 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {90 \degrees + 45 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 45 \degrees\)





Secant of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sqrt 2\)





Cosecant of $45 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
