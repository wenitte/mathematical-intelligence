Example of Cubic Recurring Digital Invariant
$160$ is a cubic recurring digital invariant:








\(\ds 160: \ \ \)





\(\ds 1^3 + 6^3 + 0^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + 216 + 0\)

\(\ds = 217\)












\(\ds 217: \ \ \)





\(\ds 2^3 + 1^3 + 7^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 8 + 1 + 343\)

\(\ds = 352\)












\(\ds 352: \ \ \)





\(\ds 3^3 + 5^3 + 2^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 27 + 125 + 8\)

\(\ds = 160\)







$\blacksquare$
