Theorem
In order to multiply $3367$ by a $2$-digit integer $\sqbrk {xy}$:

divide the $6$-digit integer $\sqbrk {xyxyxy}$ by $3$.


Proof
We have that:

$10101 = 3367 \times 3$
Then:

$10101 \times \sqbrk {xy} = \sqbrk {xyxyxy}$
The result follows.
$\blacksquare$
