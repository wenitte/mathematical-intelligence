Examples of Divisors of Integer
The divisors of $6$ are $1$, $2$, $3$ and $6$.


Proof
See $\sigma_0$ of $6$:

$\map {\sigma_0} 6 = 4$
where $\sigma_0$ denotes the divisor count function.
Then we have:














\(\ds 6\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 6\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 3\)









which accounts for them all.
$\blacksquare$
