Theorem
Let $\struct {\R, \tau_d}$ be the real number line with the usual (Euclidean) topology.

Then $\struct {\R, \tau_d}$ is separable.


Proof
Follows from:

Real Number Line is Second-Countable
Second-Countable Space is Separable
$\blacksquare$
