Modern Puzzles by Henry Ernest Dudeney: $9$
Fractional Value
What part of threepence is one-third of twopence?


Solution
Two-ninths of threepence.


Proof
$\dfrac 1 3 \times 2 = \dfrac 2 3$
So $\dfrac 1 3$ of $2 \oldpence$ is $\dfrac 2 3$ of a penny.
Then:

$\dfrac 2 3$ of a penny is $\dfrac 2 3$ of $\dfrac 1 3$ of $3 \oldpence$
That is:

$\dfrac 2 9$ of $3 \oldpence$
$\blacksquare$
