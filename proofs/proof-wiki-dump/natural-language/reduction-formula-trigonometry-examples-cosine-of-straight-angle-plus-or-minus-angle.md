Example of Reduction Formula in context of Trigonometry
$\map \cos {180 \degrees \pm \theta} = -\cos \theta$


Proof
From Cosine of Angle plus Straight Angle:

$\map \cos {180 \degrees + \theta} = -\cos \theta$
From Cosine of Supplementary Angle:

$\map \cos {180 \degrees - \theta} = -\cos \theta$
$\blacksquare$
