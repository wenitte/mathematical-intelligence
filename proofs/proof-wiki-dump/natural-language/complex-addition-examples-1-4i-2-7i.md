Example of Complex Addition
$\paren {-1 + 4 i} + \paren {2 + \paren {-7} i} = 1 + \paren {-3} i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {-1 + 4 i} + \paren {2 + \paren {-7} i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-1 + 2} + \paren {4 + \paren {-7} } i\)





Definition of Complex Addition














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + \paren {-3} i\)









$\blacksquare$
