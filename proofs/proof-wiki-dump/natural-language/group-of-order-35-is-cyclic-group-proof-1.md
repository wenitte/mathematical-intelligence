Theorem
Let $G$ be a group whose order is $35$.
Then $G$ is cyclic.


Proof
We have that $35 = 5 \times 7$.
Then we have that $5$ and $7$ are primes such that $5 < 7$ and $5$ does not divide $7 - 1$.
Thus Group of Order $p q$ is Cyclic can be applied.
$\blacksquare$
