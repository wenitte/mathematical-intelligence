Theorem
Let $n \in \Z_{\ge 0}$ be a positive integer.

$1^{\overline n} = n!$
where:

$1^{\overline n}$ denotes the rising factorial
$n!$ denotes the factorial.


Proof













\(\ds 1^{\overline n}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\paren {1 + n - 1}!} {\paren {1 - 1}!}\)





Rising Factorial as Quotient of Factorials














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {n!} {0!}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds n!\)





Factorial of Zero



$\blacksquare$
