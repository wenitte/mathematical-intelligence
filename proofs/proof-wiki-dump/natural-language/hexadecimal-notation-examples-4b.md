Example of Hexadecimal Notation
The integer expressed in decimal as $75$ is expressed in hexadecimal as $4 \mathrm B$.


Proof













\(\ds {4 \mathrm B}_{16}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 \times 16 + 11\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 64 + 11\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 75\)









$\blacksquare$
