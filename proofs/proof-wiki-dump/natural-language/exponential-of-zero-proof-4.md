Theorem
$\exp 0 = 1$


Proof
This proof assumes the Definition of $\exp x$ as the unique continuous extension of $e^x$.














\(\ds \exp 0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e^0\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)





Definition of $x^0$, where $x \ne 0$



$\blacksquare$
