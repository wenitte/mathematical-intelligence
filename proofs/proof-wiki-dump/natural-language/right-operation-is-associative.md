Theorem
The right operation is associative:

$\forall x, y, z: \paren {x \to y} \to z = x \to \paren {y \to z}$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {x \to y} \to z\)

\(=\)







\(\ds y \to z\)





Definition of Right Operation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds z\)





Definition of Right Operation


















\(\ds x \to \paren {y \to z}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \to z\)





Definition of Right Operation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds z\)





Definition of Right Operation



$\blacksquare$
