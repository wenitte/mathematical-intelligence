Theorem
$\floor {\sqrt {10} } = 3$
where $\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof













\(\ds \sqrt 9\)

\(\le\)







\(\ds \sqrt 10\)




















\(\ds \)

\(<\)







\(\ds \sqrt 16\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 3\)

\(\le\)







\(\ds \sqrt 10\)




















\(\ds \)

\(<\)







\(\ds 4\)









Hence $3$ is the floor of $\sqrt {10}$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$
