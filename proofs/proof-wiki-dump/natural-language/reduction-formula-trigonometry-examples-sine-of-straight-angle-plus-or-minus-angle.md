Example of Reduction Formula in context of Trigonometry
$\map \sin {180 \degrees \pm \theta} = \mp \sin \theta$


Proof
From Sine of Angle plus Straight Angle:

$\map \sin {180 \degrees + \theta} = -\sin \theta$
From Sine of Supplementary Angle:

$\map \sin {180 \degrees - \theta} = \sin \theta$
$\blacksquare$
