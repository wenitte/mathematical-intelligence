Theorem
$\hat \phi = - \dfrac 1 \phi$
where:

$\phi$ denotes the golden mean
$\hat \phi$ denotes one minus the golden mean: $\hat \phi = 1 - \phi$.


Proof













\(\ds \hat \phi\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 - \phi\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\left({\phi - 1}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\frac 1 \phi\)
