Theorem
$\csc 300^\circ = \csc \dfrac {5 \pi} 3 = -\dfrac {2 \sqrt 3} 3$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 300^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \csc \left({360^\circ - 60^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 60^\circ\)





Cosecant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\frac {2 \sqrt 3} 3\)





Cosecant of 60 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
