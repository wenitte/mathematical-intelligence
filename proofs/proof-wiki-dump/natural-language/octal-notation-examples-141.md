Example of Octal Notation
The integer expressed in octal as $215$ is expressed in decimal as $141$.


Proof













\(\ds 215_8\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 8^2 + 1 \times 8^1 + 5 \times 8^0\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 128 + 8 + 5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 141\)









$\blacksquare$
