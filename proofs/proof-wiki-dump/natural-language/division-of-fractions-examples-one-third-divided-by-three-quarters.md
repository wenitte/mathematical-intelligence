Example of Division of Fractions
$\dfrac 1 3 \div \dfrac 3 4 = \dfrac 4 9$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac 1 3 \div \dfrac 3 4\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {1 \times 4} {3 \times 3}\)





Division of Fractions














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 4 9\)









$\blacksquare$
