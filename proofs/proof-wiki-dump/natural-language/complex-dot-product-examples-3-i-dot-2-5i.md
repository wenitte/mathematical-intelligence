Examples of Complex Dot Product
Let:

$z_1 = 3 - i$
$z_1 = 2 + 5 i$
Then:

$z_1 \circ z_2 = 1$
where $\circ$ denotes (complex) dot product.


Proof













\(\ds z_1 \circ z_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 - i} \circ \paren {2 + 5 i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 2 + \paren {-1} \times 5\)





Definition 1 of Dot Product














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6 - 5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
