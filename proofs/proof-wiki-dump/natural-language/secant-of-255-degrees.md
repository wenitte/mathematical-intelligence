Theorem
$\sec 255 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {17 \pi} {12} = -\paren {\sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2}$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 255 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 105 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 105 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {\sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2}\)





Secant of $105 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
