Theorem
$\cot 300 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {5 \pi} 3 = - \dfrac {\sqrt 3} 3$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 300 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cot {360 \degrees - 60 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 60 \degrees\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\frac {\sqrt 3} 3\)





Cotangent of $60 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
