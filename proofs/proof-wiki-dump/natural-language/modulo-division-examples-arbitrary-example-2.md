Example of Modulo Division
$1 \div_m 3 \equiv 6 \pmod {17}$


Proof
We have:














\(\ds 3 \times 6\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 18\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 1\)

\(\ds \pmod {17}\)












\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 1 \div_m 3\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 6\)

\(\ds \pmod {17}\)







$\blacksquare$
