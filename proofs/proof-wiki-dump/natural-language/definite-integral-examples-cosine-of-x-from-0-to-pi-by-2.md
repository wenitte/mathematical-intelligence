Example of Definite Integral
$\ds \int_0^{\pi / 2} \cos x \rd x = 1$


Proof













\(\ds \int_0^{\pi / 2} \cos x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \bigintlimits {\sin x} 0 {\pi / 2}\)





Primitive of Cosine Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sin \dfrac \pi 2 - \sin 0\)





Definition of Definite Integral














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 - 0\)





Sine of Right Angle, Sine of $0 \degrees$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
