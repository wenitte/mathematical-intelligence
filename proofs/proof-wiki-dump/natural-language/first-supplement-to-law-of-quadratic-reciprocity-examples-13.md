Examples of First Supplement to Law of Quadratic Reciprocity
$-1$ is a quadratic residue of $13$.


Proof
We have that:

$5^2 = 25 \equiv -1 \pmod 13$
and so $-1$ is therefore a quadratic residue of $13$.

We also have that:

$13 = 4 \times 3 + 1$
and so is of the form $4 n + 1$.
$\blacksquare$
