Theorem
The repunit number $R_n$ can be expressed as:

$R_n = \dfrac {10^n - 1} 9$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac {10^n - 1} 9\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {10^n - 1} {10 - 1}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 0}^{n - 1} 10^k\)





Sum of Geometric Sequence














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + 10 + 100 + \ldots + 10^{n - 2} + 10^{n - 1}\)









The result follows from the Basis Representation Theorem.
$\blacksquare$
