Examples of Quasiamicable Pair
$48$ and $75$ form a quasiamicable pair.


Proof













\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {48}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 124\)





$\sigma_1$ of $48$














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {75}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 124\)





$\sigma_1$ of $75$














\(\ds 48 + 75 + 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 124\)









Hence the result by definition of quasiamicable pair.
$\blacksquare$
