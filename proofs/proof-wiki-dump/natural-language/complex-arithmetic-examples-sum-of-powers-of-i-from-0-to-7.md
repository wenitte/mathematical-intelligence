Example of Complex Arithmetic
$1 + i + i^2 + i^3 + i^4 + i^5 + i^6 + i^7 = 0$


Proof













\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 1 + i + i^2 + i^3 + i^4 + i^5 + i^6 + i^7\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {1 - i^8} {1 - i}\)





Sum of Geometric Sequence














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {1 - \paren {i^4}^2} {1 - i}\)





simplifying














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {1 - 1^2} {1 - i}\)





Powers of Imaginary Unit














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)









$\blacksquare$
