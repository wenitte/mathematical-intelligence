Theorem
$\csc x - \sin x = \cos x \ \cot x$


Proof













\(\ds \csc x - \sin x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\sin x} - \sin x\)





Definition of Cosecant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {1 - \sin^2 x} {\sin x}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cos^2 x} {\sin x}\)





Sum of Squares of Sine and Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos x \ \cot x\)





Definition of Cotangent



$\blacksquare$
