Example of Hexadecimal Notation
The integer expressed in decimal as $300$ is expressed in hexadecimal as $12 \mathrm C$.


Proof













\(\ds 12 \mathrm C_{1 6}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 16^2 + 2 \times 16 + 12\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 256 + 32 + 12\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 300\)









$\blacksquare$
