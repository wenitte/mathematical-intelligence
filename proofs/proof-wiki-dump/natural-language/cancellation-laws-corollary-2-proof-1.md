Corollary to Cancellation Laws
$h g = g \implies h = e$


Proof













\(\ds h g\)

\(=\)







\(\ds g\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds h g\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e g\)





Group Axiom $\text G 2$: Existence of Identity Element








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds h\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e\)





Right Cancellation Law



$\blacksquare$
