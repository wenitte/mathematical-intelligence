Example of Kaprekar Triple
$297$ is a Kaprekar triple.


Proof













\(\ds 297^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 26 \, 198 \, 073\)




















\(\ds 297\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 26 + 198 + 073\)









$\blacksquare$
