Example of Divisor Sum of Integer
$\map {\sigma_1} {21 \, 735} = 46 \, 080$
where $\sigma_1$ denotes the divisor sum function.


Proof
We have that:

$21 \, 735 = 3^3 \times 5 \times 7 \times 23$

Hence:














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {21 \, 735}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {3^4 - 1} {3 - 1} \times \paren {5 + 1} \times \paren {7 + 1} \times \paren {23 + 1}\)





Divisor Sum of Integer














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {80} 2 \times 6 \times 8 \times 24\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 40 \times 6 \times 8 \times 24\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2^3 \times 5} \times \paren {2 \times 3} \times 2^3 \times \paren {2^3 \times 3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^10 \times 3^2 \times 5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 46 \, 080\)









$\blacksquare$
