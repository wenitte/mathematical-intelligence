Theorem
Let $x \in \R$ be a real number.
Then:

$x - x = 0$
where $x - x$ denotes the operation of real subtraction.


Proof













\(\ds x - x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x + \paren {-x}\)





Definition of Real Subtraction














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)

$\blacksquare$
