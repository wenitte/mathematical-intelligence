Theorem
$\cot 285 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {19 \pi} {12} = -\paren {2 - \sqrt 3}$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 285 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot \paren {360 \degrees - 75 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 75^\circ\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {2 - \sqrt 3}\)





Cotangent of $75 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
