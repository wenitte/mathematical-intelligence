Example of Amicable Triplet
$\tuple {1980, 2016, 2556}$ form an amicable triplet.


Proof
We have














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {1980}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6552\)





$\sigma_1$ of $1980$














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {2016}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6552\)





$\sigma_1$ of $2016$














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {2556}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6552\)





$\sigma_1$ of $2556$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1980 + 2016 + 2556\)









Hence the result, by definition of amicable triplet.
$\blacksquare$
