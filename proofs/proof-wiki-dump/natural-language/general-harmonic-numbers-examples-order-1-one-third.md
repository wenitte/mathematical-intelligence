Example of General Harmonic Number
$\harm 1 {\dfrac 1 3} = 3 - \dfrac 3 2 \ln 3 - \dfrac \pi {2 \sqrt 3}$
where $\harm 1 {\dfrac 1 3}$ denotes the general harmonic number of order $1$ evaluated at $\dfrac 1 3$.
