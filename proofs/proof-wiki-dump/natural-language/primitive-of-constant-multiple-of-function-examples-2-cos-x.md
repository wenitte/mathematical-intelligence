Example of Use of Primitive of Constant Multiple of Function
$\ds \int 2 \cos x \rd x = 2 \sin x + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int 2 \cos x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \int \cos x \rd x\)





Primitive of Constant Multiple of Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \sin x + C\)





Primitive of Cosine Function



$\blacksquare$
