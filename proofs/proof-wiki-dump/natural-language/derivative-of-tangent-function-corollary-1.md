Corollary to Derivative of Tangent Function
$\map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tan a x} = a \sec^2 a x$


Proof













\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tan x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec^2 x\)





Derivative of $\tan x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tan a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a \sec^2 a x\)





Derivative of Function of Constant Multiple



$\blacksquare$
