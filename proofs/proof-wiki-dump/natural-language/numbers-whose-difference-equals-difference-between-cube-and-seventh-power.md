Theorem
The following $2$ pairs of integers are the only ones known which exhibit this pattern:














\(\ds \size {5^3 - 2^7}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 - 2\)




















\(\ds \size {13^3 - 3^7}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 13 - 3\)











Proof
We have:














\(\ds \size {5^3 - 2^7}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \size {125 - 128}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 - 2\)
























\(\ds \size {13^3 - 3^7}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \size {2197 - 2187}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 13 - 3\)









$\blacksquare$
