Example of Hexadecimal Notation
The integer expressed in decimal as $200$ is expressed in hexadecimal as $\mathrm C 8$.


Proof













\(\ds \mathrm C 8_{1 6}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 12 \times 16 + 8\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 192 + 8\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 200\)









$\blacksquare$
