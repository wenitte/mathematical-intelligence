Theorem
Let $R$ be a region of space in which there exists an scalar field $F$.
Let $\mathbf V$ denote the gradient of $F$.

Then $\mathbf V$ is invariant under a change of coordinate system on $R$.


Proof
The result follows directly from the geometrical representation of the gradient operator.
$\blacksquare$
