Example of Modulo Division
$4 \div_m 5 \equiv 8 \pmod {12}$


Proof
We have:














\(\ds 5 \times 8\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 40\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 4\)

\(\ds \pmod {12}\)












\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 4 \div_m 5\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 8\)

\(\ds \pmod {12}\)







$\blacksquare$
