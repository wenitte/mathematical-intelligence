Example of Complex Cross Product
Let:

$z_1 = 3 - i$
$z_2 = 2 + 5 i$
Then:

$z_1 \times z_2 = 17$
where $\times$ denotes (complex) cross product.


Proof













\(\ds z_1 \circ z_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 - i} \times \paren {2 + 5 i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times \paren 5 - \paren {-1} \times 2\)





Definition 1 of Complex Cross Product














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 15 - \paren {-2}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 17\)









$\blacksquare$
