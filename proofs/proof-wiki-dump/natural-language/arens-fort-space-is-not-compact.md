Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau}$ be the Arens-Fort space.

Then $T$ is not a compact space.


Proof
We have:

The Arens-Fort Space is not Weakly Locally Compact.
A Compact Space is Weakly Locally Compact.
$\blacksquare$
