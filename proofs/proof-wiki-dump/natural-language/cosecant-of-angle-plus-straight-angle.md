Theorem
$\map \csc {x + \pi} = -\csc x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \csc {x + \pi}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\map \sin {x + \pi} }\)





Cosecant is Reciprocal of Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {-\sin x}\)





Sine of Angle plus Straight Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc x\)





Cosecant is Reciprocal of Sine



$\blacksquare$
