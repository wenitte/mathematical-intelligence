Example of Power of 2 is Difference between Two Powers
$2^1 = 3^3 - 5^2$


Proof













\(\ds 3^2 - 5^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 27 - 25\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^1\)









$\blacksquare$
