Theorem
$\cosh^2 x = \dfrac {\cosh 2 x + 1} 2$


Proof













\(\ds \cosh^2 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 4 \paren {e^x + e^{-x} }^2\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {e^{2 x} + e^{-2 x} + 2} 4\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cosh 2 x + 1} 2\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Cosine



$\blacksquare$
