Example of Complex Division
$\dfrac {3 - 2 i} {-1 + i} = \dfrac {-5 - i} 2$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac {3 - 2 i} {-1 + i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\paren {3 - 2 i} \paren {-1 - i} } {\paren {-1 + i} \paren {-1 - i} }\)





multiplying top and bottom by $-1 - i$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {-3 - i + 2 i^2} {1^2 + 1^2}\)





Definition of Complex Multiplication














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {-5 - i} 2\)





simplifying














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -i\)









$\blacksquare$
