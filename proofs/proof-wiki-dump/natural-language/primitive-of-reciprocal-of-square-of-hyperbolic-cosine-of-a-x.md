Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\cosh^2 a x} = \frac {\tanh a x} a + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\cosh^2 a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \sech^2 a x \rd x\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Secant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\tanh a x} a + C\)





Primitive of $\sech^2 a x$



$\blacksquare$
