Theorem
$x^4 - y^4 = \paren {x - y} \paren {x + y} \paren {x^2 + y^2}$


Proof













\(\ds x^4 - y^4\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {x^2}^2 - \paren {y^2}^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {x^2 - y^2} \paren {x^2 + y^2}\)





Difference of Two Squares














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {x - y} \paren {x + y} \paren {x^2 + y^2}\)





Difference of Two Squares



$\blacksquare$
