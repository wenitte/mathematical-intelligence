Example of Convergent Real Sequence
$\ds \lim_{n \mathop \to \infty} \paren {\dfrac {n^3 + 5 n^2 + 2} {2 n^3 + 9} } = \dfrac 1 2$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac {n^3 + 5 n^2 + 2} {2 n^3 + 9}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {1 - \dfrac 5 n + \dfrac 2 {n^3} } {2 + \dfrac 9 {n^3} }\)





dividing top and bottom by $n^3$














\(\ds \)

\(\to\)







\(\ds \dfrac {1 + 0 + 0} {2 + 0}\)

\(\ds \text {as $n \to \infty$}\)



Sequence of Powers of Reciprocals is Null Sequence














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 2\)









$\blacksquare$
