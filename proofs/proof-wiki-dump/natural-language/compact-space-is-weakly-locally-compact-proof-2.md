Theorem
Let $T = \left({S, \tau}\right)$ be a compact space.

Then $T$ is a weakly locally compact.


Proof
Follows directly from:

Compact Space is Strongly Locally Compact
Strongly Locally Compact Space is Weakly Locally Compact
$\blacksquare$
