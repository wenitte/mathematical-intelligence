Theorem
Let $S$ be a set.

Then $S$ has a Hartogs number.


Proof
Follows immediately from Cardinal Equal to Collection of All Dominated Ordinals.
The collection of all dominated ordinals:

$\set {y \in \On: y \preccurlyeq S}$
is the Hartogs number of $S$.
$\blacksquare$
