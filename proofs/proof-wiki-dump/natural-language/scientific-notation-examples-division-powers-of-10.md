Example of Division using Scientific Notation
$\dfrac {10^6} {10^4} = 10^2$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac {10^6} {10^4}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {10^6} \paren {10^{-4} }\)





Negative Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10^{6 - 4}\)





Product of Powers














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10^2\)










That is:














\(\ds \dfrac {10^6} {10^4}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {1 \, 000 \, 000} {10 \, 000}\)





Definition of Scientific Notation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 100\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10^2\)





Definition of Scientific Notation



$\blacksquare$
