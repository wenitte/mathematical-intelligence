Theorem
$\ds \int_0^\infty \frac {1 - \cos p x} {x^2} \rd x = \frac {\pi \size p} 2$
where $p$ is a real number.


Proof













\(\ds \int_0^\infty \frac {1 - \cos p x} {x^2} \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int_0^\infty \frac {2 \sin^2 \paren {\frac {p x} 2} } {x^2} \rd x\)





Square of Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times \frac {\pi \size p} {2 \times 2}\)





Integral to Infinity of $\paren {\dfrac {\sin p x} x}^2$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\pi \size p} 2\)









$\blacksquare$
