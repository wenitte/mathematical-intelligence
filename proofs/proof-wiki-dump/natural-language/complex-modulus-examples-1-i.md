Example of Complex Modulus
$\cmod {1 + i} = \sqrt 2$


Proof













\(\ds \cmod {1 + i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cmod {1 + 1 i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {1^2 + 1^2}\)





Definition of Complex Modulus














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 2\)









$\blacksquare$
