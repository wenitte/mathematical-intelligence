Theorem
For all $k \in \Z$:

$e^{i \paren {\theta + 2 k \pi} } = e^{i \theta}$


Proof













\(\ds e^{i \paren {\theta + 2 k \pi} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {\theta + 2 k \pi} + i \, \map \sin {\theta + 2 k \pi}\)





Euler's Formula














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos \theta + i \sin \theta\)





Sine and Cosine are Periodic on Reals














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds e^{i \theta}\)





Euler's Formula



$\blacksquare$
