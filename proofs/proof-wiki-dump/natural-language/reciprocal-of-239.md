Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $239$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {239} = 0 \cdotp \dot 00418 \, 4 \dot 1$
This sequence is A021243 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
