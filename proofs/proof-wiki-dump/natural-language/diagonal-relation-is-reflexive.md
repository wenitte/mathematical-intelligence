Theorem
The diagonal relation $\Delta_S$ on a set $S$ is a reflexive relation in $S$.


Proof









\(\ds \forall x \in S: \, \)



\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x\)





Definition of Equals








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \tuple {x, x}\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds \Delta_S\)





Definition of Diagonal Relation



So $\Delta_S$ is reflexive.
$\blacksquare$
