Theorem
Let $G$ be a group of order $30$.
Then $G$ is not simple.


Proof
From Group of Order 30 has Normal Cyclic Subgroup of Order 15, $G$ has a normal subgroup of order $15$.
Hence the result, by definition of simple group.
$\blacksquare$
