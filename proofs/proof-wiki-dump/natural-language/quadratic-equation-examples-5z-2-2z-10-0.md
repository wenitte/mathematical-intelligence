Example of Quadratic Equation
The quadratic equation in $\C$:

$5 z^2 + 2 z + 10 = 0$
has the solutions:

$z = \dfrac {-1 \pm 7 i} 5$


Proof













\(\ds 5 z^2 + 2 z + 10\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds z\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {-2 \pm \sqrt {2^2 - 4 \times 5 \times 10} } {2 \times 5}\)





Quadratic Formula: $a = 5$, $b = 2$, $c = 10$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {-1 \pm \sqrt {1 - 50} } 5\)





dividing top and bottom by $2$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {-1 \pm 7 i} 5\)









$\blacksquare$
