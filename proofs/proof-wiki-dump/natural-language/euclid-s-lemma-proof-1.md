Theorem
Let $a, b, c \in \Z$.
Let $a \divides b c$, where $\divides$ denotes divisibility.

Let $a \perp b$, where $\perp$ denotes relative primeness.
Then $a \divides c$.


Proof
Follows directly from Integers are Euclidean Domain.
$\blacksquare$
