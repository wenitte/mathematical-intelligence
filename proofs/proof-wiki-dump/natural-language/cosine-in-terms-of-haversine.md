Theorem
$\cos \theta = 1 - 2 \hav \theta$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine and $\hav$ denotes haversine.


Proof













\(\ds \hav \theta\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 2 \paren {1 - \cos \theta}\)





Definition of Haversine








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 2 \hav \theta\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 - \cos \theta\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \cos \theta\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 - 2 \hav \theta\)









$\blacksquare$
