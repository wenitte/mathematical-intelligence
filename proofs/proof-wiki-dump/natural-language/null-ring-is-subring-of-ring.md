Theorem
Let $R$ be a ring.
Then the null ring is a subring of $R$.


Proof
From Null Ring is Ring, the null ring $\struct {\set {0_R}, +, \circ}$ is a ring.
We also have that $\set {0_R}$ is a subset of $R$
Hence the result by definition of subring.
$\blacksquare$
