Theorem
$\csc 90 \degrees = \csc \dfrac \pi 2 = 1$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 90 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\sin 90 \degrees}\)





Cosecant is Reciprocal of Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 1\)





Sine of Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
