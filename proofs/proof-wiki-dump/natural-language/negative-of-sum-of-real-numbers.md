Theorem
$\forall x, y \in \R: -\paren {x + y} = -x - y$


Proof













\(\ds -\paren {x + y}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-1} \times \paren {x + y}\)





Multiplication by Negative Real Number: Corollary














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren {-1} \times x} + \paren {\paren {-1} \times y}\)





Real Number Axiom $\R \text D$: Distributivity of Multiplication over Addition














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-x} + \paren {-y}\)





Multiplication by Negative Real Number: Corollary














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -x - y\)





Definition of Real Subtraction



$\blacksquare$
