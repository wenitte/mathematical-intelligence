Theorem
Let $n \in \Z$ be an integer.
Then:

$n \divides 0$
That is, $n$ divides $0$.


Proof
From Integers form Integral Domain, the concept divides is fully applicable to the integers.
Therefore this result follows directly from Element of Integral Domain Divides Zero.
$\blacksquare$
