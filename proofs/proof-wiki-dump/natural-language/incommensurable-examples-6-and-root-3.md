Example of Incommensurability
$6$ and $\sqrt 3$ are incommensurable.


Proof
From Square Root of Prime is Irrational, $\sqrt 3$ is irrational.
Hence the ratio $6$ to $\sqrt 3$ is irrational.
Hence the result by definition of incommensurable.
$\blacksquare$
