Theorem
$\cos 300 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {5 \pi} 3 = \dfrac 1 2$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 300 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {360 \degrees - 60 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 60 \degrees\)





Cosine of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 2\)





Cosine of $60 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
