Theorem
The operation of multplication on the quaternions $H$ is not commutative.


Proof
By definition of multplication:














\(\ds \mathbf i \times \mathbf j\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \mathbf k\)




















\(\ds \mathbf j \times \mathbf i\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\mathbf k\)









$\blacksquare$
