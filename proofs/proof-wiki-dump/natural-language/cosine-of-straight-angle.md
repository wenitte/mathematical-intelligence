Theorem
$\cos 180 \degrees = \cos \pi = -1$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof
A direct implementation of Cosine of Multiple of Pi:

$\forall n \in \Z: \cos n \pi = \paren {-1}^n$
In this case, $n = 1$ and so:

$\cos \pi = -1^1 = -1$
$\blacksquare$
