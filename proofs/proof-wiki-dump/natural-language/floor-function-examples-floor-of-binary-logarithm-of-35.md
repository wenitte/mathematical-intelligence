Theorem
$\floor {\lg 35} = 5$
where:

$\lg x$ denotes the binary logarithm ($\log_2$) of $x$
$\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof













\(\ds \lg 32\)

\(\le\)

\(\, \ds \lg 35 \, \)

\(\, \ds < \, \)



\(\ds \lg 64\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 5\)

\(\le\)

\(\, \ds \lg 35 \, \)

\(\, \ds < \, \)



\(\ds 6\)









Hence $5$ is the floor of $\lg 35$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$
