Corollary to Derivative of Identity Function
$\map {\dfrac {\d} {\d x} } {c x} = c$
where $c$ is a constant.


Proof













\(\ds \map {\frac \d {\d x} } {c x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds c \frac \d {\d x} x\)





Derivative of Constant Multiple














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds c \times 1\)





Derivative of Identity Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds c\)









$\blacksquare$
