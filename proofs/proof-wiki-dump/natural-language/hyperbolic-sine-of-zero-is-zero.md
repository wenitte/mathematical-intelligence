Theorem
$\map \sinh 0 = 0$
where $\sinh$ denotes the hyperbolic sine.


Proof













\(\ds \map \sinh 0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {e^0 - e^{-0} } 2\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {1 - 1} 2\)





Definition of Integer Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)









$\blacksquare$
