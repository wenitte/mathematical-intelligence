Example of Use of Legendre Symbol
$\paren {\dfrac 2 7} = 1$


Proof
From Quadratic Residue Examples: $7$:
The set of quadratic residues modulo $7$ is:

$\set {1, 2, 4}$

That is, $2$ is a quadratic residue of $7$:

$2^2 \equiv 4 \pmod 7$
Hence the result by definition of Legendre symbol.
$\blacksquare$
