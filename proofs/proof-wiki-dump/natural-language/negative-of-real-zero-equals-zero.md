Theorem
Let $0$ denote the identity for addition in the real numbers $\R$.
Then:

$-0 = 0$


Proof













\(\ds -0 + 0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





Real Number Axiom $\R \text A4$: Inverses for Addition








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds -0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





Real Addition Identity is Zero: Corollary



$\blacksquare$
