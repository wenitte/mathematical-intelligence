Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $31$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {31} = 0 \cdotp \dot 03225 \, 80645 \, 1612 \dot 9$
This sequence is A021035 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
