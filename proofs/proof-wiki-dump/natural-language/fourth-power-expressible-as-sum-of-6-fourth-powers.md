Theorem
$28 \, 561$ can be expressed as the sum of $6$ fourth powers:

$28 \, 561 = 13^4 = 12^4 + 8^4 + 7^4 + 6^4 + 2^4 + 2^4$


Proof













\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 12^4 + 8^4 + 7^4 + 6^4 + 2^4 + 2^4\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 20 \, 736 + 4096 + 2401 + 1296 + 16 + 16\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 28 \, 561\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 13^4\)









$\blacksquare$
