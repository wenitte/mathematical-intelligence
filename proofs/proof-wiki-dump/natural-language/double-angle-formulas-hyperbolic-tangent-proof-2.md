Theorem
$\tanh 2 x = \dfrac {2 \tanh x} {1 + \tanh^2 x}$


Proof













\(\ds \tanh 2 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tanh \left({x + x}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\tanh x + \tanh x} {1 + \tanh x \tanh x}\)





Hyperbolic Tangent of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2 \tanh x} {1 + \tanh^2 x}\)









$\blacksquare$
