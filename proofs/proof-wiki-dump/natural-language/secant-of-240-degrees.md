Theorem
$\sec 240 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {4 \pi} 3 = - 2$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 240 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 120 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 120 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -2\)





Secant of $120 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
