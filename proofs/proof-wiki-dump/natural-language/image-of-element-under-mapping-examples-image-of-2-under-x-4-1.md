Example of Image of Element under Mapping
Let $f: \R \to \R$ be the mapping defined as:

$\forall x \in \R: \map f x = x^4 - 1$
The image of $2$ is:

$\map f 2 = 15$


Proof
$2^4 - 1 = 16 - 1 = 15$
$\blacksquare$
