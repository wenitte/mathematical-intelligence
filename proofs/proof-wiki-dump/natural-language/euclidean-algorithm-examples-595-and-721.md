Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $595$ and $721$ is found to be:

$\gcd \set {595, 721} = 7$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 721\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 595 + 126\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 595\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 \times 126 - 35\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 126\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 \times 35 - 14\)










\(\text {(4)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 35\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 14 + 7\)










\(\text {(5)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 14\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times  7\)










Thus:

$\gcd \set {595, 721} = 7$
$\blacksquare$
