Theorem
Let $\struct {G, \circ}$ be a group.
Let $H$ be a subgroup of $G$.
Then:

$H^{-1} = H$
where $H^{-1}$ is the inverse of $H$.


Proof
As $H$ is a subgroup of $G$:

$\forall h \in H: h^{-1} \in H$
The result follows.
$\blacksquare$
