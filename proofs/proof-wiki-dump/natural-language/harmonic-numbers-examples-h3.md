Example of Harmonic Number
$H_3 = \dfrac {11} 6$
where $H_3$ denotes the third harmonic number.


Proof













\(\ds H_3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 1}^3 \frac 1 k\)





Definition of Harmonic Number














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 1 + \frac 1 2 + \frac 1 3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {11} 6\)









$\blacksquare$
