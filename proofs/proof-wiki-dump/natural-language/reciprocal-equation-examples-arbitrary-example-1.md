Example of Reciprocal Equation
The equation:

$x^2 + 1 = 0$
is an example of a reciprocal equation.


Proof
Replacing $x$ with $\dfrac 1 x$ gives:

$\dfrac 1 {x^2} + 1 = 0$
which, on multiplying through by $x^2$, gives:

$1 + x^2 = 0$
$\blacksquare$
