Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {x^3 \rd x} {x^4 - a^4} = \frac {\ln \size {x^4 - a^4} } 4 + C$


Proof













\(\ds \frac \d {\d x} x^4\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 x^3\)





Primitive of Power








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \frac {x^3 \rd x} {x^4 - a^4}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\ln \size {x^4 - a^4} } 4 + C\)





Primitive of Function under its Derivative



$\blacksquare$
