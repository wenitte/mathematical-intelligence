Theorem
The haversine is an even function:

$\forall \theta \in \R: \map \hav {-\theta} = \hav \theta$


Proof













\(\ds \map \hav {-\theta}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 2 \paren {1 - \map \cos {-\theta} }\)





Definition of Haversine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 2 \paren {1 - \cos \theta}\)





Cosine Function is Even














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \hav \theta\)





Definition of Haversine



$\blacksquare$
