Examples of Integer Division













\(\ds 1 \div \paren {-7}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)

\(\ds \rem 1\)


















\(\ds -2 \div \paren {-7}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)

\(\ds \rem 5\)


















\(\ds 61 \div \paren {-7}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -8\)

\(\ds \rem 5\)


















\(\ds -59 \div \paren {-7}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 9\)

\(\ds \rem 4\)









Proof
We have that:

$29 = 3 \times 8 + 5$















\(\ds 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0 \times \paren {-7} + 1\)




















\(\ds -2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times \paren {-7} + 5\)




















\(\ds 61\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-8} \times \paren {-7} + 5\)




















\(\ds -59\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 9 \times \paren {-7} + 4\)










Hence the result by definition of integer division.
$\blacksquare$
