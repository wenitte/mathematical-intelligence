Theorem
Let $\map u x$, $\map v x$ and $\map w x$ be real functions differentiable on the open interval $I$.
Then:

$\forall x \in I: \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {u v w} = u v \dfrac {\d w} {\d x} + u w \dfrac {\d v} {\d x} + v w \dfrac {\d u} {\d x}$


Proof
Let $y = u v w$.
Then:














\(\ds y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds u \paren {v w}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\d u} {\d x} \paren {v w} + u \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {v w}\)





Product Rule for Derivatives














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds v w \dfrac {\d u} {\d x} + u \paren {w \dfrac {\d v} {\d x} + v \dfrac {\d w} {\d x} }\)





Product Rule for Derivatives














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds u v \dfrac {\d w} {\d x} + u w \dfrac {\d v} {\d x} + v w \dfrac {\d u} {\d x}\)





simplification



$\blacksquare$
