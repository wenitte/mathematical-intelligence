Theorem
Let $x \in \R$ be a real number.
Let $n \in \Z$ be an integer.

Then:

$\size {x^{2 n} } = x^{2 n}$


Proof
From Even Power is Non-Negative:

$x^{2 n} \ge 0$
The result follows from the definition of absolute value.
$\blacksquare$
