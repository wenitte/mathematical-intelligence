Example of Cyclic Permutation of Kaprekar Number
$972$ is a cyclic permutation of the $3$-digit Kaprekar number $297$.
Thus we have:














\(\ds 972^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 944 \, 784\)




















\(\ds 944 + 784\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1728\)




















\(\ds 1 + 728\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 729\)









and it is seen that $729$ is another cyclic permutation of $297$.
$\blacksquare$
