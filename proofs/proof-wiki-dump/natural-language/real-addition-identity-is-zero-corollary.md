Corollary to Real Addition Identity is Zero
$\forall x, y \in \R: x + y = x \implies y = 0$


Proof













\(\ds x + y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \paren {-x} + \paren {x + y}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-x} + x\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \paren {\paren {-x} + x} + y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-x} + x\)





Real Number Axiom $\R \text A1$: Associativity of Addition








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 0 + y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





Real Number Axiom $\R \text A4$: Inverses for Addition








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





Real Addition Identity is Zero



$\blacksquare$
