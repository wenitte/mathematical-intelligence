Theorem
The complete graph $K_1$ of order $1$ is the edgeless graph $N_1$.


Proof
By definition, $K_1$ has $1$ vertex.
From Complete Graph is Regular, $K_1$ is $0$-regular.
Hence the result from Graph is 0-Regular iff Edgeless.
$\blacksquare$
