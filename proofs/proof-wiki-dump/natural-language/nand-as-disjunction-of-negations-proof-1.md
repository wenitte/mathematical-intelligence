Theorem
$p \uparrow q \dashv \vdash \neg p \lor \neg q$


Proof













\(\ds p \uparrow q\)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds \map \neg {p \land q}\)





Definition of Logical NAND














\(\ds \)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds \neg p \lor \neg q\)





De Morgan's Laws: Disjunction of Negations



$\blacksquare$
