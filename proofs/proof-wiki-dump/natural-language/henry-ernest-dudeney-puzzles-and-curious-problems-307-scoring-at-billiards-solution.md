Puzzles and Curious Problems by Henry Ernest Dudeney: $307$
Scoring at Billiards
What is the highest score that you can make in two consecutive shots at billiards?


Solution
$18$.


Proof
$(1): \quad$ Cannon $(2)$, pot the red $(3)$, and in-off red $(3)$, leaving white up, total $8$.
$(2): \quad$ Cannon $(2)$, pot the white $(2)$, pot the red $(3)$, and in-off red $(3)$, total $10$.
$\blacksquare$
