Theorem
$\csc 135 \degrees = \csc \dfrac {3 \pi} 4 = \sqrt 2$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 135 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \csc {180 \degrees - 45 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \csc 45 \degrees\)





Cosecant of Supplementary Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 2\)





Cosecant of $45 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
