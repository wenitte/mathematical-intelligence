Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $7$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 7 = 0 \cdotp \dot 14285 \, \dot 7$
This sequence is A020806 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
