Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $13$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {13} = 0 \cdotp \dot 07692 \dot 3 \ldots$
This sequence is A021017 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
