Theorem
Let $M = \struct {S, \mathscr I}$ be a matroid.
Let $\rho : \powerset S \to \Z$ be the rank function of $M$.

Then:

$\map \rho \O = 0$


Proof
By matroid axiom $(\text I 1)$:

$\O$ is independent
From Rank of Independent Subset Equals Cardinality:

$\map \rho \O = \size \O$
From Cardinality of Empty Set:

$\size \O = 0$
The result follows.
$\blacksquare$
