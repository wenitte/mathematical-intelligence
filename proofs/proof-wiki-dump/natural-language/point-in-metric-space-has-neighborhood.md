Theorem
Let $M = \struct {A, d}$ be a metric space.
Let $a \in A$ be a point in $M$.

Then there exists some neighborhood of $a$ in $M$.


Proof
Let $a \in A$.
Then $A$ is a neighborhood of $a$ in $M$.
$\blacksquare$
