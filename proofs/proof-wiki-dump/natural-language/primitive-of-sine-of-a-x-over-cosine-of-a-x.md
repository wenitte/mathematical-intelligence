Theorem
$\ds \int \dfrac {\sin a x} {\cos a x} \rd x = -\dfrac 1 a \ln \size {\cos a x} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \dfrac {\sin a x} {\cos a x} \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \cot a x \rd x\)





Tangent is Sine divided by Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\dfrac 1 a \ln \size {\cos a x} + C\)





Primitive of $\tan a x$: Cosine Form



$\blacksquare$
