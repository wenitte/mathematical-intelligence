Theorem
$\cos 255^\circ = \cos \dfrac {17 \pi} {12} = - \dfrac {\sqrt 6 - \sqrt 2} 4$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 255^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos \left({360^\circ - 105^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 105^\circ\)





Cosine of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds - \frac {\sqrt 6 - \sqrt 2} 4\)





Cosine of 105 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
