Theorem
Let $a \in \Z$ be an integer such that $a \ne 0$.
Then:

$\gcd \set {a, 0} = \size a$
where $\gcd$ denotes greatest common divisor (GCD).


Proof
Follows from:

Integer Divides Zero
and:

GCD for Negative Integers.
$\blacksquare$
