Example of Octal Notation
The integer expressed in octal as $273$ is expressed in decimal as $187$.


Proof













\(\ds 273_8\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 8^2 + 7 \times 8^1 + 3 \times 8^0\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 128 + 56 + 3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 187\)









$\blacksquare$
