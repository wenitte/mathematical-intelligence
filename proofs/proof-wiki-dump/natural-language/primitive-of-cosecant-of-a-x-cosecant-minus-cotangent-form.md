Theorem
$\ds \int \csc a x \rd x = \frac 1 a \ln \size {\csc a x - \cot a x} + C$
where $\csc a x - \cot a x \ne 0$.


Proof













\(\ds \int \csc x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\csc x - \cot x}\)





Primitive of $\csc x$: Cosecant minus Cotangent Form








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \csc a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \ln \size {\csc a x - \cot a x} + C\)





Primitive of Function of Constant Multiple



$\blacksquare$
