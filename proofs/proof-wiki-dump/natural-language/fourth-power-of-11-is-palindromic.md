Theorem
$11^4 = 14641$


Proof
From Square of 11 is Palindromic:

$11^2 = 121$
Then from Square of 121 is Palindromic:

$121^2 = 14641 = \paren {11^2}^2 = 11^4$
$\blacksquare$
