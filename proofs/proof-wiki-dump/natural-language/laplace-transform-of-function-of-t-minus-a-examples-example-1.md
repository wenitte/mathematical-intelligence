Example of Use of Laplace Transform of Function of t minus a
Let $\laptrans f$ denote the Laplace transform of the real function $f$.


$\laptrans {\paren {t - 2}^3} = \dfrac {6 e^{-2 s} } {s^4}$
where $t > 2$.


Proof













\(\ds \laptrans {\paren {t - 2}^3}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e^{-2 s} \laptrans {t^3}\)





Laplace Transform of Function of t minus a














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds e^{-2 s} \dfrac {3!} {s^4}\)





Laplace Transform of Positive Integer Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {6 e^{-2 s} } {s^4}\)





simplification



$\blacksquare$
