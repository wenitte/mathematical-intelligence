Theorem













\(\ds \prod_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac {\paren {5 n - 3} \paren {5 n - 2} } {\paren {5 n - 4} \paren {5 n - 1} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\frac 2 1 \times \frac 3 4 } \times \paren {\frac 7 6 \times \frac 8 9 } \times \paren {\frac {12} {11} \times \frac {13} {14} } \times \cdots\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \phi\)











Proof













\(\ds \prod_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac {\paren {5 n - 3} \paren {5 n - 2} } {\paren {5 n - 4} \paren {5 n - 1} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \prod_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac {\paren {5 n - 3} \paren {5 n - 2} \times \dfrac 1 5 \times \dfrac 1 5} {\paren {5 n - 4} \paren {5 n - 1} \times \dfrac 1 5 \times \dfrac 1 5}\)





multiplying top and bottom by $\dfrac 1 5 \times \dfrac 1 5$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \prod_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac {\paren {n - \dfrac 3 5} \paren {n - \dfrac 2 5} } {\paren {n - \dfrac 4 5} \paren {n - \dfrac 1 5} }\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac { \map \Gamma {\dfrac 1 5} \map \Gamma {\dfrac 4 5} } {\map \Gamma {\dfrac 2 5} \map \Gamma {\dfrac 3 5} }\)





Infinite Product of Product of Sequence of n plus alpha over Sequence of n plus beta














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac \pi {\map \sin {\dfrac \pi 5} } \times \dfrac {\map \sin {2 \times \dfrac \pi 5} } \pi\)





Euler's Reflection Formula














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\map \sin {2 \times \dfrac \pi 5} } {\map \sin {\dfrac \pi 5} }\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2 \map \sin {\dfrac \pi 5} \map \cos {\dfrac \pi 5} } {\map \sin {\dfrac \pi 5} }\)





Double Angle Formula for Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \map \cos {\dfrac \pi 5}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \phi\)
