Example of Algebraic Number
$\sqrt [3] 6$ is an algebraic number.


Proof













\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt [3] 6\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x^3 - 6\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)









Hence the result by definition of algebraic number.
$\blacksquare$
