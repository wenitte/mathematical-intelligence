Theorem
$\sec \left({x + 2 \pi}\right) = \sec x$


Proof













\(\ds \sec \left({x + 2 \pi}\right)\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\cos \left({x + 2 \pi}\right)}\)





Secant is Reciprocal of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\cos x}\)





Cosine of Angle plus Full Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec x\)





Secant is Reciprocal of Cosine



$\blacksquare$
