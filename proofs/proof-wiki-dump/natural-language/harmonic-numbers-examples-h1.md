Example of Harmonic Number
$H_1 = 1$
where $H_1$ denotes the first harmonic number.


Proof













\(\ds H_1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 1}^1 \frac 1 k\)





Definition of Harmonic Number














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
