Theorem
$\tan \theta \cot \theta = 1$


Proof













\(\ds \tan \theta \cot \theta\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sin \theta} {\cos \theta} \cot \theta\)





Tangent is Sine divided by Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sin \theta} {\cos \theta} \frac {\cos \theta} {\sin \theta}\)





Cotangent is Cosine divided by Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sin \theta} {\sin \theta} \frac {\cos \theta} {\cos \theta}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
