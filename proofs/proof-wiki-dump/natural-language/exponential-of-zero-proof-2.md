Theorem
$\exp 0 = 1$


Proof
Using the definition of the exponential as a limit of a sequence:














\(\ds \exp 0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \lim_{n \mathop \to \infty} \left({1 + \frac 0 n}\right)^n\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
