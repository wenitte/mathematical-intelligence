Theorem
The ring of Gaussian integers:

$\struct {\Z \sqbrk i, +, \times}$
forms a principal ideal domain.


Proof
Follows immediately from:

Gaussian Integers form Euclidean Domain
Euclidean Domain is Principal Ideal Domain.
$\blacksquare$
