Theorem
Let $A, B \in \Sigma$ such that $A \subseteq B$.

Then:

$\map \Pr A \le \map \Pr B$


Proof
As by definition a probability measure is a measure, we can directly use the result Measure is Monotone.
$\blacksquare$
