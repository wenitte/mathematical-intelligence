Theorem
$S \cup \paren {S \cap T} = S \cap \paren {S \cup T}$


Proof













\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds S \cup \paren {S \cap T}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\leadstoandfrom\)







\(\ds \paren {S \cup S} \cap \paren {S \cup T}\)





Union Distributes over Intersection














\(\ds \)

\(\leadstoandfrom\)







\(\ds S \cap \paren {S \cup T}\)





Set Union is Idempotent



$\blacksquare$
