Corollary to Multiplication by Negative Real Number
$\forall x \in \R: \paren {-1} \times x = -x$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {-1} \times x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {1 \times x}\)





Multiplication by Negative Real Number














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -x\)





Real Number Axiom $\R \text M3$: Identity Element for Multiplication



$\blacksquare$
