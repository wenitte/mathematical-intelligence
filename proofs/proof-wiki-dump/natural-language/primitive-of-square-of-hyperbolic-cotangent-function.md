Theorem
$\ds \int \coth^2 x \rd x = x - \coth x + C$
where $C$ is an arbitrary constant.


Proof













\(\ds \int \coth^2 x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \paren {1 + \csch^2 x} \rd x\)





Difference of Squares of Hyperbolic Cotangent and Cosecant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int 1 \rd x + \int \csch^2 x \rd x\)





Linear Combination of Primitives














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int 1 \rd x - \coth x + C\)





Primitive of Square of Hyperbolic Cosecant Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x - \coth x + C\)





Primitive of Constant



$\blacksquare$
