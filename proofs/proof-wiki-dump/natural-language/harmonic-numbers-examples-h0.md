Example of Harmonic Number
$H_0 = 0$
where $H_0$ denotes the zeroth harmonic number.


Proof













\(\ds H_0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 1}^0 \frac 1 k\)





Definition of Harmonic Number














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





Definition of Vacuous Summation



$\blacksquare$
