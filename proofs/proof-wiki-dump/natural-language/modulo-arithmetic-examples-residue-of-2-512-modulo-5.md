Example of Modulo Arithmetic
The least positive residue of $2^{512} \pmod 5$ is $1$.


Proof













\(\ds 2^4\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 1\)

\(\ds \pmod 5\)



as $2^4 = 16 = 3 \times 5 + 1$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \paren {2^4}^{128}\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 1^{128}\)

\(\ds \pmod 5\)



Congruence of Powers








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 2^{512}\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 1\)

\(\ds \pmod 5\)








Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
