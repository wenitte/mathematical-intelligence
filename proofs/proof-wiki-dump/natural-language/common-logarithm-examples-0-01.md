Example of Common Logarithm
The common logarithm of $0 \cdotp 01$ is:

$\log_{10} 0 \cdotp 01 = -2$


Proof













\(\ds 0 \cdotp 01\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 {10 \times 10}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10^{-2}\)





Definition of Integer Power








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \log_{10} 0 \cdotp 01\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -2\)









$\blacksquare$
