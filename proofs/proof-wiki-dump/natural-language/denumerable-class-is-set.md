Theorem
Let $A$ be a class.
Let $\N$ be the natural numbers.
Suppose that $F: \N \to A$ is a bijection.

Then $A$ is a set.


Proof
By the Axiom of Infinity, $\N$ is a set.
Thus by the Axiom of Replacement, $A$ is also a set.
$\blacksquare$
