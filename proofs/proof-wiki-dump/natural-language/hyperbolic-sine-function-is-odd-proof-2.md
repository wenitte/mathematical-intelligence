Theorem
$\map \sinh {-x} = -\sinh x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \sinh {-x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -i \, \map \sin {-i x}\)





Hyperbolic Sine in terms of Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds i \, \map \sin {i x}\)





Sine Function is Odd














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sinh x\)





Hyperbolic Sine in terms of Sine



$\blacksquare$
