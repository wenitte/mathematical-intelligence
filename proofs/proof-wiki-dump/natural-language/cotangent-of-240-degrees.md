Theorem
$\cot 240^\circ = \cot \dfrac {4 \pi} 3 = \dfrac {\sqrt 3} 3$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 240^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot \left({360^\circ - 120^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 120^\circ\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sqrt 3} 3\)





Cotangent of 120 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
