Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $272$ and $1479$ is:

$\gcd \set {272, 1479} = 17$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 1479\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 \times 272 + 119\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 272\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 119 + 34\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 119\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 34 + 17\)










\(\text {(4)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 34\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 17\)









Thus:

$\gcd \set {272, 1479} = 17$
$\blacksquare$
