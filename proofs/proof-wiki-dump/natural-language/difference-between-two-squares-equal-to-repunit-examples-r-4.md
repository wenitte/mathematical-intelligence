Example of Difference between Two Squares equal to Repunit
We have that:














\(\ds 1111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 101 \times 11\)




















\(\ds 1111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 1111\)
























\(\ds 1111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1111 \times 1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {1111 + 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 556\)




















\(\ds \frac {1111 - 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 555\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 556^2 - 555^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 309 \, 136 - 308 \, 025\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1111\)
























\(\ds 1111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 101 \times 11\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {101 + 11} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 56\)




















\(\ds \frac {101 - 11} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 45\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 56^2 - 45^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3136 - 2025\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1111\)









$\blacksquare$
