Theorem
The product of the number of edges, edges per face and faces of a cube is $288$.


Proof
A cube has:

$12$ edges
and:

$6$ faces.
Each face is a square, and so has $4$ edges.
Hence:

$12 \times 6 \times 4 = 288$
$\blacksquare$
