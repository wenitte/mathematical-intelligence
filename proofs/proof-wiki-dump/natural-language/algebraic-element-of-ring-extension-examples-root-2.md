Example of Algebraic Element of Ring Extension
$\sqrt 2$ is an algebraic element over the integers $\Z$.


Proof
We have that:














\(\ds x^2 - 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \pm \sqrt 2\)





Quadratic Formula



Thus $\sqrt 2$ is a root of $x^2 - 2$.
Hence the result by definition of algebraic element of $\Z$.
$\blacksquare$
