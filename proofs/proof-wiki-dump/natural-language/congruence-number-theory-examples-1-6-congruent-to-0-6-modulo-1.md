Example of Congruence Modulo an Integer
$1 \cdotp 6 \equiv 0 \cdotp 6 \pmod 1$


Proof













\(\ds 1 \cdotp 6\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 1 + 0 \cdotp 6\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 1 \cdotp 6\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 0 \cdotp 6\)

\(\ds \pmod 1\)







$\blacksquare$
