Example of Use of LCM from Prime Decomposition
The lowest common multiple of $132$ and $154$ is:

$\lcm \set {132, 154} = 924$


Proof













\(\ds 132\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^2 \times 3 \times 11\)




















\(\ds 154\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 7 \times 11\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 132\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^2 \times 3^1 \times 7^0 \times 11^1\)




















\(\ds 154\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^1 \times 3^0 \times 7^1 \times 11^1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \lcm \set {132, 154}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^2 \times 3^1 \times 7^1 \times 11^1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 924\)









$\blacksquare$
