Theorem
$\csc 240 \degrees = \csc \dfrac {4 \pi} 3 = -\dfrac {2 \sqrt 3} 3$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 240 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \csc {360 \degrees - 120 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 120 \degrees\)





Cosecant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\frac {2 \sqrt 3} 3\)





Cosecant of $120 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
