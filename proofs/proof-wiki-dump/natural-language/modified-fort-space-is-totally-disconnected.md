Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau_{a, b} }$ be a modified Fort space.

Then $T$ is totally disconnected.


Proof
We have:

Modified Fort Space is Scattered
Modified Fort Space is $T_1$
We also have:

Scattered $T_1$ Space is Totally Disconnected
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
