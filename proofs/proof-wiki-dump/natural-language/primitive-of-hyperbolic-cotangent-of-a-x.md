Theorem
$\ds \int \coth a x \rd x = \frac {\ln \size {\sinh a x} } a + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \coth x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\sinh x} + C\)





Primitive of $\coth x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \coth a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \paren {\ln \size {\sinh a x} } + C\)





Primitive of Function of Constant Multiple














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\ln \size {\sinh a x} } a + C\)





simplifying



$\blacksquare$
