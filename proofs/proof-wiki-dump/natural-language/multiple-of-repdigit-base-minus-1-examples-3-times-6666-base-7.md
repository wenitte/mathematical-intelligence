Example of Multiple of Repdigit Base minus 1
$3 \times 6666$ (base $7$) is equal to $26664$ (base $7$).


Proof
From Multiple of Repdigit Base minus 1:

the first digit of $3 \times 6666$ is $3 - 1 = 2$
the last digit of $3 \times 6666$ is $7 - 3 = 4$
there are $4 - 1 = 3$ instances of $6$ in between.
Hence:

$3 \times 6666 = 26664$
$\blacksquare$
