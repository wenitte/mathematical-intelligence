Example of Reversal of Number Multiplied by 11
$11$ multiplied by the reversal of $45 \, 173$ does not equal the reversal of the product of $45 \, 173$ and $11$.


Proof
  45,173     37,154
 x    11    x    11
 -------    -------
  45,173     37,154
 451,730    371,540
 -------    -------
 496,903    408,694
 -------    -------

$\blacksquare$
