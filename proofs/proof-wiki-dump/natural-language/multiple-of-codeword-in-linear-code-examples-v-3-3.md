Example of Multiple of Codeword in $V \paren {3, 3}$
In the master code $V \paren {3, 3}$, the codeword $102$ is multiplied by $\eqclass 2 3$ thus:

$2 \paren {102} = 201$


Proof
Taking the elements of $201$ in turn, we have:














\(\ds 2 \times 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 2\)

\(\ds \pmod 3\)






















\(\ds 2 \times 0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 0\)

\(\ds \pmod 3\)






















\(\ds 2 \times 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 1\)

\(\ds \pmod 3\)







Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
