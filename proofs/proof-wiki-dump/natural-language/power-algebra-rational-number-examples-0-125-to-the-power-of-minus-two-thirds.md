Example of Rational Power
$0 \cdotp 125^{-2/3} = 4$


Proof













\(\ds 0 \cdotp 125^{-2/3}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\dfrac 1 8}^{-2/3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 8^{2/3}\)





Definition of Rational Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 64^{1/3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt [3] {64}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4\)









$\blacksquare$
