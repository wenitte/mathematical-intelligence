Theorem
$\cot 270 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {3 \pi} 2 = 0$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 270 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cot {360 \degrees - 90 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 90 \degrees\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





Cotangent of Right Angle



$\blacksquare$
