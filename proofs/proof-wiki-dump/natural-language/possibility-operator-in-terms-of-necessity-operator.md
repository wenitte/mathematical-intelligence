Theorem
The possibility operator $\pos$ can be expressed in terms of the necessity operator $\nec$ thus:

$\pos P \iff \lnot \nec \lnot P$


Proof









\(\ds \forall P: \, \)



\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds \pos P\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\leadstoandfrom\)







\(\ds \exists w: \map P w\)





Definition of Possibility Operator














\(\ds \)

\(\leadstoandfrom\)







\(\ds \lnot \forall w: \lnot \map P w\)





Assertion of Existence














\(\ds \)

\(\leadstoandfrom\)







\(\ds \lnot \nec \lnot P\)





Definition of Necessity Operator



$\blacksquare$
