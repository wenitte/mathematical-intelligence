Theorem
Let $x \in \R$ be a real number.

$x^{\overline 0} = 1$
where $x^{\overline 0}$ denotes the rising factorial.


Proof













\(\ds x^{\overline 0}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \prod_{j \mathop = 0}^{-1} \paren {x + j}\)





Definition of Rising Factorial














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)





Product is Vacuous



$\blacksquare$
