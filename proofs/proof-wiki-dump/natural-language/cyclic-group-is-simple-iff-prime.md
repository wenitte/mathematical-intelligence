Theorem
Let $G$ be a cyclic group.

Then $G$ is simple if and only if $G$ is a prime group.


Proof
Let $G$ be a cyclic group.
From Cyclic Group is Abelian it follows that $G$ is an abelian group.
The result follows from Abelian Group is Simple iff Prime.
$\blacksquare$
