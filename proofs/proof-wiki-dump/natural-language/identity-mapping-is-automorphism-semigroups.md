Theorem
Let $\struct {S, \circ}$ be a semigroup.
Then $I_S: \struct {S, \circ} \to \struct {S, \circ}$ is a semigroup automorphism.


Proof
The main result Identity Mapping is Automorphism holds directly.
$\blacksquare$
