Example of Reciprocal of Logarithm
The reciprocal of $\log_{10} 2$ is $\log_2 10$.


Proof
From Reciprocal of Logarithm:
$\dfrac 1 {\log_x y} = \log_y x$

Let $x = 10, y = 2$:
Then:
$\dfrac 1 {\log_{10} 2} = \log_2 10$
$\blacksquare$
