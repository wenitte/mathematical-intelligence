Example of Number whose Divisor Sum is Square
$\map {\sigma_1} {22} = 36 = 6^2$


Proof
From $\sigma_1$ of $22$:

$\map {\sigma_1} {22} = 36$
which equals $6^2$.
$\blacksquare$
