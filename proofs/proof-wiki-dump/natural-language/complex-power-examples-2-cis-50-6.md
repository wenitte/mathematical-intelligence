Example of Complex Power
$\paren {2 \cis 50 \degrees}^6 = 32 - 32 \sqrt 3 i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {2 \cis 50 \degrees}^6\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^6 \map \cis {6 \times 50 \degrees}\)





De Moivre's Formula














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 64 \cis 300 \degrees\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 64 \paren {\cos 300 \degrees + i \sin 300 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 64 \times \paren {\dfrac 1 2} + 64 i \paren {-\dfrac {\sqrt 3} 2}\)





Cosine of $300 \degrees$, Sine of $300 \degrees$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 32 - 32 \sqrt 3 i\)





simplifying



$\blacksquare$
