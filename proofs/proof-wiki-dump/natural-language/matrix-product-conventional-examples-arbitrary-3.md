Example of (Conventional) Matrix Product
$\begin {bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 6 \end {bmatrix} \begin {bmatrix} 5 & 3 & 1 \end {bmatrix} = \begin {bmatrix} 10 & 6 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 30 & 18 & 6 \end {bmatrix}$


Proof













\(\ds \begin {bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 6 \end {bmatrix} \begin {bmatrix} 5 & 3 & 1 \end {bmatrix}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \begin {bmatrix} 2 \times 5 & 2 \times 3 & 2 \times 1 \\ 0 \times 5 & 0 \times 3 & 0 \times 1 \\ 6 \times 5 & 6 \times 3 & 6 \times 1 \end {bmatrix}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \begin {bmatrix} 10 & 6 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 30 & 18 & 6 \end {bmatrix}\)









$\blacksquare$
