Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau}$ be a finite complement topology on an infinite set $S$.

Then $T$ is a locally connected space.


Proof
We have that a Finite Complement Space is Irreducible.
The result follows from Irreducible Space is Locally Connected.
$\blacksquare$
