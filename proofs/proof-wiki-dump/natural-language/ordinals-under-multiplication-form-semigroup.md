Theorem
$\struct {\On, \times}$ forms an semigroup, where:

$\On$ denotes the class of all ordinals
$\times$ denotes ordinal multiplication.


Proof
The result follows from Ordinal Multiplication is Closed and Ordinal Multiplication is Associative.
$\blacksquare$
