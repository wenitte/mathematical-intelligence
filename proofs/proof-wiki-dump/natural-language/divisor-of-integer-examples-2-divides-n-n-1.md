Theorem
Let $n$ be an integer.
Then:

$2 \divides n \paren {n + 1}$
where $\divides$ indicates divisibility.


Proof
Suppose $n$ is even.
Then $2 \divides n$ by definition.
Hence from Divisor Divides Multiple:

$2 \divides n \paren {n + 1}$
$\Box$

Suppose $n$ is odd.
Then $n + 1$ is even
Then $2 \divides n + 1$ by definition.
Hence from Divisor Divides Multiple:

$2 \divides n \paren {n + 1}$
$\blacksquare$
