Theorem
Let $G$ be a group whose order is $p^n$ where $p$ is a prime number and $n$ is a positive integer.

Then $G$ is solvable.


Proof
A direct consequence of Factors of Composition Series for Prime Power Group and the definition of solvable group.
$\blacksquare$
