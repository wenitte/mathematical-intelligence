Theorem
Let $f: S \to T$ be a bijection.

Then:

$\paren {f^{-1} }^{-1} = f$
where $f^{-1}$ is the inverse of $f$.


Proof
A mapping is a relation.
Thus it follows that Inverse of Inverse Relation can be applied directly.
$\blacksquare$
