Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $79$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {79} = 0 \cdotp \dot 01265 \, 82278 \, 48 \dot 1$
This sequence is A021083 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
