Example of Divisor Sum of Integer
$\map {\sigma_1} {19 \, 845} = 41 \, 382$
where $\sigma_1$ denotes the divisor sum function.


Proof
We have that:

$19 \, 845 = 3^4 \times 5 \times 7^2$

Hence:














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {19 \, 845}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {3^5 - 1} {3 - 1} \times \paren {5 + 1} \times \dfrac {7^3 - 1} {7 - 1}\)





Divisor Sum of Integer














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {242} 2 \times 6 \times \dfrac {342} 6\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 121 \times 6 \times 57\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11^2 \times \paren {2 \times 3} \times \paren {3 \times 19}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 3^2 \times 11^2 \times 19\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 41 \, 382\)









$\blacksquare$
