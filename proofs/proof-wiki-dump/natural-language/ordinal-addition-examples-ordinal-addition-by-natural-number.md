Theorem
Let $x$ be an ordinal.
Let $x^+$ denote the successor of $x$.
Let $n$ be a natural number.

Then:

$x + \paren {n + 1} = \paren {x + n}^+$
where $+$ denotes ordinal addition.


Proof













\(\ds x + \paren {n + 1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x + n^+\)





Ordinal Addition by One














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {x + n}^+\)





Definition of Ordinal Addition



$\blacksquare$
