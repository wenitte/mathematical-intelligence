Example of Complex Addition
$\paren {3 + 2 i} + \paren {5 + 6 i} = 8 + 8 i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {3 + 2 i} + \paren {5 + 6 i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 + 5} + \paren {2 + 6} i\)





Definition of Complex Addition














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 8 + 8 i\)









$\blacksquare$
