Theorem
Let $G$ be a group
The center $\map Z G$ of $G$ is a normal subgroup of $G$.


Proof
Recall that Center of Group is Abelian Subgroup.
Since $g x = x g$ for each $g \in G$ and $x \in \map Z G$:

$g \map Z G = \map Z G g$
Thus:

$\map Z G \lhd G$
$\blacksquare$
