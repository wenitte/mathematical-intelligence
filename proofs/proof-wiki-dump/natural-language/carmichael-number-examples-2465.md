Example of Carmichael Number
$2465$ is a Carmichael number:

$\forall a \in \Z: a \perp 2465: a^{2465} \equiv a \pmod {2465}$
while $2465$ is composite.


Proof
We have that:

$2465 = 5 \times 17 \times 29$
and so:














\(\ds 5^2\)

\(\nmid\)







\(\ds 2465\)




















\(\ds 17^2\)

\(\nmid\)







\(\ds 2465\)




















\(\ds 29^2\)

\(\nmid\)







\(\ds 2465\)










We also have that:














\(\ds 2464\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 616 \times 4\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 154 \times 16\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 88 \times 28\)









The result follows by Korselt's Theorem.
$\blacksquare$
