Theorem
Everywhere that the function is defined:

$\map \artanh {\dfrac 1 x} = \arcoth x$
where $\artanh$ and $\arcoth$ denote real area hyperbolic tangent and real area hyperbolic cotangent respectively.


Proof













\(\ds \map \artanh {\dfrac 1 x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds y\)














\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac 1 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tanh y\)





Definition of Real Area Hyperbolic Tangent








\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac 1 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cosh y} {\sinh y}\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Tangent








\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sinh y} {\cosh y}\)














\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \coth y\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Cotangent








\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds \arcoth x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds y\)





Definition of Real Area Hyperbolic Cotangent



$\blacksquare$
