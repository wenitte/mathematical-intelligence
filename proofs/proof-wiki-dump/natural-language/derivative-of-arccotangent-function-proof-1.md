Theorem
$\dfrac {\map \d {\arccot x} } {\d x} = \dfrac {-1} {1 + x^2}$


Proof













\(\ds y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \arccot x\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot y\)





Definition of Real Arccotangent








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {\d x} {\d y}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc^2 y\)





Derivative of Cotangent Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {1 + \cot^2 y}\)





Difference of Squares of Cosecant and Cotangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {1 + x^2}\)





Definition of $x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {\d y} {\d x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {-1} {1 + x^2}\)





Derivative of Inverse Function



$\blacksquare$
