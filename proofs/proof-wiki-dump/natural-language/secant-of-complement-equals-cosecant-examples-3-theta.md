Example of Secant of Complement equals Cosecant
$\map \cos {90 \degrees - 3 \theta} = \csc 3 \theta$


Proof
From Secant of Complement equals Cosecant:

$\map \cos {90 \degrees - \theta} = \csc \theta$
The result follows by substituting $3 \theta$ for $\theta$.
$\blacksquare$
