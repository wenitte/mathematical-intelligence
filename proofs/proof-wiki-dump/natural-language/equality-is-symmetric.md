Theorem
Equality is symmetric.

That is:

$\forall a, b: a = b \implies b = a$


Proof













\(\ds a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds b\)














\(\ds \vdash \ \ \)





\(\ds \map P a\)

\(\iff\)







\(\ds \map P b\)





Leibniz's Law








\(\ds \vdash \ \ \)





\(\ds \map P b\)

\(\iff\)







\(\ds \map P a\)





Biconditional is Commutative








\(\ds \vdash \ \ \)





\(\ds b\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a\)





Leibniz's Law



$\blacksquare$
