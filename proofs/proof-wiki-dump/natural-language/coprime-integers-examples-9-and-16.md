Example of Coprime Integers
$-9$ and $16$ are coprime.


Proof
The positive divisors of $-9$ are $1$, $3$ and $9$.
The positive divisors of $16$ are $1$, $2$, $4$, $8$ and $16$.
The only integer in common between them is $1$.
Hence the result by definition of coprime.
$\blacksquare$
