Sequence

$7$, $17$, $19$, $23$, $29$, $47$, $59$, $61$, $97$, $109$, $113$, $131$, $149$, $167$, $179$, $\ldots$
This sequence is A001913 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
