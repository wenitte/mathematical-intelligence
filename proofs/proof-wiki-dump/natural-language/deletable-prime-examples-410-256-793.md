Example of Deletable Prime
$410 \, 256 \, 793$ is a deletable prime.


Proof
The following sequence can be created by deleting one digit at a time from $410 \, 256 \, 793$:














\(\ds 410 \, 256 \, 793\)

\(\)







\(\ds \)





is prime














\(\ds 41 \, 256 \, 793\)

\(\ds \)

\(\ds 4 \, 125 \, 673\)

\(\ds \)

\(\ds \)
