Example of Reciprocal of Square of 1 Less than Number Base
Expressed in base $6$, the reciprocal of $5^2$ has the expansion:

$\dfrac 1 {5^2} = \sqbrk {0 \cdotp \dot 0123 \dot 5}_6$


Proof
From Square of $1$ Less than Number Base: $5$ we have:

$5^2 = 41_6$
