Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $71$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {71} = 0 \cdotp \dot 01408 \, 45070 \, 42253 \, 52112 \, 67605 \, 63380 \, 2816 \dot 9$
This sequence is A021075 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
