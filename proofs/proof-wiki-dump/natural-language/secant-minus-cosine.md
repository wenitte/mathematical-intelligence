$\sec x - \cos x = \sin x \tan x$

\(\ds \sec x - \cos x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\cos x} - \cos x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {1 - \cos^2 x} {\cos x}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sin^2 x} {\cos x}\)
