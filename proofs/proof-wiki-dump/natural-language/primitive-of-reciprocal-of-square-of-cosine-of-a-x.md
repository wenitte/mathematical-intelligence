Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\cos^2 a x} = \frac {\tan a x} a + C$


Corollary
$\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\cos^2 x} = \tan x + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\cos^2 a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \sec^2 a x \rd x\)





Definition of Cosecant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\tan a x} a + C\)





Primitive of $\sec^2 a x$



$\blacksquare$
