Example of Multiplication of Fractions
$\dfrac 2 3 \times \dfrac 4 7 = \dfrac 8 {21}$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac 2 3 \times \dfrac 4 7\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {2 \times 4} {3 \times 7}\)





Multiplication of Fractions














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 8 {21}\)









$\blacksquare$
