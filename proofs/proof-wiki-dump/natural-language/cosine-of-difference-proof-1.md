Corollary to Cosine of Sum
$\map \cos {a - b} = \cos a \cos b + \sin a \sin b$


Proof













\(\ds \map \cos {a - b}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos a \map \cos {-b} - \sin a \map \sin {-b}\)





Cosine of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos a \cos b - \sin a \map \sin {-b}\)





Cosine Function is Even














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos a \cos b + \sin a \sin b\)





Sine Function is Odd



$\blacksquare$
