Theorem
$\cot 150 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {5 \pi} 6 = -\sqrt 3$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 150 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cot {90 \degrees + 60 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\tan 60 \degrees\)





Cotangent of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sqrt 3\)





Tangent of $60 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
