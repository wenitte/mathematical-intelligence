Example of Power of 2 is Difference between Two Powers
$2^5 = 3^4 - 7^2$


Proof













\(\ds 3^4 - 7^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 81 - 49\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 32\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^5\)









$\blacksquare$
