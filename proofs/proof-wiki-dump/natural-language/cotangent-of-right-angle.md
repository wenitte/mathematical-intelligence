Theorem
$\cot 90 \degrees = \cot \dfrac \pi 2 = 0$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 90 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cos 90 \degrees} {\sin 90 \degrees}\)





Cotangent is Cosine divided by Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 0 1\)





Cosine of Right Angle and Sine of Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)









$\blacksquare$
