Example of Use of Euler $\phi$ Function
$\map \phi 4 = 2$
where $\phi$ denotes the Euler $\phi$ function.


Proof
From Euler Phi Function of Prime Power: Corollary:

$\map \phi {2^k} = 2^{k - 1}$
Thus:

$\map \phi 4 = \map \phi {2^2} = 2^1 = 2$
They can be enumerated as:

$1, 3$
$\blacksquare$
