Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $156$ and $1740$ is:

$\gcd \set {156, 1740} = 12$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 1740\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11 \times 156 + 24\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 156\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6 \times 24 + 12\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 24\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 12\)









Thus:

$\gcd \set {156, 1740} = 12$
$\blacksquare$
