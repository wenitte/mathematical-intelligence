Theorem
$\cos 240 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {4 \pi} 3 = -\dfrac 1 2$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 240 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {360 \degrees - 120 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 120 \degrees\)





Cosine of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\frac 1 2\)





Cosine of $120 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
