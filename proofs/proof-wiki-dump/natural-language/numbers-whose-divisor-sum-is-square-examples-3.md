Example of Number whose Divisor Sum is Square
$\map {\sigma_1} 3 = 4 = 2^2$


Proof













\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} 3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 + 1\)





$\sigma_1$ of $3$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^2\)









$\blacksquare$
