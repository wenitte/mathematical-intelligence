Example of Use of LCM from Prime Decomposition
The lowest common multiple of $39$ and $143$ is:

$\lcm \set {39, 143} = 429$


Proof













\(\ds 39\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 13\)




















\(\ds 143\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11 \times 13\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 39\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^1 \times 11^0 \times 13^1\)




















\(\ds 143\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^0 \times 11^1 \times 13^1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \lcm \set {39, 143}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^1 \times 11^1 \times 13^1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 429\)









$\blacksquare$
