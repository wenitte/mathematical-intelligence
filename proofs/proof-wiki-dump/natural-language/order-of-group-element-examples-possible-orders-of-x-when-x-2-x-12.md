Examples of Order of Group Element
Let $G$ be a group.
Let $x \in G \setminus \set e$ be such that $x^2 = x^{12}$.
Then the possible orders of $x$ are $2$, $5$ and $10$.


Proof













\(\ds x^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x^{12}\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x^2 \cdot x^{10}\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x^{10}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \order x\)

\(\divides\)







\(\ds 10\)









Hence:

$\order x \in \set {2, 5, 10}$
as required.
$\blacksquare$
