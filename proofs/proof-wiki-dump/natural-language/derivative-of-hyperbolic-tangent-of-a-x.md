Theorem
$\map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tanh a x} = a \sech^2 a x$


Proof













\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tanh x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sech^2 x\)





Derivative of $\tanh x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tanh a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a \sech^2 a x\)





Derivative of Function of Constant Multiple



$\blacksquare$
