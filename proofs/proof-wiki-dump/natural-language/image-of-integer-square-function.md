Theorem
The image of the integer square function is the set of square numbers.


Sequence of Square Numbers
The sequence of square numbers, for $n \in \Z_{\ge 0}$, begins:

$0, 1, 4, 9, 16, 25, 36, 49, 64, 81, 100, 121, \ldots$


Proof
Follows directly by definition of the integer square function.
$\blacksquare$
