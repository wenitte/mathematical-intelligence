Problem
How can an oval be drawn with one turn of a compass?


Solution
By wrapping the paper around a circular cylinder, drawing the circle, then unwrapping the paper.
$\blacksquare$


Historical Note
This puzzle is typical of those found in collections of Victorian amusements.
