Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $108$ and $243$ is:

$\gcd \set {108, 243} = 27$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 243\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 108 + 27\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 108\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 \times 27\)









Thus:

$\gcd \set {108, 243} = 27$
$\blacksquare$
