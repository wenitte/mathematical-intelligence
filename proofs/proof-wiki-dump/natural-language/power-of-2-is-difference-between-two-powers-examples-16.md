Example of Power of 2 is Difference between Two Powers
$2^4 = 5^2 - 3^2$


Proof













\(\ds 5^2 - 3^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 25 - 9\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 16\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^4\)









$\blacksquare$
