Theorem
$\cosh^2 x = \dfrac {\cosh 2 x + 1} 2$


Proof













\(\ds 2 \cosh^2 x - 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cosh 2 x\)





Double Angle Formula for Hyperbolic Cosine: Corollary $1$














\(\ds \cosh^2 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cosh 2 x + 1} 2\)





solving for $\cosh^2 x$



$\blacksquare$
