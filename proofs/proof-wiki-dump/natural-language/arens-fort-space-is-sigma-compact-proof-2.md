Theorem
Let $T = \left({S, \tau}\right)$ be the Arens-Fort space.

Then $T$ is a $\sigma$-compact space.


Proof
The result follows from Arens-Fort Space is Countable and Countable Space is Sigma-Compact.
$\blacksquare$
