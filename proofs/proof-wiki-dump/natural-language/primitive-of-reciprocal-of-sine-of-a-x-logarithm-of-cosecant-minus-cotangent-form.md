Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\sin a x} = \frac 1 a \ln \size {\csc a x - \cot a x} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\sin x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \csc x \rd x\)





Definition of Real Cosecant Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\csc x - \cot x} + C\)





Primitive of $\csc x$: Cosecant minus Cotangent Form








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\sin a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \ln \size {\csc a x - \cot a x} + C\)





Primitive of Function of Constant Multiple



$\blacksquare$
