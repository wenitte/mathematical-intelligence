Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $24$ and $138$ is found to be:

$\gcd \set {24, 138} = 6$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 138\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 \times 24 + 18\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 24\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 18 + 6\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 18\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 6\)










Thus:

$\gcd \set {24, 138} = 6$
$\blacksquare$
