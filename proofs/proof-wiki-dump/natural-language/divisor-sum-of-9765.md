Example of Divisor Sum of Integer
$\map {\sigma_1} {9765} = 19 \, 968$
where $\sigma_1$ denotes the divisor dum function.


Proof
We have that:

$9765 = 3^2 \times 5 \times 7 \times 31$

Hence:














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {9765}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {3^3 - 1} {3 - 1} \times \paren {5 + 1} \times \paren {7 + 1} \times \paren {31 + 1}\)





Divisor Sum of Integer














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {26} 2 \times 6 \times 8 \times 32\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 13 \times 6 \times 8 \times 32\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 13 \times \paren {2 \times 3} \times 2^3 \times 2^5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^9 \times 3 \times 13\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 19 \, 968\)









$\blacksquare$
