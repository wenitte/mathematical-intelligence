Theorem
$\ceiling {-\dfrac 1 2} = 0$
where $\ceiling x$ denotes the ceiling of $x$.


Proof
We have:

$0 \ge -\dfrac 1 2$
and:

$-1 = -\dfrac 2 2 < -\dfrac 1 2$
Hence $0$ is the ceiling of $-\dfrac 1 2$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$
