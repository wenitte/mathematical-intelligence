Theorem
For small values of $x$ measured in radians:

$\sin x \approx x$


Proof
From Limit of Sine of X over X at Zero:

$\ds \lim_{x \mathop \to 0} \frac {\sin x} x = 1$
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
