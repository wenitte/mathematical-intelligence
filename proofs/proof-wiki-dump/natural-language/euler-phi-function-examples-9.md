Example of Use of Euler $\phi$ Function
$\map \phi 9 = 6$
where $\phi$ denotes the Euler $\phi$ function.


Proof
From Euler Phi Function of Prime Power:

$\map \phi {3^k} = 2 \times 3^{k - 1}$
Thus:

$\map \phi 9 = \map \phi {3^2} = 2 \times 3 = 6$
They can be enumerated as:

$1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 8$
$\blacksquare$
