Problem
$2$ men each ascend a vertical ladder, at the same speed, and yet they get further apart.
Explain.


Solution
The men are at the center of the Earth, ascending in opposite directions.
$\blacksquare$


Historical Note
This puzzle is typical of those found in collections of Victorian amusements.
