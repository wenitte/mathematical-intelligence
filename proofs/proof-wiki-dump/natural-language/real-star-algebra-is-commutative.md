Theorem
Let $A = \struct {A_F, \oplus}$ be a real $*$-algebra whose conjugation is denoted as $*$.
Then:

$\forall a, b \in A, a \oplus b = b \oplus a$
That is, real $*$-algebra is commutative.


Proof













\(\ds a \oplus b\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {a \oplus b}^*\)





Definition of Real $*$-Algebra














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds b^* \oplus a^*\)





Definition of Conjugation on Algebra














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds b \oplus a\)





Definition of Real $*$-Algebra



$\blacksquare$
