Theorem
$\ln x \to +\infty$ as $x \to +\infty$


Proof
From the definition of the natural logarithm:














\(\ds \ln x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int_1^x \dfrac 1 t \rd t\)









The result follows from Integral of Reciprocal is Divergent.
$\blacksquare$
