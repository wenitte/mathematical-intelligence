Theorem
$\csc \left({x + \dfrac \pi 2}\right) = \sec x$


Proof













\(\ds \csc \left({x + \frac \pi 2}\right)\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\sin \left({x + \frac \pi 2}\right)}\)





Cosecant is Reciprocal of Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\cos x}\)





Sine of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec x\)





Secant is Reciprocal of Cosine



$\blacksquare$
