Theorem
$\map \csc {x + \dfrac {3 \pi} 2} = -\sec x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \csc {x + \frac {3 \pi} 2}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\map \sin {x + \frac {3 \pi} 2} }\)





Cosecant is Reciprocal of Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {-\cos x}\)





Sine of Angle plus Three Right Angles














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sec x\)





Secant is Reciprocal of Cosine



$\blacksquare$
