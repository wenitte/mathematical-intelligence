Theorem
Let $\struct {F, +, \circ}$ be a field.
Then $\struct {F, +, \circ}$ is a subfield of $\struct {F, +, \circ}$.


Proof
$F$ is a field and $F \subseteq F$ from Set is Subset of Itself.
$\blacksquare$
