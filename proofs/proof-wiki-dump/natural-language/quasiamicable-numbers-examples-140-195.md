Examples of Quasiamicable Pair
$140$ and $195$ form a quasiamicable pair.


Proof













\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {140}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 336\)





$\sigma_1$ of $140$














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {195}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 336\)





$\sigma_1$ of $195$














\(\ds 140 + 195 + 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 336\)









Hence the result by definition of quasiamicable pair.
$\blacksquare$
