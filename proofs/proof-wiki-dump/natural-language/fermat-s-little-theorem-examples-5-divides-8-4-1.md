Examples of Use of Fermat's Little Theorem
$5 \divides 8^4 - 1$
where $\divides$ denotes divisibility.

Proof













\(\ds 8^4\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4096\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 8^4 - 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4095\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 \times 819\)









$\blacksquare$
