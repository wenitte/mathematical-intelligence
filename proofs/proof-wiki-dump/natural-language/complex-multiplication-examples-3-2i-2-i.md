Example of Complex Multiplication
$\paren {3 + 2 i} \paren {2 - i} = 8 + i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {3 + 2 i} \paren {2 - i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 \times 2 - 2 \times \paren {-1} } + \paren {3 \times \paren {-1} + 2 \times 2} i\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {6 - \paren {-2} } + \paren {-3 + 4} i\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 8 + i\)









$\blacksquare$
