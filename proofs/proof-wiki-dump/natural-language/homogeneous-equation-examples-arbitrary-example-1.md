Example of Homogeneous Equation
The equation:

$x^2 + y^2 = 0$
is a homogeneous equation.

This is demonstrated by showing that $x^2 + y^2$ is a homogeneous expression:














\(\ds \map E {x, y}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x^2 + y^2\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \map E {k x, k y}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds k^2 x^2 + k^2 y^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds k^2 \paren {x^2 + y^2}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds k^2 \map E {x, y}\)









$\blacksquare$
