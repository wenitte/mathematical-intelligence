Example of Algebraic Number
$3 - i$ is an algebraic number.


Proof













\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 - i\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x - 3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -i\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \paren {x - 3}^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-i}^2\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x^2 - 6 x + 9\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x^2 - 6 x + 10\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)









Hence the result by definition of algebraic number.
$\blacksquare$
