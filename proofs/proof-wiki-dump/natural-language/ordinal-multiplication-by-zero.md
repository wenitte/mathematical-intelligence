Theorem
Let $x$ be an ordinal.














\(\ds x \cdot \O\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)




















\(\ds \O \cdot x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)











Proof













\(\ds x \cdot \O\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)





Definition of Ordinal Multiplication



For $\O \cdot x = \O$, the proof shall proceed by Transfinite Induction on $x$.


Basis for the Induction













\(\ds \O \cdot \O\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)





Definition of Ordinal Multiplication



This proves the basis for the induction.


Induction Step













\(\ds \O \cdot x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)





Inductive Hypothesis








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \paren {\O \cdot x} + \O\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)





Definition of Ordinal Addition














\(\ds \O \cdot x^+\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\O \cdot x} + \O\)





Definition of Ordinal Multiplication








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \O \cdot x^+\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)





Equality is Transitive



This proves the induction step.


Limit Case









\(\ds \forall y \in x: \, \)



\(\ds \O \cdot y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)





Hypothesis








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \bigcup_{y \mathop \in x} \paren {\O \cdot y}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)





Indexed Union Equality














\(\ds \O \cdot x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \bigcup_{y \mathop \in x} \paren {\O \cdot y}\)





Definition of Ordinal Multiplication








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \O \cdot x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \O\)





Equality is Transitive



This proves the limit case.
$\blacksquare$
