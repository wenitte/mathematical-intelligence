Theorem
$\cot \left({x + 2 \pi}\right) = \cot x$


Proof













\(\ds \cot \left({x + 2 \pi}\right)\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cos \left({x + 2 \pi}\right)} {\sin \left({x + 2 \pi}\right)}\)





Cotangent is Cosine divided by Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cos x} {\sin x}\)





Cosine of Angle plus Full Angle and Sine of Angle plus Full Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot x\)





Cotangent is Cosine divided by Sine



$\blacksquare$
