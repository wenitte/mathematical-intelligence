Theorem
Let $S$ and $T$ be finite sets.
Let $\card S > \card T$.

Then:

$S \setminus T \ne \O$


Proof
From Cardinality of Subset of Finite Set:

$S \nsubseteq T$
From the contrapositive statement of Set Difference with Superset is Empty Set:

$S \setminus T \ne \O$.
$\blacksquare$
