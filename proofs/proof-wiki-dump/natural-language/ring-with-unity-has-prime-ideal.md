Theorem
Let $A$ be a non-trivial commutative ring with unity.

Then $A$ has a prime ideal.


Proof
By Krull's Theorem, $A$ has a maximal ideal.
By Maximal Ideal of Commutative and Unitary Ring is Prime Ideal, $A$ has a prime ideal.
$\blacksquare$
