Example of Difference between Two Squares equal to Repunit
We have that $11$ is a prime.














\(\ds 11\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 11\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {11 + 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6\)




















\(\ds \frac {11 - 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 6^2 - 5^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 36 - 25\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11\)









$\blacksquare$
