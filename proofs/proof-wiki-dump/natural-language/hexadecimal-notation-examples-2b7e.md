Example of Hexadecimal Notation
The integer expressed in hexadecimal as $2 \mathrm B 7 \mathrm E$ is expressed in decimal as $11 \, 134$.


Proof













\(\ds 2 \mathrm B 7 \mathrm E\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 16^3 + 11 \times 16^2 + 7 \times 16 + 14\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 8192 + 2816 + 112 + 14\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11 \, 134\)









$\blacksquare$
