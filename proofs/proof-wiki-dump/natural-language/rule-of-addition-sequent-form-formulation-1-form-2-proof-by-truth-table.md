Theorem













\(\ds q\)

\(\)







\(\ds \)














\(\ds \vdash \ \ \)





\(\ds p \lor q\)

\(\)







\(\ds \)











Proof
We apply the Method of Truth Tables.
$\begin{array}{|c||ccc|} \hline
q & p & \lor & q \\
\hline
\F & \F & \F & \F \\
\T & \F & \T & \T \\
\F & \T & \T & \F \\
\T & \T & \T & \T \\
\hline
\end{array}$
As can be seen, when $q$ is true so is $p \lor q$.
$\blacksquare$
