Example of Giuga Number
$858$ is a Giuga number:

$\dfrac 1 2 + \dfrac 1 3 + \dfrac 1 {11} + \dfrac 1 {13} - \dfrac 1 {858} = 1$


Proof
We have that:

$858 = 2 \times 3 \times 11 \times 13$
Then:














\(\ds \dfrac 1 2 + \dfrac 1 3 + \dfrac 1 {11} + \dfrac 1 {13}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {3 \times 11 \times 13 + 2 \times 11 \times 13 + 2 \times 3 \times 13 + 2 \times 3 \times 11} {2 \times 3 \times 11 \times 13}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {429 + 286 + 78 + 66} {858}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {859} {858}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + \frac 1 {858}\)









Hence the result by definition of Giuga number.
$\blacksquare$
