Corollary to Derivative of Tangent Function
$\dfrac \d {\d x} \tan x = 1 + \tan^2 x$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac \d {\d x} \tan x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec^2 x\)





Derivative of $\tan x$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + \tan^2 x\)





Difference of Squares of Secant and Tangent



$\blacksquare$
