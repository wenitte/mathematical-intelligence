Example of Use of Ramanujan's Arctangent Sum













\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 1}^\infty \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 {2 k^2} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 2 } + \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 8 } + \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 {18} } + \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 {32} } + \cdots\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac \pi 4\)











Proof













\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 1}^\infty \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 {2 \paren {n + k}^2} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 {2 n + 1} }\)





Ramanujan's Arctangent Sum/Examples/Sum over k from 1 to Infinity of Arctan of Reciprocal of Twice the Square of N Plus K

\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 1}^\infty \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 {2 k^2} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \arctan {1 }\)





setting $n$ to $0$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 1}^\infty \map \arctan {\dfrac 1 {2 k^2} }\)
