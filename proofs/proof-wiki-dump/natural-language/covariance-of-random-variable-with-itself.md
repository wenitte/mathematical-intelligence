Theorem
Let $X$ be a random variable.
Then $\cov {X, X} = \var X$.


Proof
We have: 














\(\ds \cov {X, X}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \expect {\paren {X - \expect X} \paren {X - \expect X} }\)





Definition of Covariance














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \expect {\paren {X - \expect X}^2}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \var X\)





Definition of Variance



$\blacksquare$
