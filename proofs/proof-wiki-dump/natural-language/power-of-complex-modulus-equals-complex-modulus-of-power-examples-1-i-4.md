Example of Power of Complex Modulus equals Complex Modulus of Power
$\left\vert{\left({1 + i}\right)^4}\right\rvert = 4$


Proof













\(\ds \left\vert{1 + i}\right\rvert\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 2\)





Examples of Complex Modulus: $1 + i$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \left\lvert{\left({1 + i}\right)^4}\right\rvert\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \left({\sqrt 2}\right)^4\)





Power of Complex Modulus equals Complex Modulus of Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4\)









$\blacksquare$
