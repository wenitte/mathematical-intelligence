Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $99$ is:

$\dfrac 1 {99} = 0 \cdotp \dot 0 \dot 1$
This sequence is A000035 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).


Proof
Performing the calculation using long division:

   0.0101...
  ----------
99)1.0000000
     99
     --
      100
       99
      ---
      ...

$\blacksquare$
