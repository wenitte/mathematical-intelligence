Theorem
A relation $\RR$ is symmetric if and only if its inverse $\RR^{-1}$ is also symmetric.


Proof
Let $\RR$ be symmetric.
Then from Relation equals Inverse iff Symmetric:

$\RR = \RR^{-1}$
The result follows.
$\blacksquare$
