Theorem
$\cot 120 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {2 \pi} 3 = -\dfrac {\sqrt 3} 3$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 120 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cot {90 \degrees + 30 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\tan 30 \degrees\)





Cotangent of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\frac {\sqrt 3} 3\)





Tangent of 30 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
