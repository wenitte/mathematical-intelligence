Example of Derivative of Function of Constant Multiple
$\map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\sin 2 x} = 2 \cos 2 x$


Proof













\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\sin 2 x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \map {\dfrac \d {\map \d {2 x} } } {\sin 2 x}\)





Derivative of Function of Constant Multiple














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \cos 2 x\)





Derivative of Sine Function



$\blacksquare$
