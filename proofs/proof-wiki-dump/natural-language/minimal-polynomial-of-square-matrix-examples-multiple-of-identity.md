Example of Minimal Polynomial of Square Matrix
Let $\mathbf A$ be a square matrix of order $n$ such that:

$\mathbf A = k \mathbf I_n$
where $\mathbf I_n$ denotes the identity matrix of order $n$

Then the minimal polynomial of $\mathbf A$ is:

$\mathbf x - k \mathbf I_n$


Proof
We have by hypothesis that:
$\mathbf A = k \mathbf I_n$
from which:

$\mathbf x - k \mathbf I_n = \mathbf 0$
Hence the result by definition of minimal polynomial.
$\blacksquare$
