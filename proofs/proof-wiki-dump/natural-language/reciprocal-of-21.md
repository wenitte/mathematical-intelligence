Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $21$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {21} = 0 \cdotp \dot 04761 \, \dot 9$
This sequence is A021025 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
