Theorem
$\ds \int_1^n \frac {\d x} x \to +\infty$ as $n \to + \infty$


Proof
From the definition of natural logarithm:

$\ds \ln x = \int_1^x \dfrac 1 t \rd t$
The result follows from Logarithm Tends to Infinity.
$\blacksquare$
