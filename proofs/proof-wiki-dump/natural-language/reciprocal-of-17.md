Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $17$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {17} = 0 \cdotp \dot 05882 \, 35294 \, 11764 \, \dot 7$
This sequence is A007450 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
