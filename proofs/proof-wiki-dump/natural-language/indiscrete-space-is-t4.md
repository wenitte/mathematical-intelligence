Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \set {\O, S} }$ be an indiscrete topological space.

Then $T$ is a $T_4$ space.


Proof
We have that an indiscrete space is a $T_5$ space.
Then we have that a $T_5$ Space is a $T_4$ Space.
$\blacksquare$
