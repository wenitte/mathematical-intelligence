Theorem
$\ds \int \csch a x \rd x = \frac 1 a \ln \size {\tanh \frac {a x} 2} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \csch x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\tanh \frac x 2} + C\)





Primitive of $\csch x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \csch a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \ln \size {\tanh \frac {a x} 2} + C\)





Primitive of Function of Constant Multiple



$\blacksquare$
