Example of Hexadecimal Notation
The integer expressed in hexadecimal as $6 \mathrm C 5$ is expressed in decimal as $1733$.


Proof













\(\ds 6 \mathrm C 5_{1 6}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6 \times 16^2 + 12 \times 16 + 5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1536 + 192 + 5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1733\)









$\blacksquare$
