Theorem
Let $F$ be an algebraically closed field.

Then $F$ is perfect.


Proof
Let $E / F$ be any algebraic extension.
Since $F$ is an algebraically closed field, $E = F$.
By Field is Separable over itself, $E$ is separable over $F$.
Hence $F$ is perfect.
$\blacksquare$
