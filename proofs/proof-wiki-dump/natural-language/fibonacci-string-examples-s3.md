Example of Fibonacci Strings
The Fibonacci string $S_3$ is $\text{ba}$.


Proof
By definition of Fibonacci string:














\(\ds S_1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \text{a}\)




















\(\ds S_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \text{b}\)









Then:














\(\ds S_3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds S_2 S_1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \text{ba}\)









$\blacksquare$
