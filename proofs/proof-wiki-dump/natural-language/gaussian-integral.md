Theorem
$\ds \int_{-\infty}^\infty e^{-x^2} \rd x = \sqrt \pi$


Proof













\(\ds \int_{-\infty}^\infty e^{-x^2} \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} \rd x\)





Definite Integral of Even Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2 \sqrt \pi} 2\)





Integral to Infinity of Exponential of -t^2














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt \pi\)









$\blacksquare$
