Theorem
$\sec 90 \degrees = \sec \dfrac \pi 2$ is undefined
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof
From Secant is Reciprocal of Cosine:

$\sec \theta = \dfrac 1 {\cos \theta}$
From Cosine of Right Angle:

$\cos \dfrac \pi 2 = 0$
Thus $\sec \theta$ is undefined at this value.
$\blacksquare$
