Theorem
$\floor {1 \cdotp 1} = 1$
where $\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof
We have that:

$1 \le 1 \cdotp 1 < 2$
Hence $1$ is the floor of $1 \cdotp 1$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$


Also see
Floor of $-1\cdotp 1$: $\floor {-1 \cdotp 1} = -2$
Ceiling of $-1\cdotp 1$: $\ceiling {-1 \cdotp 1} = -1$
