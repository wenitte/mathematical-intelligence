Example of Complex Subtraction
$\paren {6 - 2 i} - \paren {2 - 5 i} = 4 + 3 i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {6 - 2 i} - \paren {2 - 5 i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {6 - 2} + \paren {-2 - \paren {-5} } i\)





Definition of Complex Subtraction














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 + 3 i\)









$\blacksquare$
