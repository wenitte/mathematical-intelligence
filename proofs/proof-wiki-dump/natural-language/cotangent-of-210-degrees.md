Theorem
$\cot 210^\circ = \cot \dfrac {7 \pi} 6 = \sqrt 3$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 210^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot \left({360^\circ - 150^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 150^\circ\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 3\)





Cotangent of 150 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
