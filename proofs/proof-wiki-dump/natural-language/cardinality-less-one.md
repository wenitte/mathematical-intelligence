Theorem
Let $S$ be a finite set.
Let:

$\card S = n + 1$
where $\card S$ is the cardinality of $S$.
Let $a \in S$.

Then:

$\card {S \setminus \set a} = n$
where $\setminus$ denotes set difference.


Proof
This follows as an immediate consequence of Set Equivalence Less One Element.
$\blacksquare$
