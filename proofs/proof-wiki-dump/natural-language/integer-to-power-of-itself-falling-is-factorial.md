Theorem
Let $n \in \Z_{\ge 0}$ be a positive integer.

$n^{\underline n} = n!$
where:

$n^{\underline n}$ denotes the falling factorial
$n!$ denotes the factorial.


Proof













\(\ds n^{\underline n}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {n!} {\paren {n - n}!}\)





Falling Factorial as Quotient of Factorials














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {n!} {0!}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds n!\)





Factorial of Zero



$\blacksquare$
