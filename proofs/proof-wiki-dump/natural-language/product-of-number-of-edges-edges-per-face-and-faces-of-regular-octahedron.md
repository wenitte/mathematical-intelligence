Theorem
The product of the number of edges, edges per face and faces of a regular octahedron is $288$.


Proof
A regular octahedron has:

$12$ edges
and:

$8$ faces.
Each face is a triangle, and so has $3$ edges.
Hence:

$12 \times 8 \times 3 = 288$
$\blacksquare$
