Examples of Divisors Modulo $m$
In modulo $12$ division, $5$ has the following divisors:

$1, 5, 7, 11$


Proof
We have:














\(\ds 5 \times 1\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 5\)

\(\ds \pmod {12}\)


















\(\ds 7 \times 11\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 5\)

\(\ds \pmod {12}\)







$\blacksquare$
