Theorem
Let $T = \struct {\R, \tau}$ be the compact complement topology on $\R$.

Then $T$ is a separable space.


Proof
We have:

Compact Complement Topology is Second-Countable
Second-Countable Space is Separable
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
