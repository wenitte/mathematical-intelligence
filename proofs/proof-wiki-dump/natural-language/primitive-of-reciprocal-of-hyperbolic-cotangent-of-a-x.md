Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\coth a x} = \frac {\ln \size {\cosh a x} } a + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\coth a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \tanh a x \rd x\)





Hyperbolic Cotangent is Reciprocal of Hyperbolic Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\ln \size {\cosh a x} } a + C\)





Primitive of $\tanh a x$



$\blacksquare$
