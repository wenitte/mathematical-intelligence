Theorem
$\sec 360 \degrees = \sec 2 \pi = 1$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 360 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 0 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 0 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)





Secant of Zero



$\blacksquare$
