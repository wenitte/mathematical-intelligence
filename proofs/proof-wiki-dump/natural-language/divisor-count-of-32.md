Example of Use of Divisor Count Function
$\map {\sigma_0} {32} = 6$
where $\sigma_0$ denotes the divisor count function.


Proof













\(\ds \map {\sigma_0} {32}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map {\sigma_0} {2^5}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 + 1\)





Divisor Count Function of Power of Prime














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6\)










The divisors of $32$ can be enumerated as:

$1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32$
$\blacksquare$
