Example of Complex Addition
$\paren {5 + 3 i} + \paren {\paren {-1 + 2 i} + \paren {7 - 5 i} } = 11$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {5 + 3 i} + \paren {\paren {-1 + 2 i} + \paren {7 - 5 i} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {5 + 3 i} + \paren {\paren {-1 + 7} + \paren {2 - 5} i}\)





Definition of Complex Addition














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {5 + 3 i} + \paren {6 - 3 i}\)





simplification














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {5 + 6} + \paren {3 i - 3 i}\)





Definition of Complex Addition














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11\)









$\blacksquare$
