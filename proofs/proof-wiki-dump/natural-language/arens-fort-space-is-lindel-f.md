Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau}$ be the Arens-Fort space.

Then $T$ is a Lindelöf space.


Proof
We have:

The Arens-Fort Space is Sigma-Compact.
A $\sigma$-compact space is Lindelöf.
$\blacksquare$
