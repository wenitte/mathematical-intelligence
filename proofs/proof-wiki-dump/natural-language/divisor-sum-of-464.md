Example of Divisor Sum of Integer
$\map {\sigma_1} {464} = 930$
where $\sigma_1$ denotes the divisor sum function.


Proof
We have that:

$464 = 2^4 \times 29$
Hence:














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {464}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2^5 - 1} {2 - 1} \times \paren {29 + 1}\)





Divisor Sum of Integer














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {31 - 1} 1 \times 30\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 31 \times 30\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2 \times 3 \times 5} \times 31\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 930\)









$\blacksquare$
