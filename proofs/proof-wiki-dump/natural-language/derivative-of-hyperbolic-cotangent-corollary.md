Theorem
$\map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\coth x} = 1 - \coth^2 x$
where $\coth x$ denotes the hyperbolic cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac \d {\d x} \coth x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csch^2 x\)





Derivative of Hyperbolic Cotangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 - \coth^2 x\)





Difference of Squares of Hyperbolic Cotangent and Cosecant



$\blacksquare$
