Primitive of $x^2 \sqrt {x^2 - a^2}$: Also presented as
This result is also seen presented in the form:

$\ds \int x^2 \sqrt {x^2 - a^2} \rd x = \frac x 8 \paren {2 x^2 - a^2} \sqrt {x^2 - a^2} - \frac {a^4} 8 \arcosh \dfrac x a + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int x^2 \sqrt {x^2 - a^2} \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {x \paren {\sqrt {x^2 - a^2} }^3} 4 + \frac {a^2 x \sqrt {x^2 - a^2} } 8 - \frac {a^4} 8 \ln \size {x + \sqrt {x^2 - a^2} } + C\)





Primitive of $x^2 \sqrt {x^2 - a^2}$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {x \paren {\sqrt {x^2 - a^2} }^3} 4 + \frac {a^2 x \sqrt {x^2 - a^2} } 8 - \frac {a^4} 8 \arcosh \dfrac x a + C\)





Definition of Inverse Hyperbolic Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2 x \paren {\sqrt {x^2 - a^2} }^3 + a^2 x \sqrt {x^2 - a^2} } 8 - \frac {a^4} 8 \arcosh \dfrac x a + C\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {x \sqrt {x^2 - a^2} \paren {2 \paren {x^2 - a^2} + a^2} } 8 - \frac {a^4} 8 \arcosh \dfrac x a + C\)





Distributive Laws of Arithmetic














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac x 8 \paren {2 x^2 - a^2} \sqrt {x^2 - a^2} - \frac {a^4} 8 \arcosh \dfrac x a + C\)





simplifying



$\blacksquare$
