Theorem
Let $\struct {S, \preceq}$ be a meet semilattice.
Let $H$ be a non-empty upper section of $S$.
Then $H$ is filtered if and only if

$\forall x, y \in H: x \wedge y \in H$


Proof
This follows by mutatis mutandis of the proof of Directed in Join Semilattice.
$\blacksquare$
