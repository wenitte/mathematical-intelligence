Theorem
$\sec 300 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {5 \pi} 3 = 2$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 300 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 60 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 60 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2\)





Secant of $60 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
