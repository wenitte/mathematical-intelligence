Theorem
Let $\struct {S, \preceq}$ be an ordered set.
Let $s$ be an element of $S$.

Then:

$\set s^\preceq = s^\preceq$
where:

$\set s^\preceq$ denotes the lower closure of $\set s$
$s^\preceq$ denotes the lower closure of $s$.


Proof













\(\ds \set s^\preceq\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \bigcup \set {t^\preceq: t \in \set s}\)





Definition of Lower Closure of Subset














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \bigcup \set {s^\preceq}\)





Definition of Singleton














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds s^\preceq\)





Union of Singleton



$\blacksquare$
