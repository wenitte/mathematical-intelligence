Theorem
$\ds \pi \map \coth {\pi z} = \frac 1 z + 2 \sum_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac z {z^2 + n^2}$
where: 

$z \in \C$ is not an integer multiple of $i$
$\coth$ is the hyperbolic cotangent function.


Proof













\(\ds \pi \map \coth {\pi z}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \pi i \map \cot {\pi i z}\)





Hyperbolic Cotangent in terms of Cotangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds i \paren {\frac 1 {i z} + 2 i \sum_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac z {\paren {i z}^2 - n^2} }\)





Mittag-Leffler Expansion for Cotangent Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 z - 2 \sum_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac z {-z^2 - n^2}\)





$i^2 = -1$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 z + 2 \sum_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac z {z^2 + n^2}\)









$\blacksquare$
