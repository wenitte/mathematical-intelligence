Example of Empty Set
The set:

$S = \set {x \in \R: x^2 + 1 = 0}$
is an instance of a specification of the empty set.


Proof
From Existence of Real Polynomial with no Real Root, there exists no $x \in \R$ such that $x^2 + 1 = 0$.
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
