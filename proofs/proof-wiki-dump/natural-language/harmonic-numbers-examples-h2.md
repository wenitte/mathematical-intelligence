Example of Harmonic Number
$H_2 = \dfrac 3 2$
where $H_2$ denotes the second harmonic number.


Proof













\(\ds H_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sum_{k \mathop = 1}^2 \frac 1 k\)





Definition of Harmonic Number














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 1 + \frac 1 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 3 2\)









$\blacksquare$
