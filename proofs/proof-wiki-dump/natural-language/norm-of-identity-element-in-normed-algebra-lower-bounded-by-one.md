Theorem
Let $\struct {R, \norm {\, \cdot \,}_R}$ be a normed division ring.
Let $\struct {A, \norm {\, \cdot \,} }$ be a normed algebra over $R$ with identity element ${\mathbf 1}_A \ne {\mathbf 0}_A$. 

Then $1 \le \norm { {\mathbf 1}_A}$. 


Proof
By the definition of a algebra norm, we have:

$\norm { {\mathbf 1}_A} = \norm { {\mathbf 1}_A^2} \le \norm { {\mathbf 1}_A}^2$
From Norm Axiom $\text N 1$: Positive Definiteness, we have $\norm { {\mathbf 1}_A} \ne 0$, we have:

$1 \le \norm { {\mathbf 1}_A}$
$\blacksquare$
