Theorem
Let $\tuple {0, 1}$ denote the imaginary unit.
Then:

$\tuple {0, 1} \times \tuple {0, 1} = \tuple {-1, 0}$
where $\times$ denotes complex multiplication.


Proof













\(\ds \tuple {0, 1} \times \tuple {0, 1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tuple {0 \times 0 - 1 \times 1, 0 \times 1 + 0 \times 1}\)





Definition of Complex Multiplication














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tuple {-1, 0}\)









$\blacksquare$
