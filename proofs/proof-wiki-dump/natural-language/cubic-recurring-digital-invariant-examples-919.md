Example of Cubic Recurring Digital Invariant
$919$ is a cubic recurring digital invariant:








\(\ds 919: \ \ \)





\(\ds 9^3 + 1^3 + 9^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 729 + 1 + 729\)

\(\ds = 1459\)












\(\ds 1459: \ \ \)





\(\ds 1^3 + 4^3 + 5^3 + 9^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + 64 + 125 + 729\)

\(\ds = 919\)







$\blacksquare$
