Theorem
$x - 1 < \floor x \le x$
where $\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof
By definition of floor function:

$\floor x \le x < \floor x + 1$
Thus by subtracting $1$:

$x - 1 < \paren {\floor x + 1} - 1 = \floor x$
So:

$\floor x \le x$
and:

$x - 1 < \floor x$
as required.
$\blacksquare$
