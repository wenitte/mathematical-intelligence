Theorem
Let $x \in \R$.
Let $0 < x < 1$.

Then:

$0 < x^2 < x$


Proof
We have that Real Numbers form Ordered Integral Domain.
Thus Square of Element Less than Unity in Ordered Integral Domain applies directly.
$\blacksquare$
