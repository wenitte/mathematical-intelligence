Theorem
Let $S$ be a set.
Let $\powerset S$ denote the power set of $S$.

There is no bijection $f: S \to \powerset S$.


Proof
A bijection is by its definition also a surjection.
By Cantor's Theorem there is no surjection from $S$ to $\powerset S$.
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
