Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau_{a, b} }$ be a modified Fort space.

Then $T$ is not zero dimensional.


Proof
We have Modified Fort Space is not $T_3$.
We also have Zero Dimensional Space is $T_3$.
The result follows by Modus Tollendo Tollens.
$\blacksquare$
