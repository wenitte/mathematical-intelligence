Theorem
$\map \cosh {-x} = \cosh x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \cosh {-x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {-i x}\)





Hyperbolic Cosine in terms of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {i x}\)





Cosine Function is Even














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cosh x\)





Hyperbolic Cosine in terms of Cosine



$\blacksquare$
