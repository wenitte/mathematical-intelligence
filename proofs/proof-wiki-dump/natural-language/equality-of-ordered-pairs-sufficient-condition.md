Theorem
Let $\tuple {a, b}$ and $\tuple {c, d}$ be ordered pairs.
Let $a = c$ and $b = d$.

Then:

$\tuple {a, b} = \tuple {c, d}$


Proof
Suppose $a = c$ and $b = d$.
Then:

$\set a = \set c$
and:

$\set {a, b} = \set {c, d}$
Thus:

$\set {\set a, \set {a, b} } = \set {\set c, \set {c, d} }$
and so by the Kuratowski formalization:

$\tuple {a, b} = \tuple {c, d}$
$\blacksquare$
