Theorem
$\csc 105 \degrees = \csc \dfrac {7 \pi} {12} = \sqrt 6 - \sqrt 2$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 105 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \csc {180 \degrees - 75 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \csc 75 \degrees\)





Cosecant of Supplementary Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 6 - \sqrt 2\)





Cosecant of $75 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
