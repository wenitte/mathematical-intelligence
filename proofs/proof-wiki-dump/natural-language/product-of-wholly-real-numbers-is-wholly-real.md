Theorem
Let $x = \tuple {a, 0}$ and $y = \tuple {b, 0}$ be wholly real complex numbers.
Then $x y$ is also wholly real.


Proof
We have:














\(\ds x y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tuple {a, 0} \tuple {b, 0}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tuple {a \times b - 0 \times 0, a \times 0 + 0 \times b}\)





Definition of Complex Multiplication














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tuple {a \times b, 0}\)









$\blacksquare$
