Examples of Use of Laplace Transform of Constant Multiple
Let $\laptrans f$ denote the Laplace transform of the real function $f$.


$\laptrans {\sin 3 t} = \dfrac 3 {s^2 + 9}$


Proof













\(\ds \laptrans {\sin 3 t}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 3 \dfrac 1 {\paren {s / 3}^2 + 1}\)





Laplace Transform of Constant Multiple, Laplace Transform of Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 3 {s^2 + 9}\)





simplification



$\blacksquare$
