Examples of Operations on Product Elements
Solve for $x$ in:

$b x a^{-1} = a^{-1} b$


Solution













\(\ds b x a^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^{-1} b\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds b x a^{-1} a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^{-1} b a\)





Product of both sides with $a$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds b^{-1} b x a^{-1} a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds b^{-1} a^{-1} b a\)





Product of both sides with $b^{-1}$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds b^{-1} a^{-1} b a\)





Group Axiom $\text G 3$: Existence of Inverse Element



$\blacksquare$
