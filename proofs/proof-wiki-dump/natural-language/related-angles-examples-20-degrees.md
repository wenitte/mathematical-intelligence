Examples of Related Angles
The angles $20 \degrees$, $160 \degrees$, $200 \degrees$ and $340 \degrees$ are related angles.


Proof
We note that:














\(\ds 160 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 180 \degrees - 20 \degrees\)




















\(\ds 200 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 180 \degrees + 20 \degrees\)




















\(\ds 340 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 360 \degrees - 20 \degrees\)










It follows from:

Sine of Supplementary Angle that $\sin 160 \degrees = \sin 20 \degrees$
Cosine of Supplementary Angle that $\cos 160 \degrees = -\cos 20 \degrees$
Tangent of Supplementary Angle that $\tan 160 \degrees = -\tan 20 \degrees$
Sine of Angle plus Straight Angle that $\sin 200 \degrees = -\sin 20 \degrees$
Cosine of Angle plus Straight Angle that $\cos 200 \degrees = -\cos 20 \degrees$
Tangent of Angle plus Straight Angle that $\tan 200 \degrees = \tan 20 \degrees$
Sine of Explementary Angle that $\sin 340 \degrees = -\sin 20 \degrees$
Cosine of Explementary Angle that $\cos 340 \degrees = \cos 20 \degrees$
Tangent of Explementary Angle that $\tan 340 \degrees = -\tan 20 \degrees$
$\blacksquare$
