Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $56$ and $72$ is found to be:

$\gcd \set {56, 72} = 8$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 72\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 56 + 16\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 56\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 16 + 8\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 16\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 8\)










Thus:

$\gcd \set {56, 72} = 8$
$\blacksquare$
