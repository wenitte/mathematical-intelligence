Theorem
$\cos 270 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {3 \pi} 2 = 0$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 270 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {360 \degrees - 90 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 90 \degrees\)





Cosine of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





Cosine of Right Angle



$\blacksquare$
