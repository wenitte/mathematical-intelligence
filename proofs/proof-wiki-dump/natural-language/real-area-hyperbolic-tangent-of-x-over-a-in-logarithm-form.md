Theorem
$\artanh \dfrac x a = \dfrac 1 2 \map \ln {\dfrac {a + x} {a - x} }$


Proof













\(\ds \artanh  \frac x a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 2 \map \ln {\frac {1 + \frac x a} {1 - \frac x a} }\)





Definition of Real Area Hyperbolic Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 2 \map \ln {\frac {a + x} {a - x} }\)





multiplying top and bottom by $a$



$\blacksquare$
