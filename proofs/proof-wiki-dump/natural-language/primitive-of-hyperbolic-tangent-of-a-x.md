Theorem
$\ds \int \tanh a x \rd x = \frac {\map \ln {\cosh a x} } a + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \tanh x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \ln {\cosh x} + C\)





Primitive of $\tanh x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \tanh a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \paren {\map \ln {\cosh a x} } + C\)





Primitive of Function of Constant Multiple














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\map \ln {\cosh a x} } a + C\)





simplifying



$\blacksquare$
