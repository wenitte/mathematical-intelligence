Theorem
$\cos 285^\circ = \cos \dfrac {19 \pi} {12} = \dfrac {\sqrt 6 - \sqrt 2} 4$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 285^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos \left({360^\circ - 75^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 75^\circ\)





Cosine of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sqrt 6 - \sqrt 2} 4\)





Cosine of 75 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
