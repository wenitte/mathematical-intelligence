Example of Complex Addition
$\paren {-7 - i} + \paren {3 + 2 i} = -4 + i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {-7 - i} + \paren {3 + 2 i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren {-7} + 3} + \paren {\paren {-1} + 2} i\)





Definition of Complex Addition














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -4 + i\)









$\blacksquare$
