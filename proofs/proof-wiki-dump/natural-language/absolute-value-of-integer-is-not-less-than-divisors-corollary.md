Corollary to Absolute Value of Integer is not less than Divisors
Let $a, b \in \Z_{>0}$ be (strictly) positive integers.
Let $a \divides b$.

Then:

$a \le b$


Proof
Follows directly from Absolute Value of Integer is not less than Divisors.
$\blacksquare$
