Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $3$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 3 = 0 \cdotp \dot 3$
This sequence is A010701 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
