Example of Use of Fourth Power is Sum of 2 Triangular Numbers
$7^4 = T_{41} + T_{55}$


Proof
We have:

$41 = 7^2 - 7 - 1$
and:

$55 = 7^2 + 7 - 1$
Hence from Fourth Power is Sum of 2 Triangular Numbers:

$7^4 = T_{41} + T_{55}$
$\blacksquare$
