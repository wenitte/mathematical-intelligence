Theorem
$\map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tanh x} = \sech^2 x = \dfrac 1 {\cosh^2 x}$
where $\tanh$ is the hyperbolic tangent, $\sech$ is the hyperbolic secant and $\cosh$ is the hyperbolic cosine.


Corollary
$\map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tanh x} = 1 - \tanh^2 x$


Proof













\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tanh x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\dfrac {\sinh x} {\cosh x} }\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\paren {\dfrac \d {\d x}  \sinh x} \cosh x - \sinh x \paren {\dfrac \d {\d x}  \cosh x} } {\cosh^2 x}\)





Quotient Rule for Derivatives














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\cosh^2 x - \sinh x \paren {\dfrac \d {\d x} \cosh x} } {\cosh^2 x}\)





Derivative of Hyperbolic Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\cosh^2 x - \sinh^2 x} {\cosh^2 x}\)





Derivative of Hyperbolic Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 {\cosh^2 x}\)





Difference of Squares of Hyperbolic Cosine and Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sech^2 x\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Secant



$\blacksquare$
