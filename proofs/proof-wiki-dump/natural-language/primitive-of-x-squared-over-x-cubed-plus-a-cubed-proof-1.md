Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {x^2 \rd x} {x^3 + a^3} = \frac 1 3 \ln \size {x^3 + a^3} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \map {\frac \d {\d x} } {x^3 + a^3}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 x^2\)





Derivative of Power








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \frac {x^2 \rd x} {x^3 + a^3}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 3 \ln \size {x^3 + a^3} + C\)





Primitive of Function under its Derivative



$\blacksquare$
