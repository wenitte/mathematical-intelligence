Theorem
Let $k \in \Z$.
Then:

$\map \csch {x + 2 k \pi i} = \csch x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \csch {x + 2 k \pi i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\map \sinh {x + 2 k \pi i} }\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Cosecant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\sinh x}\)





Periodicity of Hyperbolic Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \csch x\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Cosecant



$\blacksquare$
