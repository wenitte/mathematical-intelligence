Example of Addition of Codewords in $V \paren {2, 3}$
In the master code $V \paren {2, 3}$, the codewords $12$ and $11$ are added thus:

$12 + 11 = 20$


Proof
Taking the elements of $12$ and $11$ in pairs, successively, we have:














\(\ds 1 + 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 2\)

\(\ds \pmod 3\)






















\(\ds 2 + 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 0\)

\(\ds \pmod 3\)







Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
