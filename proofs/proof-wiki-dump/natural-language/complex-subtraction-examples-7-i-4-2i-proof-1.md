Example of Complex Subtraction
$\paren {7 + i} - \paren {4 - 2 i} = 3 + 3 i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {7 + i} - \paren {4 - 2 i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {7 - 4} + \paren {1 - \paren {-2} } i\)





Definition of Complex Subtraction














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 + 3 i\)









$\blacksquare$
