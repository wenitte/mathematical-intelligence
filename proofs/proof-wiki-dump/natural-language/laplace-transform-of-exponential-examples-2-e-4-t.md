Example of Use of Laplace Transform of Exponential
$\laptrans {2 e^{4 t} } = \dfrac 2 {s - 4}$
for $s > 4$.


Proof













\(\ds \laptrans {2 e^{4 t} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \laptrans {e^{4 t} }\)





Linear Combination of Laplace Transforms














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 2 {s - 4}\)





Laplace Transform of Exponential



$\blacksquare$
