Theorem
$\map \sec {x + \pi} = -\sec x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \sec {x + \pi}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\map \cos {x + \pi} }\)





Secant is Reciprocal of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {-\cos x}\)





Cosine of Angle plus Straight Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sec x\)





Secant is Reciprocal of Cosine



$\blacksquare$
