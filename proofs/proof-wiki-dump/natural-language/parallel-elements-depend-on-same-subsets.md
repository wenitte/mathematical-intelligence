Theorem
Let $M = \struct {S, \mathscr I}$ be a matroid.
Let $A \subseteq S$.
Let $x, y \in S$.
Let $x$ be parallel to $y$.

Then:

$x$ depends on $A$ if and only if $y$ depends on $A$


Proof
This follows directly from Closure of Subset Contains Parallel Elements.
$\blacksquare$
