Theorem
Let $\KK$ be a rectangular hyperbola in standard form.

$\KK$ can be expressed in parametric form as:

$\begin {cases} x = c t \\ y = \dfrac c t \end {cases}$


Proof
Let the point $\tuple {x, y}$ satisfy the equations:














\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds c t\)




















\(\ds y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac c t\)










Then:














\(\ds x y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds c t \times \dfrac c t\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds c^2\)









$\blacksquare$
