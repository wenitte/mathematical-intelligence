Theorem
The set of integers under addition $\struct {\Z, +}$ forms a semigroup.


Proof
Closure
Integer Addition is Closed.
$\Box$


Associativity
Integer Addition is Associative.
$\Box$

The criteria for $\struct {\Z, +}$ to be a semigroup are seen to be satisfied. 
$\blacksquare$
