Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau}$ be a finite complement topology on an infinite set $S$.

Then $T$ is not a metrizable space.


Proof
We have:

Metrizable Space is Hausdorff
Finite Complement Space is not $T_2$ (Hausdorff)
$\blacksquare$
