Theorem
Let the Axiom of Choice be accepted.
Then Zorn's Lemma holds.


Proof
We have that Axiom of Choice implies Tukey's Lemma.
Then we have Tukey's Lemma implies Zorn's Lemma.
$\blacksquare$
