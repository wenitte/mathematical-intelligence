Theorem
Let $B = \tuple {S, \preceq}$ be a Boolean lattice.
Let $I$ be a proper ideal in $B$.
Then, there exists a prime ideal $P$ in $B$ such that $I \subseteq P$.


Proof from Ultrafilter Lemma
