Corollary to Forward Difference of Power
$\Delta 2^x = 2^x$
where $\Delta$ denotes the forward difference operator.


Proof













\(\ds \Delta 2^x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2 - 1} 2^x\)





Forward Difference of Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^x\)









$\blacksquare$
