Theorem
Let $\Sigma^1$ be a smooth $1$-manifold.
Let $\Sigma^1$ satisfy:

$H_0 \struct {\Sigma; \Z} = 0$
and:

$H_m \struct {\Sigma; \Z} = \Z$
Then $\Sigma^1$ is homeomorphic to the $1$-sphere $\Bbb S^1$.
