Theorem
Let $\exp x$ be the exponential of $x$.

Then:

$\exp 1 = e$
where $e$ is Euler's number: $e = 2.718281828\ldots$


Proof
We have that the exponential function is the inverse of the natural logarithm function:

$\ln e = 1$
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
