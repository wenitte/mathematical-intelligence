Theorem
Let $M = \struct {A, d}$ be a metric space.
Let $a \in A$ be a point in $M$.
Let $N$ be a neighborhood of $a$ in $M$.

Then $a \in N$.


Proof
Trivially follows by definition of neighborhood of $a$.
$\blacksquare$
