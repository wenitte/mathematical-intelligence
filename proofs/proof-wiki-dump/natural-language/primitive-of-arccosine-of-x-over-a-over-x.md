Theorem
$\ds \int \dfrac 1 x \arccos \frac x a \rd x = \frac \pi 2 \ln \size x - \int \frac {\arcsin \frac x a \rd x} x + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \dfrac 1 x \arccos \frac x a \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \dfrac 1 x \paren {\frac \pi 2 - \arcsin \frac x a} \rd x\)





Sum of Arcsine and Arccosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac \pi 2 \int \frac {\d x} x - \int \dfrac 1 x \arcsin \frac x a \rd x\)





Linear Combination of Primitives














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac \pi 2 \ln \size x - \int \dfrac 1 x \arcsin \frac x a \rd x + C\)





Primitive of Reciprocal



$\blacksquare$
