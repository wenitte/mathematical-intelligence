Theorem
Let $\struct {S, \preceq}$ be an ordered set.
Let $s$ be an element of $S$.

Then:

$\map \inf {s^\succeq} = s$
where $s^\succeq$ denotes the upper closure of $s$.


Proof













\(\ds \map \inf {s^\succeq}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \inf {\set s^\succeq}\)





Upper Closure of Singleton














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \inf {\set s}\)





Infimum of Upper Closure of Set














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds s\)





Infimum of Singleton



$\blacksquare$
