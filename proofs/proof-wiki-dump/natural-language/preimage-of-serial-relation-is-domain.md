Theorem
Let $\RR$ be a serial relation on $S$.
Then the preimage of $\RR$ is $S$ (the domain of $\RR$).


Proof













\(\ds S\)

\(\supseteq\)







\(\ds \Preimg \RR\)





Definition of Preimage of Relation










\(\ds \forall s \in S: \exists t \in S: \, \)



\(\ds \tuple {s, t}\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds \RR\)





Definition of Serial Relation








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)

\(\ds \forall s \in S: \, \)



\(\ds s\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds \Preimg \RR\)





Definition of Preimage of Relation








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds S\)

\(\subseteq\)







\(\ds \Preimg \RR\)





Definition of Subset








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds S\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \Preimg \RR\)
