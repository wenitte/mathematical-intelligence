Theorem
Let $x \in \R$ be a real number.

$x^{\overline 1} = x$
where $x^{\overline 1}$ denotes the rising factorial.


Proof













\(\ds x^{\overline 1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\map \Gamma {x + 1} } {\map \Gamma x}\)





Rising Factorial as Quotient of Factorials














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x\)





Gamma Difference Equation



$\blacksquare$
