Example of Multiplication using Scientific Notation
$\paren {10^3} \paren {10^2} = 10^5$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {10^3} \paren {10^2}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10^{3 + 2}\)





Product of Powers














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10^5\)










That is:














\(\ds \paren {10^3} \paren {10^2}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1000 \times 100\)





Definition of Scientific Notation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 100 \, 000\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10^5\)





Definition of Scientific Notation



$\blacksquare$
