Theorem
$\ds \int \tan x \rd x = -\ln \size {\cos x} + C$
where $\cos x \ne 0$.


Proof













\(\ds \int \tan x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \frac {\sin x} {\cos x} \rd x\)





Definition of Real Tangent Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\int \frac {-\sin x} {\cos x} \rd x\)





Primitive of Constant Multiple of Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\int \frac {\paren {\cos x}'} {\cos x} \rd x\)





Derivative of Cosine Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\ln \size {\cos x} + C\)





Primitive of Function under its Derivative



$\blacksquare$
