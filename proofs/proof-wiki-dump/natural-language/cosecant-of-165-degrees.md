Theorem
$\csc 165 \degrees = \csc \dfrac {11 \pi} {12} = \sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 165 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \csc {180 \degrees - 15 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \csc 15 \degrees\)





Cosecant of Supplementary Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2\)





Cosecant of 15 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
