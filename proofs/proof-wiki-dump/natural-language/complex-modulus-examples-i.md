Example of Complex Modulus
$\cmod i = \cmod {-i} = 1$


Proof













\(\ds \cmod i\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cmod {0 + 1 i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {0^2 + 1^2}\)





Definition of Complex Modulus














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)
























\(\ds \cmod {-i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cmod {0 + \paren {-1} i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {0^2 + \paren {-1}^2}\)





Definition of Complex Modulus














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
