Example of Power of 2 is Difference between Two Powers
$2^0 = 3^2 - 2^3$


Proof













\(\ds 3^2 - 2^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 9 - 8\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^0\)









$\blacksquare$
