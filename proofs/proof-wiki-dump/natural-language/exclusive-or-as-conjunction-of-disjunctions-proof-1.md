$p \oplus q \dashv \vdash \left({p \lor q}\right) \land \left({\neg p \lor \neg q}\right)$


Proof













\(\ds p \oplus q\)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds \paren {p \lor q} \land \neg \paren {p \land q}\)

\(\ds \)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds \paren {p \lor q} \land \paren {\neg p \lor \neg q}\)
