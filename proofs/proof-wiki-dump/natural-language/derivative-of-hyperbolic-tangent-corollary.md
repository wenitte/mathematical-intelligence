Theorem
$\map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tanh x} = 1 - \tanh^2 x$
where $\tanh$ is the hyperbolic tangent.


Proof













\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\tanh x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sech^2 x\)





Derivative of Hyperbolic Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 - \tanh^2 x\)





Sum of Squares of Hyperbolic Secant and Tangent



$\blacksquare$
