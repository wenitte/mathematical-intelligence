Theorem
Let $\Sigma$ be an alphabet.

Let $\Sigma^{+}$ be the Kleene plus of $\Sigma$ and $\circ$ denote concatenation. 

Then $(\Sigma^{+}, \circ)$ is a semigroup.


Proof
Follows immediately from Kleene Plus is Linguistic Structure and Concatenation is Associative.

$\blacksquare$
