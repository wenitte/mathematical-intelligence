Example of Factorial
$10! = 3 \, 628 \, 000$


Proof
From Examples of Factorials:

$9! = 362 \, 880$
Then:














\(\ds 10!\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10 \times 9!\)





Definition of Factorial














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \, 628 \, 000\)









$\blacksquare$
