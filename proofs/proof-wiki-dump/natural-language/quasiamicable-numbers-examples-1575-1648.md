Examples of Quasiamicable Pair
$1575$ and $1648$ form a quasiamicable pair.


Proof













\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {1575}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3224\)





$\sigma_1$ of $1575$














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {1648}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3224\)





$\sigma_1$ of $1648$














\(\ds 1575 + 1648 + 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3224\)









Hence the result by definition of quasiamicable pair.
$\blacksquare$
