Example of Congruence Modulo an Integer
$42 \equiv 18 \pmod 8$


Proof













\(\ds 42\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 8 + 18\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 42\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 18\)

\(\ds \pmod 8\)







$\blacksquare$
