Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\cot a x} = \frac {-\ln \size {\cos a x} } a + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\cot a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \tan a x \rd x\)





Cotangent is Reciprocal of Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {-\ln \size {\cos a x} } a + C\)





Primitive of $\tan a x$



$\blacksquare$
