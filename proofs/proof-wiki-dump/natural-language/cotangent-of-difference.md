Corollary to Cotangent of Sum
$\map \cot {a - b} = \dfrac {\cot a \cot b + 1} {\cot b - \cot a}$
where $\cot $ is cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \map \cot {a - b}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cot a \, \map \cot {-b} - 1} {\cot a + \map \cot {-b} }\)





Cotangent of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {-\cot a \cot b - 1} {\cot a - \cot b}\)





Cotangent Function is Odd














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cot a \cot b + 1} {\cot b - \cot a}\)





multiplying numerator and denominator by $-1$ and rearranging



$\blacksquare$
