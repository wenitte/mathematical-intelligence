Example of Secant of Conjugate Angle
$\map \sec {360 \degrees - 3 x} = \sec 3 x$


Proof
From Secant of Conjugate Angle:

$\map \sec {2 \pi - \theta} = \sec \theta$
The result follows by setting $\theta = 3 x$.
$\blacksquare$
