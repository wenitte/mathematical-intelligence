Theorem
Let $X$ be a discrete random variable with a Bernoulli distribution with parameter $p$.

Then the expectation of $X$ is given by:

$\expect X = p$


Proof
Follows directly from Expectation of Binomial Distribution, putting $n = 1$.
$\blacksquare$
