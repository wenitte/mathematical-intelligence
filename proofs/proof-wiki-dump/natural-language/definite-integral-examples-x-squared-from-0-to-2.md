Example of Definite Integral
$\ds \int_0^2 x^2 \rd x = \dfrac 8 3$


Proof













\(\ds \int_0^2 x^2 \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \intlimits {\dfrac {x^3} 3} 0 2\)





Primitive of Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {2^3} 3 - \dfrac {0^3} 3\)





Definition of Definite Integral














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 8 3\)





evaluating



$\blacksquare$
