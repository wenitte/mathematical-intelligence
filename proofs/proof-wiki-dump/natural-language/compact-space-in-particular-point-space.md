Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau_p}$ be a particular point space.

Then $\set p$ is compact in $T$.


Proof
Any open cover of $\set p$ has a finite subcover: any single set that contains $p$ is a cover for $\set p$.
So $\set p$ is compact in $T$.
$\blacksquare$
