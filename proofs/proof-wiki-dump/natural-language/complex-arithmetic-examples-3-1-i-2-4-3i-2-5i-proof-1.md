Example of Complex Arithmetic
$3 \paren {1 + i} + 2 \paren {4 - 3 i} - \paren {2 + 5 i} = 9 - 8 i$


Proof













\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 3 \paren {1 + i} + 2 \paren {4 - 3 i} - \paren {2 + 5 i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 + 3} + \paren {8 - 6 i} - \paren {2 + 5 i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren 3 + 8 - 2} + \paren {3 - 6 - 5} i\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 9 - 8 i\)









$\blacksquare$
