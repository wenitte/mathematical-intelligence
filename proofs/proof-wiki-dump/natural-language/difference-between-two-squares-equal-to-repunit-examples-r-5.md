Example of Difference between Two Squares equal to Repunit
We have that:














\(\ds 11 \, 111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 41 \times 271\)




















\(\ds 11 \, 111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 11 \, 111\)
























\(\ds 11 \, 111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11 \, 111 \times 1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {11 \, 111 + 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5556\)




















\(\ds \frac {11 \, 111 - 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5555\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 5556^2 - 5555^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 30 \, 869 \, 136 - 30 \, 858 \, 025\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11 \, 111\)
























\(\ds 11 \, 111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 271 \times 41\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {271 + 41} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 156\)




















\(\ds \frac {271 - 41} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 115\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 156^2 - 115^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 24 \, 336 - 13 \, 225\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11 \, 111\)









$\blacksquare$
