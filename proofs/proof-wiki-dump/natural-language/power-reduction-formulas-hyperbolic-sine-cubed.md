Theorem
$\sinh^3 x = \dfrac {\sinh 3 x - 3 \sinh x} 4$
where $\sin$ denotes hyperbolic sine.


Proof













\(\ds \sinh 3 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \sinh x + 4 \sinh^3 x\)





Triple Angle Formula for Hyperbolic Sine








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 4 \sinh^3 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sinh 3 x - 3 \sinh x\)





rearranging








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \sinh^3 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sinh 3 x - 3 \sinh x} 4\)





dividing both sides by $4$



$\blacksquare$
