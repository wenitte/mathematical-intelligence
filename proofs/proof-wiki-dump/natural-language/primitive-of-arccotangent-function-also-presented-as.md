Primitive of Arccotangent Function: Also presented as
This result can also be presented as:

$\ds \int \arccot x \rd x = x \arccot x + \ln \sqrt {x^2 + 1} + C$


Proof
From Primitive of $\arccot x$:

$\ds \int \arccot x \rd x = x \arccot x + \dfrac 1 2 \map \ln {x^2 + 1} + C$
The result follows by observing:

$\dfrac 1 2 \log x = \log \sqrt x$
from Logarithm of Power.
$\blacksquare$
