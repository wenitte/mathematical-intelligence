Theorem
$\cos 144 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {4 \pi} 5 = -\dfrac \phi 2 = -\dfrac {1 + \sqrt 5} 4$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 144 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {180 \degrees - 36 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cos 36 \degrees\)





Cosine of Supplementary Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\dfrac {1 + \sqrt 5} 4\)





Cosine of $36 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
