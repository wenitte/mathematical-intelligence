Example of Use of Primitive of Power
$\ds \int x \rd x = \dfrac {x^2} 2 + C$


Proof













\(\ds \ds \int x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ds \int x^1 \rd x\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {x^{1 + 1} } {1 + 1} + C\)





Primitive of Power: $n = 1$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {x^2} 2 + C\)





simplifying



$\blacksquare$
