Examples of Complex Dot Product
Let:

$z_1 = 3 - 4 i$
$z_2 = -4 + 3 i$
Then:

$z_1 \circ z_2 = -24$
where $\circ$ denotes (complex) dot product.


Proof













\(\ds z_1 \circ z_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 - 4 i} \circ \paren {-4 + 3 i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times \paren {-4} + \paren {-4} \times 3\)





Definition 1 of Dot Product














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -12 + -12\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -24\)









$\blacksquare$
