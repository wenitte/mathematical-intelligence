Theorem
The set of rational numbers under addition $\struct {\Q, +}$ forms a monoid.


Proof
Follows directly from Rational Numbers under Addition form Infinite Abelian Group.
By definition, an abelian group is a group.
Also by definition, a group is a monoid.
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
