Example of Hexadecimal Notation
The integer expressed in decimal as $712$ is expressed in hexadecimal as $2 \mathrm C 8$.


Proof













\(\ds 2 \mathrm C 8_{1 6}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 16^2 + 12 \times 16 + 8\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 512 + 192 + 8\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 712\)









$\blacksquare$
