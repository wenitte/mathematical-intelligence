Theorem
For all numbers $a, b$ where $a, b$ in $\N, \Z, \Q$ or $\R$:

$\max \set {a, b} = \frac 1 2 \paren {a + b + \size {a - b} }$
Proof













\(\ds \max \set {a, b}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a + b - \min \set {a, b}\)





Sum Less Minimum is Maximum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds a + b - \frac 1 2 \paren {a + b - \size {a - b} }\)





Min is Half of Sum Less Absolute Difference














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 2 \paren {2 a + 2 b - \paren {a + b - \size {a - b} } }\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 2 \paren {2 a + 2 b - a - b + \size {a - b} }\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 2 \paren {a + b + \size {a - b} }\)









$\blacksquare$
