Source: https://proofwiki.org/wiki/Ordinals_under_Addition_form_Ordered_Monoid

Theorem
$\struct {\On, +, \le}$ forms an ordered monoid, where:

$\On$ denotes the class of all ordinals
$+$ denotes ordinal addition.


Proof
The result follows from Ordinals under Addition form Monoid and Ordinals under Addition form Ordered Semigroup.
$\blacksquare$
