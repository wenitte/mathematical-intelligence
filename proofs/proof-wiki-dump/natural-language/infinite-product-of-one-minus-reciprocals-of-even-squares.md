Theorem













\(\ds \prod_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \paren {1 - \frac 1 {\paren {2 n}^2} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {1 - \frac 1 4} \times \paren {1 - \frac 1 {16} } \times \paren {1 - \frac 1 {36} } \times \cdots\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 2 \pi\)











Proof













\(\ds \prod_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \paren {1 - \frac 1 {\paren {2 n}^2} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \prod_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac {4 n^2 - 1} {4 n^2}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \prod_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac {n^2 - \frac 1 4} {n^2}\)





multiplying top and bottom by $\frac 1 4$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \prod_{n \mathop = 1}^\infty \frac {\paren {n - \frac 1 2} \paren {n + \frac 1 2} } {\paren {n - 0} \paren {n - 0} }\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\map \Gamma 1 \map \Gamma 1} {\map \Gamma {\frac 1 2} \map \Gamma {\frac 3 2} }\)





Infinite Product of Product of Sequence of n plus alpha over Sequence of n plus beta














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\map \Gamma 1 \map \Gamma 1} {\frac 1 2 \map \Gamma {\frac 1 2} \map \Gamma {\frac 1 2} }\)





Gamma Difference Equation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {0! \times 0! } {\frac 1 2 \times \sqrt \pi \times \sqrt \pi}\)





Gamma Function Extends Factorial, Gamma Function of One Half














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 2 \pi\)





Factorial of Zero



$\blacksquare$
