Theorem
$\exp 0 = 1$


Proof













\(\ds \map \exp {z + \paren {-z} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \exp z \, \map \exp {-z}\)





Exponential of Sum








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \map \exp {z - z}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\exp z} {\exp z}\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \exp 0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
