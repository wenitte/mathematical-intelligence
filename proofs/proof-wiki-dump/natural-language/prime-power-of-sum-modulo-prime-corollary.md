Corollary to Prime Power of Sum Modulo Prime
Let $p$ be a prime number.
Then:

$\forall n \in \N_{> 0}: \paren {1 + b}^{p^n} \equiv 1 + b^{p^n} \pmod p$


Proof
Follows immediately from Prime Power of Sum Modulo Prime by putting $a = 1$.
$\blacksquare$
