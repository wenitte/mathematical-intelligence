Theorem
$\floor {\dfrac 1 2} = 0$
where $\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof
We have that:

$0 \le \dfrac 1 2$
and:

$1 = \dfrac 2 2 > \dfrac 1 2$
Hence $0$ is the floor of $\dfrac 1 2$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$
