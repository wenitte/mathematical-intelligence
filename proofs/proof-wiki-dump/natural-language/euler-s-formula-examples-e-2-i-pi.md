Example of Use of Euler's Formula
$e^{2 i \pi} = 1$


Proof













\(\ds e^{2 i \pi}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 2 \pi + i \sin 2 \pi\)





Euler's Formula














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + i \times 0\)





Cosine of $2 \pi$, Sine of $2 \pi$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
