Example of Use of Complex Addition is Commutative
$\paren {3 + 2 i} + \paren {-7 - i} = -4 + i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {3 + 2 i} + \paren {-7 - i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 + \paren {-7} } + \paren {2 + \paren {-1} } i\)





Definition of Complex Addition














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -4 + i\)









$\blacksquare$
