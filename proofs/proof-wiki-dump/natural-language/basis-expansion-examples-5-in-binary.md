Example of Basis Expansion
The number $5$ has a basis expansion in binary (base $2$) notation as:

$\sqbrk {101}_2$


Proof













\(\ds \sqbrk {101}_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 2^2 + 0 \times 2^1 + 1 \times 2^0\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 + 0 + 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5\)









$\blacksquare$
