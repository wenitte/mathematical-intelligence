Lemma for Expectation of Logistic Distribution
$\ds \int_{\to 0}^{\to 1} \map \ln u \rd u = -1$


Proof













\(\ds \int_{\to 0}^{\to 1} \map \ln u \rd u\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \bigintlimits {u \map \ln u - u} 0 1\)





Primitive of Logarithm of x














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren {0 - 1} - \paren {0 - 0} }\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -1\)









$\blacksquare$
