Theorem
Let $\struct {S, \preceq}$ be a totally ordered set. 
Then the min semigroup $\struct {S, \min}$ is a semilattice.


Proof
We have:

Min Semigroup is Commutative
Min Semigroup is Idempotent.
Hence the result, by definition of a semilattice.
$\blacksquare$
