Example of Cardinality
Let $S_1 = \set {-1, 0, 1}$.

The cardinality of $S_1$ is given by:

$\card {S_1} = 3$


Proof
$S_1$ has $3$ elements: $-1, 0, 1$.
Hence the result by definition of cardinality.
$\blacksquare$
