Example of Factorial
$13! = 6 \, 227 \, 020 \, 800$


Prime Factors of $13!$
The prime decomposition of $13!$ is given as:

$13! = 2^{10} \times 3^5 \times 5^2 \times 7 \times 11 \times 13$


Proof
From $12$ factorial:

$12! = 479 \, 001 \, 600$
Then:

  479 001 600
x          13
-------------
1 437 004 800
4 790 016 000
-------------
6 227 020 800

$\blacksquare$
