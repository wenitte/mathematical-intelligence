Theorem
Let $I$ be a set.
Denote with $\FF$ the set of finite subsets of $I$.
Let $\subseteq$ be the subset relation on $\FF$.

Then $\struct {\FF, \subseteq}$ is a directed set.


Proof
A fortiori from Finite Subsets form Directed Ordering.
$\blacksquare$
