Theorem













\(\ds \paren {\sum_{k \mathop = 1}^n {a_k}^2} \paren {\sum_{k \mathop = 1}^n {b_k}^2} - \paren {\sum_{k \mathop = 1}^n a_k b_k}^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sum_{i \mathop = 1}^{n - 1} \sum_{j \mathop = i + 1}^n \paren {a_i b_j - a_j b_i}^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 2 \sum_{i \mathop = 1}^n \sum_{\substack {1 \mathop \le j \mathop \le n \\ j \mathop \ne i} } \paren {a_i b_j - a_j b_i}^2\)











Proof
A special case of the Binet-Cauchy Identity.
$\blacksquare$
