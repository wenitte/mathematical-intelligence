Example of Power of 2 is Difference between Two Powers
$2^2 = 5^3 - 11^2$


Proof













\(\ds 5^3 - 11^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 125 - 121\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^2\)









$\blacksquare$
