Theorem
Let $\struct {R, +, \circ}$ be a ring.
Then $\struct {R, +, \circ}_R$ is an $R$-module. 


Proof
This is a special case of Module on Cartesian Product is Module:

$\struct {R^n, +, \circ}_R$ is an $R$-module
where $n = 1$.
$\blacksquare$
