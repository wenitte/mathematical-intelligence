Theorem
Let $\struct {G, \circ}$ be a group which has an identity element $e \in G$.
Then $e$ is unique.


Proof
By the definition of a group, $\struct {G, \circ}$ is also a monoid.
The result follows by applying the result Identity of Monoid is Unique.
$\blacksquare$
