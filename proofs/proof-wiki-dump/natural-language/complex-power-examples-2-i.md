Example of Complex Power
$2^i = \map \cos {\ln 2} + i \map \sin {\ln 2}$


Proof













\(\ds 2^i\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \exp {i \ln 2}\)





Definition of Complex Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {\ln 2} + i \map \sin {\ln 2}\)





De Moivre's Formula



$\blacksquare$
