Example of Inverse of Subset of Group
Let $\struct {\R, \times}$ be the multiplicative group of (non-zero) real numbers.
Let $S = \set {-1, 2}$.
Then the inverse $S^{-1}$ of $S$ is:

$S^{-1} = \set {-1, \dfrac 1 2}$


Proof
Taking each element of $S$:














\(\ds \paren {-1}^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -1\)




















\(\ds 2^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 2\)









$\blacksquare$
