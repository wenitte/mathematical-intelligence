Theorem
$\ceiling {4 \cdotp 35} = 5$
where $\ceiling x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof
We have that:

$4 < 4 \cdotp 35 \le 5$
Hence $5$ is the ceiling  of $4 \cdotp 35$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$


Also see
Floor of $4 \cdotp 35$: $\floor {4 \cdotp 35} = 4$
