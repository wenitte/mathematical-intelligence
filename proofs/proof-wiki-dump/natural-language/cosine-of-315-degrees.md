Theorem
$\cos 315 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {7 \pi} 4 = \dfrac {\sqrt 2} 2$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 315 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {360 \degrees - 45 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 45 \degrees\)





Cosine of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sqrt 2} 2\)





Cosine of $45 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
