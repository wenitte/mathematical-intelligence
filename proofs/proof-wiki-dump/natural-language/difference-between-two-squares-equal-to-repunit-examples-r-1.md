Example of Difference between Two Squares equal to Repunit













\(\ds 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {1 + 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)




















\(\ds \frac {1 - 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 1^2 - 0^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
