Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \set {\O, S} }$ be an indiscrete topological space.
Then  $T$ is a second-countable space.


Proof
The only basis for $T$ is $\set S$ which is trivially countable.
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
