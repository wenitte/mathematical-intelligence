Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau}$ be the either-or space.

Then $T$ is a $T_4$ space.


Proof
Follows from:

Either-Or Topology is $T_5$
$T_5$ Space is $T_4$ Space.
$\blacksquare$
