Theorem
$\sec 210 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {7 \pi} 6 = -2 \dfrac {\sqrt 3} 3$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 210 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 150 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 150 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -2 \frac {\sqrt 3} 3\)





Secant of $150 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
