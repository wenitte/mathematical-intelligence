Theorem
$\cot 135 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {3 \pi} 4 = -1$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 135 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot \paren {90 \degrees + 45 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\tan 45 \degrees\)





Cotangent of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds - 1\)





Tangent of $45 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
