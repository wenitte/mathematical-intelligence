Corollary to Derivative of Exponential Function
Let $a \in \R_{>0}$.
Let $a^x$ be $a$ to the power of $x$.

Then:

$\map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {a^x} = a^x \ln a$


Proof













\(\ds \map {\frac \d {\d x} } {a^x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map {\frac \d {\d x} } {e^{x \ln a} }\)





Definition of Power to Real Number














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln a e^{x \ln a}\)





Derivative of $e^{a x}$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^x \ln a\)









$\blacksquare$
