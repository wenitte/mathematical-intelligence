Theorem
Let $\struct {G, \odot, \tau}$ be a topological group.
Let $A, B \subseteq G$.

Then: 

$\paren {A \odot B}^{-1} = B^{-1} \odot A^{-1}$


Proof
The proof is identical to Inverse of Product of Subsets of Group.
$\blacksquare$
