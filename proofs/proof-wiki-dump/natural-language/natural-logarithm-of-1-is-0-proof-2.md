Theorem
$\ln 1 = 0$


Proof
We use the definition of the natural logarithm as the inverse of the exponential:

$\ln x = y \iff e^y = x$

Then:














\(\ds e^0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)





Exponential of Zero








\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds \ln 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)









$\blacksquare$
