Theorem
Let $G$ be a group whose identity is $e$.
For the conjugacy action:

$\order {\Orb e} = 1$
and thus:

$\Stab e = G$


Proof













\(\ds g * e\)

\(=\)







\(\ds g e g^{-1}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds g g^{-1}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds e\)










So the only conjugate of $e$ is $e$ itself.
Thus:

$\Orb e = \set e$
and so:

$\order {\Orb e} = 1$
From the Orbit-Stabilizer Theorem, it follows immediately that:

$\Stab e = G$
$\blacksquare$
