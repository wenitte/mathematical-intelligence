Example of Complex Modulus
$\cmod {7 + 24 i} = 25$


Proof













\(\ds \cmod {7 + 24 i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {7^2 + 24^2}\)





Definition of Complex Modulus














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {625}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 25\)









$\blacksquare$
