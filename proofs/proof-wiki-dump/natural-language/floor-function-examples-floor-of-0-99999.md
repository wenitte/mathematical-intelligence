Theorem
$\floor {0 \cdotp 99999} = 0$
where $\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof
We have that:

$0 \le 0 \cdotp 99999 < 1$
Hence $0$ is the floor of $0 \cdotp 99999$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$
