Corollary to Power of Sum Modulo Prime
Let $p$ be a prime number.
Then:

$\paren {1 + b}^p \equiv 1 + b^p \pmod p$


Proof
Follows immediately from Power of Sum Modulo Prime by putting $a = 1$.
$\blacksquare$
