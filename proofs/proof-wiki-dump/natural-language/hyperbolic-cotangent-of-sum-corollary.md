Corollary to Hyperbolic Cotangent of Sum
$\map \coth {a - b} = \dfrac {\coth a \coth b - 1} {\coth b - \coth a}$
where $\coth$ denotes the hyperbolic cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \map \coth {a - b}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\coth a \map \coth {-b} + 1} {\map \coth {-b} + \coth a}\)





Hyperbolic Cotangent of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {-\coth a \coth b + 1} {\coth a - \coth b}\)





Hyperbolic Cotangent Function is Odd














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\coth a \coth b - 1} {\coth b - \coth a}\)





multiplying numerator and denominator by $-1$ and rearranging



$\blacksquare$
