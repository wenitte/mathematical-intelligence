Theorem
$\ds \int \tanh x \rd x = \map \ln {\cosh x} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \tanh x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -i \int \tan i x \rd x\)





Hyperbolic Tangent in terms of Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\int \tan i x \rd \paren {i x}\)





Primitive of Function of Constant Multiple














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \cmod {\cos i x} + C\)





Primitive of $\tan x$: Cosine Form














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \cmod {\cosh x} + C\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \ln {\cosh x} + C\)





Graph of Hyperbolic Cosine Function: $\cosh x > 0$ for all $x$



$\blacksquare$
