Example of Cubic Recurring Digital Invariant
$136$ is a cubic recurring digital invariant:








\(\ds 136: \ \ \)





\(\ds 1^3 + 3^3 + 6^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + 27 + 216\)

\(\ds = 244\)












\(\ds 244: \ \ \)





\(\ds 2^3 + 4^3 + 4^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 8 + 64 + 64\)

\(\ds = 136\)







$\blacksquare$
