Example of Use of LCM from Prime Decomposition
The lowest common multiple of $111$ and $1221$ is:

$\lcm \set {111, 1221} = 1221$


Proof













\(\ds 111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 37\)




















\(\ds 1221\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 11 \times 37\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^1 \times 11^0 \times 37^1\)




















\(\ds 1221\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^1 \times 11^1 \times 37^1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \lcm \set {111, 1221}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^1 \times 11^1 \times 37^1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1221\)









$\blacksquare$
