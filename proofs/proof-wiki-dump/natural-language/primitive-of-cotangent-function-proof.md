Theorem
$\ds \int \cot x \rd x = \ln \size {\sin x} + C$
where $\sin x \ne 0$.


Proof













\(\ds \int \cot x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \frac {\cos x} {\sin x} \rd x\)





Definition of Real Cotangent Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \frac {\paren {\sin x}'} {\sin x} \rd x\)





Derivative of Sine Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\sin x} + C\)





Primitive of Function under its Derivative



$\blacksquare$
