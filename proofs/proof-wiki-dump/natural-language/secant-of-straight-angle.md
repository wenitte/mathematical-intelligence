Theorem
$\sec 180 \degrees = \sec \pi = -1$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 180 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {90 \degrees + 90 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 90 \degrees\)





Secant of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -1\)





Cosecant of Right Angle



$\blacksquare$
