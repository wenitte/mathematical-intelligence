Theorem
The set of integers under addition $\struct {\Z, +}$ forms a group.


Proof
From Integers under Addition form Abelian Group, $\struct {\Z, +}$ forms an abelian group.
An abelian group is a fortiori a group.
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
