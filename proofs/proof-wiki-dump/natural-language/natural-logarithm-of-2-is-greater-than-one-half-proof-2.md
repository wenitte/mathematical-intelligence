Lemma
$\ln 2 \ge \dfrac 1 2$
where $\ln$ denotes the natural logarithm function.


Proof













\(\ds 1 - \frac 1 x\)

\(\le\)







\(\ds \ln x\)





Lower Bound of Natural Logarithm








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac 1 2\)

\(\le\)







\(\ds \ln 2\)





letting $x = 2$



$\blacksquare$
