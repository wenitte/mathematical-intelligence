Example of General Logarithm
The logarithm base $\pi$ of $\pi$ is:

$\log_\pi \pi = 1$


Proof
An instance of Logarithm to Own Base equals 1:

$\forall b \in \R_{>0}, b \ne 1: \log_b b = 1$
$\blacksquare$
