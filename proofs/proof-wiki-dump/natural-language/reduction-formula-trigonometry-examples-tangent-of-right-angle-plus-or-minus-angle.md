Example of Reduction Formula in context of Trigonometry
$\map \tan {90 \degrees \pm \theta} = \mp \cot \theta$


Proof
From Tangent of Angle plus Right Angle:

$\map \tan {90 \degrees + \theta} = -\cot \theta$
From Tangent of Complement equals Cotangent:

$\map \tan {90 \degrees - \theta} = \cot \theta$
$\blacksquare$
