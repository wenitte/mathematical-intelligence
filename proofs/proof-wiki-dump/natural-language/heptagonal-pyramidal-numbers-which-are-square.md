Theorem
The sequence of heptagonal pyramidal numbers which also have the property of being square begins:

$0, 1, 196, 99 \, 225$

\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds \dfrac {0 \paren {0 + 1} \paren {5 \times 0 - 2} } 6\)





Closed Form for Heptagonal Pyramidal Numbers














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {0 \times 1 \times \paren {- 2} } 6\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0^2\)
