Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $341$ and $527$ is found to be:

$\gcd \set {341, 527} = 31$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 527\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 341 + 186\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 341\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 186 + 155\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 186\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 155 + 31\)




















\(\ds 155\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 \times 31\)









Thus:

$\gcd \set {341, 527} = 31$
$\blacksquare$
