Theorem
Let $G$ be a group.
Let $e$ be the identity of $G$.

Then $e$ is in its own singleton conjugacy class:

$\conjclass e = \set e$


Proof
From Identity of Group is in Center:

$e \in \map Z G$
where $\map Z G$ is the center of $G$.
From Conjugacy Class of Element of Center is Singleton:

$\conjclass e = \set e$
$\blacksquare$
