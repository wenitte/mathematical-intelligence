Corollary to Primitive of Square of Hyperbolic Sine Function
$\ds \int \sinh^2 x \rd x = \frac {\sinh x \cosh x - x} 2 + C$
where $C$ is an arbitrary constant.


Proof













\(\ds \int \sinh^2 x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sinh 2 x} 4 - \frac x 2 + C\)





Primitive of Square of Hyperbolic Sine Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2 \sinh x \cosh x} 4 - \frac x 2 + C\)





Double Angle Formula for Hyperbolic Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\sinh x \cosh x - x} 2 + C\)





rearranging



$\blacksquare$
