Theorem
$\cos 150 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {5 \pi} 6 = -\dfrac {\sqrt 3} 2$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 150 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {90 \degrees + 60 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sin 60 \degrees\)





Cosine of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\frac {\sqrt 3} 2\)





Sine of $60 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
