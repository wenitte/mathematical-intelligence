Theorem
Everywhere that the function is defined:

$\map \arctan {\dfrac 1 x} = \arccot x$
where $\arctan$ and $\arccot$ denote arctangent and arccotangent respectively.


Proof













\(\ds \map \arctan {\frac 1 x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds y\)














\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac 1 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \tan y\)





Definition of Real Arctangent








\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot y\)





Cotangent is Reciprocal of Tangent








\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds \arccot x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds y\)





Definition of Real Arccotangent



$\blacksquare$
