Example of Congruence Modulo an Integer
$8 \equiv -1 \pmod 3$


Proof













\(\ds 8\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 3 - 1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 8\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds -1\)

\(\ds \pmod 3\)







$\blacksquare$
