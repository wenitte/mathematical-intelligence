Theorem
Let $G$ be a cyclic group.

Then $G$ is abelian.


Proof
We have that Integers under Addition form Abelian Group.
The result then follows from combining:

Epimorphism from Integers to Cyclic Group
Epimorphism Preserves Commutativity.
$\blacksquare$
