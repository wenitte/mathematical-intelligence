Theorem
Let $\arctan: \R \to \openint {-\dfrac \pi 2} {\dfrac \pi 2}$ be the real arctangent.

Then $\arctan$ is of exponential order $0$.


Proof
Follows from Function with Limit at Infinity of Exponential Order Zero.
$\blacksquare$
