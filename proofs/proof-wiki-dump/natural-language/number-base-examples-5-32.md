Example of Number expressed in Base $5$ Notation
The integer expressed in decimal as $32$ is expressed in base $5$ as $112_5$.


Proof













\(\ds 32\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 5^2 + 1 \times 5 + 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 25 + 5 + 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 112_5\)









$\blacksquare$
