Example of Octal Notation
The rational number expressed in octal as $371 \cdotp 24$ is expressed in decimal to $2$ decimal places as $249 \cdotp 31$.


Proof













\(\ds 371 \cdotp 24_8\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 8^2 + 7 \times 8 + 1 + 2 \times 8^{-1} + 4 \times 8^{-2}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 192 + 56 + 1 + 0.25 + 0.0625\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 249 \cdotp 3125\)




















\(\ds \)

\(\approx\)







\(\ds 249 \cdotp 31\)









$\blacksquare$
