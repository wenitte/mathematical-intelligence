Theorem
Let $T = \struct {\R, \tau}$ be the compact complement topology on $\R$.

Then $T$ is not an ultraconnected space.


Proof
We have:

Compact Complement Topology is $T_1$
Non-Trivial Ultraconnected Space is not $T_1$
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
