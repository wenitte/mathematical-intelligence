Theorem
Let $\triangle ABC$ be a right triangle with $c$ as the hypotenuse.
Then:

$a^2 + b^2 = c^2$


Proof

We have that $CH = BS = AB = AJ$.
Hence the result follows directly from Pythagoras's Theorem for Parallelograms.
$\blacksquare$
