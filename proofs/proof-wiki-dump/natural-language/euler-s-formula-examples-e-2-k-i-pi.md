Example of Use of Euler's Formula
$\forall k \in \Z: e^{2 k i \pi} = 1$


Proof













\(\ds e^{2 k i \pi}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 2 k \pi + i \sin 2 k \pi\)





Euler's Formula














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + i \times 0\)





Cosine of Multiple of $\pi$, Sine of Multiple of $\pi$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
