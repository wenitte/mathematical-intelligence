Example of Use of LCM from Prime Decomposition
The lowest common multiple of $253$ and $506$ is:

$\lcm \set {253, 506} = 506$


Proof













\(\ds 253\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 11 \times 23\)




















\(\ds 506\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 11 \times 23\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 253\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^0 \times 11^1 \times 23^1\)




















\(\ds 506\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^1 \times 11^1 \times 23^1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \lcm \set {253, 506}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^1 \times 11^1 \times 23^1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 506\)









$\blacksquare$
