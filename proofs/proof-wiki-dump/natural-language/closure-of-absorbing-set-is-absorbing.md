Theorem
Let $\GF \in \set {\R, \C}$.
Let $X$ be a vector space over $\GF$. 
Let $A \subseteq X$ be an absorbing set.

Then $A^-$ is absorbing.


Proof
From the definition of closure, we have $A \subseteq A^-$.
From Superset of Absorbing Set is Absorbing, $A^-$ is absorbing.
$\blacksquare$
