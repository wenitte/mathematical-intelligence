Theorem
Let $S$ be a finite set.
Let $S^n$ be a cartesian space on $S$.

Then:

$\card {S^n} = \card S^n$
where $\card {\, \cdot \,}$ denotes cardinality.


Proof
This is an instance of Cardinality of Cartesian Product of Finite Sets: General Result, where each set is equal. 
$\blacksquare$
