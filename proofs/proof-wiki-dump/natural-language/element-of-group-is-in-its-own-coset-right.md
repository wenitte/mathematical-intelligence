Theorem
Let $G$ be a group.
Let $H$ be a subgroup of $G$.
Let $x \in G$.
Let:

$H x$ be the right coset of $x$ modulo $H$.

Then:

$x \in H x$


Proof
Let $e$ be the identity of $G$.
Then:














\(\ds e\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds H\)





Identity of Subgroup














\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e x\)





Definition of Identity Element








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)

\(\ds \exists h \in H: \, \)



\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds h x\)





Existential Generalisation








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds H x\)





Definition of Right Coset



$\blacksquare$
