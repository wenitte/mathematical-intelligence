Example of Divisor Sum of Integer
$\map {\sigma_1} {22 \, 365} = 44 \, 928$
where $\sigma_1$ denotes the divisor sum function.


Proof
We have that:

$22 \, 365 = 3^2 \times 5 \times 7 \times 71$

Hence:














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {22 \, 365}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {3^3 - 1} {3 - 1} \times \paren {5 + 1} \times \paren {7 + 1} \times \paren {71 + 1}\)





Divisor Sum of Integer














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {26} 2 \times 6 \times 8 \times 72\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 13 \times 6 \times 8 \times 72\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 13 \times \paren {2 \times 3} \times 2^3 \times \paren {2^3 \times 3^2}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^7 \times 3^3 \times 13\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 44 \, 928\)









$\blacksquare$
