Theorem
Let $T$ be a triangle, which can be any shape at all, even scalene.
Then $T$ can tessellate the plane.


Proof
By placing $2$ copies of $T$ together so their corresponding sides coincide, it is possible to form a quadrilateral.
The result follows from Quadrilateral Tessellates the Plane.
$\blacksquare$
