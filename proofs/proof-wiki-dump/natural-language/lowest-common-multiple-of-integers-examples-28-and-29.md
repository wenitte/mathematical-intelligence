Example of Lowest Common Multiple of Integers
The lowest common multiple of $28$ and $29$ is:

$\lcm \set {28, 29} = 812$


Proof













\(\ds \lcm \set {28, 29}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 28 \times 29\)





Lowest Common Multiple of Consecutive Integers














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 812\)









$\blacksquare$
