Theorem
Let $f$ be an arithmetic function.
Let $*$ denote Dirichlet convolution.
Let $\iota$ be the identity arithmetic function.

Then:

$\iota * f = f$


Proof
We have:














\(\ds \map {\paren {\iota * f} } n\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sum_{d \mathop \divides n} \delta_{d 1} \map f {\frac n d}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map f n\)









Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
