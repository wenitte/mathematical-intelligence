Theorem
$\sec 150 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {5 \pi} 6 = -\dfrac {2 \sqrt 3} 3$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 150 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {90 \degrees + 60 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 60 \degrees\)





Secant of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\dfrac {2 \sqrt 3} 3\)





Cosecant of $60 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
