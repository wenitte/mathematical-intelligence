Example of Integer Power
$-3^{-3} = -\dfrac 1 {27}$


Proof













\(\ds -3^{-3}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-\dfrac 1 3}^3\)





Definition of Integer Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 {\paren {-3}^3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 {-27}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\dfrac 1 {27}\)









$\blacksquare$
