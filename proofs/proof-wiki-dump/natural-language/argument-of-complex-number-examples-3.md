Example of Argument of Complex Number
$\arg 3 = 0$


Proof
We have that:

$3 = 3 + 0 i$
and so:














\(\ds \size 3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {3^2 + 0^2}\)





Definition of Complex Modulus














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3\)










Hence:














\(\ds \map \cos {\map \arg 3}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 3 3\)





Definition of Argument of Complex Number














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \map \arg 3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





Cosine of Zero is One


















\(\ds \map \sin {\map \arg 3}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 0 1\)





Definition of Argument of Complex Number














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \map \arg 3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0 \text { or } \pi\)





Sine of Multiple of Pi




Hence:

$\map \arg 3 = 0$
$\blacksquare$
