Theorem
Let $\struct{L, \vee, \wedge, \preceq}$ be a locale.

Let $\map {\operatorname{Sp}} L$ denote the spectrum of $L$.

Then:

$\map {\operatorname{Sp}} L$ is a sober space.


Proof
$\blacksquare$
