Example of General Harmonic Number
$\harm 1 {\dfrac 2 3} = \dfrac 3 2 - \dfrac 3 2 \ln 3 + \dfrac \pi {2 \sqrt 3}$
where $\harm 1 {\dfrac 2 3}$ denotes the general harmonic number of order $1$ evaluated at $\dfrac 2 3$.
