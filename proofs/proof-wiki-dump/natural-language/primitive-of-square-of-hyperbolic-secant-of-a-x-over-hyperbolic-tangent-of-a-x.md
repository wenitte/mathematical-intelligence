Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {\sech^2 a x \rd x} {\tanh a x} = \frac 1 a \ln \size {\tanh a x} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \frac {\d} {\d x} \tanh a x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a \sech^2 a x\)





Derivative of $\tanh a x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \frac {a \sech^2 a x \rd x} {\tanh a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\tanh a x} + C\)





Primitive of Function under its Derivative








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \frac {\sech^2 a x \rd x} {\tanh a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \ln \size {\tanh a x} + C\)





Primitive of Constant Multiple of Function



$\blacksquare$
