Theorem
$\ds \int \csc a x \rd x = \frac 1 a \ln \size {\tan \frac {a x} 2} + C$
where $\tan \dfrac {a x} 2 \ne 0$.


Proof













\(\ds \int \csc x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\tan \frac x 2}\)





Primitive of $\csc x$: Tangent Form








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \csc a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \ln \size {\tan \frac {a x} 2} + C\)





Primitive of Function of Constant Multiple



$\blacksquare$
