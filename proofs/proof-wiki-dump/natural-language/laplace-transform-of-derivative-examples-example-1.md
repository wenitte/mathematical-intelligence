Examples of Use of Laplace Transform of Derivative
Let $\laptrans f$ denote the Laplace transform of the real function $f$.


$\laptrans {-3 \sin 3 t} = \dfrac {-9} {s^2 + 9}$


Proof













\(\ds \laptrans {-3 \sin 3 t}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds s \laptrans {\cos 3 t} - \cos 0\)





Laplace Transform of Derivative, Derivative of $\cos a x$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds s \dfrac s {s^2 + 9} - 1\)





Laplace Transform of Cosine, Cosine of Zero is One














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {-9} {s^2 + 9}\)





simplification



$\blacksquare$
