Examples of Quasiamicable Pair
$1050$ and $1925$ form a quasiamicable pair.


Proof













\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {1050}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2976\)





$\sigma_1$ of $1050$














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {1925}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2976\)





$\sigma_1$ of $1925$














\(\ds 1050 + 1925 + 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2976\)









Hence the result by definition of quasiamicable pair.
$\blacksquare$
