Theorem
$\cos 225 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {5 \pi} 4 = -\dfrac {\sqrt 2} 2$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 225 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {360 \degrees - 135 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos 135 \degrees\)





Cosine of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\frac {\sqrt 2} 2\)





Cosine of $135 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
