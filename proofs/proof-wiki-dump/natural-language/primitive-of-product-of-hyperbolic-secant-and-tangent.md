Theorem
$\ds \int \sech x \tanh x \rd x = -\sech x + C$
where $C$ is an arbitrary constant.


Proof
From Derivative of Hyperbolic Secant:

$\dfrac \d {\d x} \sech x = -\sech x \tanh x$
The result follows from the definition of primitive.
$\blacksquare$
