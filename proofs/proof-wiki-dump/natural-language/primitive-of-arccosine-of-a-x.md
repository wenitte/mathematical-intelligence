Theorem
$\ds \int \arccos a x \rd x = x \arccos a x - \dfrac 1 a \sqrt {1 - a^2 x^2} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \arccos x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \arccos x - \sqrt {1 - x^2} + C\)





Primitive of $\arccos x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \int \arccos a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 a \paren {\paren {a x} \arccos a x - \sqrt {1 - \paren {a x}^2} } + C\)





Primitive of Function of Constant Multiple














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \arccos a x - \dfrac 1 a \sqrt {1 - a^2 x^2} + C\)





simplifying



$\blacksquare$
