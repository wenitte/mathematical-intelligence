Theorem
$0.999 \ldots = 1$


Proof













\(\ds 0.333 \ldots\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 / 3\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 3 \paren {0.333 \ldots}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \paren {1 / 3}\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 0.999 \ldots\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 / 3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
