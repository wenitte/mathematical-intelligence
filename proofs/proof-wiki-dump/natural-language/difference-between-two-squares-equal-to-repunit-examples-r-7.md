Example of Difference between Two Squares equal to Repunit
We have that:














\(\ds 1 \, 111 \, 111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 239 \times 4649\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 1 \, 111 \, 111\)
























\(\ds 1 \, 111 \, 111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \, 111 \, 111 \times 1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {1 \, 111 \, 111 + 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 555 \, 556\)




















\(\ds \frac {1 \, 111 \, 111 - 1} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 555 \, 555\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 555 \, 556^2 - 555 \, 555^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 308 \, 642 \, 469 \, 136 - 308 \, 641 \, 358 \, 025\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \, 111 \, 111\)
























\(\ds 1 \, 111 \, 111\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4649 \times 239\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \frac {4649 + 239} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2444\)




















\(\ds \frac {4649 - 239} 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2205\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 2444^2 - 2205^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 \, 973 \, 136 - 4 \, 862 \, 025\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \, 111 \, 111\)









$\blacksquare$
