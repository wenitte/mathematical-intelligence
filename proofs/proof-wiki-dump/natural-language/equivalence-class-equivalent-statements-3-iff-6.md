Theorem
Let $\RR$ be an equivalence relation on $S$.
Let $x, y \in S$.
The following statements are equivalent:

$x \mathrel \RR y$
$\eqclass x \RR \cap \eqclass y \RR \ne \O$


Proof
Follows directly from Equivalence Classes are Disjoint.
$\blacksquare$
