Theorem
$\ds \int \sec x \rd x = \ln \size {\map \tan {\frac x 2 + \frac \pi 4} } + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \sec x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\tan x + \sec x} + C\)





Primitive of $\sec x$: Secant plus Tangent Form














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\map \tan {\frac x 2 + \frac \pi 4} } + C\)





Tangent of Half Angle plus $\dfrac \pi 4$



$\blacksquare$
