Theorem
Let $\mathbb U$ be a Grothendieck universe.
Let $u \in \mathbb U$.
Let $v \subseteq u$ be a subset of $u$.

Then $v \in \mathbb U$.


Proof













\(\ds v\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds \powerset u\)





Definition of Power Set














\(\ds u\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds \mathbb U\)





by hypothesis








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \powerset u\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds \mathbb U\)





Grothendieck Universe: Axiom $(3)$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds v\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds \mathbb U\)





Grothendieck Universe: Axiom $(1)$



$\blacksquare$
