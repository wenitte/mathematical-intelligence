Theorem
Let $G$ be a group.
Let $Z$ be the kernel of the conjugacy action.

Then $Z$ is the center of $G$.


Proof

\(\)

\(\)

\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)

\(\ds \forall y \in G: \, \)



\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds x y = y x\)
