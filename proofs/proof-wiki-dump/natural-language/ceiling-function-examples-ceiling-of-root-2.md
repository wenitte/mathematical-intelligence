Theorem
$\ceiling {\sqrt 2} = 2$
where $\ceiling x$ denotes the ceiling of $x$.


Proof
The decimal expansion of $\sqrt 2$ is:

$\sqrt 2 \approx 1.41421 \ 35623 \ 73095 \ 0488 \ldots$
Thus:

$1 < \sqrt 2 \le 2$
Hence $2$ is the ceiling of $\sqrt 2$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$
