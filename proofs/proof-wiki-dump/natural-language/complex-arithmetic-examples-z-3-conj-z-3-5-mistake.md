Source Work
1981: Murray R. Spiegel: Theory and Problems of Complex Variables (SI ed.)

Chapter $1$: Complex Numbers
Supplementary Problems: Fundamental Operations with Complex Numbers: $54 \ \text {(c)}$


Mistake
If ... $z_3 = \sqrt 3 - 2 i$, evaluate ...:
$\paren {z_3 - \overline {z_3} }^5$


Ans. $1024 i$


Correction
This result actually works out to $-1024 i$:














\(\ds \paren {z_3 - \overline {z_3} }^5\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren {\sqrt 3 - 2 i} - \overline {\paren {\sqrt 3 - 2 i} } }^5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren {\sqrt 3 - 2 i} - \paren {\sqrt 3 + 2 i} }^5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-4 i}^5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-1}^5 \times 4^5 \times i^5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-1} \times 1024 \times i\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -1024 i\)









$\blacksquare$
