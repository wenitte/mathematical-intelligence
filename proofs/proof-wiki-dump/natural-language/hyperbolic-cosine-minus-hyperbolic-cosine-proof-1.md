Theorem
$\cosh x - \cosh y = 2 \map \sinh {\dfrac {x + y} 2} \map \sinh {\dfrac {x - y} 2}$


Proof













\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 2 \, \map \sinh {\frac {x + y} 2} \, \map \sinh {\frac {x - y} 2}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \, \frac {\map \cosh {\dfrac {x + y} 2 + \dfrac {x - y} 2} - \map \cosh {\dfrac {x + y} 2 - \dfrac {x - y} 2} } 2\)





Werner Formula for Hyperbolic Sine by Hyperbolic Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cosh \frac {2 x} 2 - \cosh \frac {2 y} 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cosh x - \cosh y\)









$\blacksquare$
