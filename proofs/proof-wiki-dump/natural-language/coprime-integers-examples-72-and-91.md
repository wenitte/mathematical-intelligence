Example of Coprime Integers
$72$ and $91$ are coprime.


Proof
The positive divisors of $91$ are $1$, $7$, $13$ and $91$.
Apart from $1$, none of these integers is also a divisor of $72$.
Hence the result by definition of coprime.
$\blacksquare$
