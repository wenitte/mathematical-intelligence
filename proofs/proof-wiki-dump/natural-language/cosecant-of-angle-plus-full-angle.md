Theorem
$\csc \left({x + 2 \pi}\right) = \csc x$


Proof













\(\ds \csc \left({x + 2 \pi}\right)\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\sin \left({x + 2 \pi}\right)}\)





Cosecant is Reciprocal of Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\sin x}\)





Sine of Angle plus Straight Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \csc x\)





Cosecant is Reciprocal of Sine



$\blacksquare$
