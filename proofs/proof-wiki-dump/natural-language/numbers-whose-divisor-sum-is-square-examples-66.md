Example of Number whose Divisor Sum is Square
$\map {\sigma_1} {66} = 144 = 12^2$


Proof
From $\sigma_1$ of $66$:

$\map {\sigma_1} {66} = 144$
which equals $12^2$.
$\blacksquare$
