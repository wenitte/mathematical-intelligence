Theorem
Let $\RR$ be an equivalence relation on $S$.
Let $x, y \in S$.
The following statements are equivalent:

$x \mathrel \RR y$
$y \in \eqclass x \RR$


Proof
This follows through dint of the symmetry of $\RR$ and the definition of Equivalence Class.
$\blacksquare$
