Theorem
A recurrence relation for the square numbers is:

$S_n = S_{n - 1} + 2 n - 1$


Proof













\(\ds S_n\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sum_{j \mathop = 1}^n \paren {2 j - 1}\)





Odd Number Theorem














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sum_{j \mathop = 1}^{n - 1} \paren {2 j - 1} + \paren {2 n - 1}\)





Definition of Summation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds S_{n - 1} + \paren {2 n - 1}\)





Odd Number Theorem



$\blacksquare$
