Examples of Operations on Product Elements
Solve for $x$ in:

$a x a^{-1} = e$


Solution













\(\ds a x a^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds a^{-1} a x a^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^{-1} e\)





Product of both sides with $a^{-1}$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds a^{-1} a x a^{-1} a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^{-1} e a\)





Product of both sides with $a$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^{-1} e a\)





Group Axiom $\text G 3$: Existence of Inverse Element








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^{-1} a\)





Group Axiom $\text G 2$: Existence of Identity Element








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e\)





Group Axiom $\text G 3$: Existence of Inverse Element



$\blacksquare$
