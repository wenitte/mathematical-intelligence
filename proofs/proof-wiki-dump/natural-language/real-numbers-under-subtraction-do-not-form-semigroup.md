Theorem
The set of real numbers under subtraction $\struct {\R, -}$ does not form a semigroup.


Proof
We have that Subtraction on Numbers is Not Associative.
Hence $\struct {\R, -}$ is not a semigroup by definition.
$\blacksquare$
