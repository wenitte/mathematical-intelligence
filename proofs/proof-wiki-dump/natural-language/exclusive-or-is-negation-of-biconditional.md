Theorem
Exclusive or is equivalent to the negation of the biconditional:

$p \oplus q \dashv \vdash \neg \paren {p \iff q}$


Proof













\(\ds p \oplus q\)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds \paren {p \lor q} \land \neg \paren {p \land q}\)





Definition of Exclusive Or














\(\ds \)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds \neg \paren {p \iff q}\)





Non-Equivalence as Conjunction of Disjunction with Negation of Conjunction



$\blacksquare$
