Theorem
$\sec 0 = 0$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 0 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {\cos 0 \degrees}\)





Secant is Reciprocal of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 1\)





Cosine of Zero is One














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
