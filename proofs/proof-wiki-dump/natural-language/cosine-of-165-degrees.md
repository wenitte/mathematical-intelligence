Theorem
$\cos 165 \degrees = \cos \dfrac {11 \pi} {12} = - \dfrac {\sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2} 4$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof













\(\ds \cos 165 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {90 \degrees + 75 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sin 75 \degrees\)





Cosine of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\dfrac {\sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2} 4\)





Sine of $75 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
