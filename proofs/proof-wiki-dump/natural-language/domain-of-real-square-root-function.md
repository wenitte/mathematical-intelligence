Theorem
The domain of the real square root function is the set of positive real numbers $\R_{\ge 0}$:

$\set{x \in \R: x \ge 0}$


Proof
From Square of Real Number is Non-Negative:

$\forall x \in \R: x^2 \ge 0$
Hence the result by definition of domain.
$\blacksquare$
