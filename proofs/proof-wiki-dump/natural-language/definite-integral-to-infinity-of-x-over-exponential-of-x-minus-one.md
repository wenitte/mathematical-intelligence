Theorem
$\ds \int_0^\infty \frac x {e^x - 1} \rd x = \frac {\pi^2} 6$


Proof













\(\ds \int_0^\infty \frac x {e^x - 1} \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int_0^\infty \frac {x^{2 - 1} } {e^x - 1} \rd x\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \zeta 2 \map \Gamma 2\)





Integral Representation of Riemann Zeta Function in terms of Gamma Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\pi^2} 6 \times 1!\)





Basel Problem, Gamma Function Extends Factorial














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\pi^2} 6\)









$\blacksquare$
