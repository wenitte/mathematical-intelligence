Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau}$ be the Arens-Fort space.

Then $T$ is a $T_1$ (Fréchet) space.


Proof
We have that the Arens-Fort Space is Completely Hausdorff.
Then from Sequence of Implications of Separation Axioms, a $T_{2 \frac 1 2}$ (completely Hausdorff) space is a $T_1$ space.
$\blacksquare$
