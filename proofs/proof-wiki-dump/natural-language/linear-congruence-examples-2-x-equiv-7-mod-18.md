Example of Linear Congruence
The linear congruence:

$2 x \equiv 7 \pmod {18}$
has no solution in $\Z$.


Proof
We have that:

$\gcd \set {2, 18} = 2$
But $2$ is not a divisor of $7$.
Hence by Solution of Linear Congruence, $2 x \equiv 7 \pmod {18}$ has no solutions in the integers.
$\blacksquare$
