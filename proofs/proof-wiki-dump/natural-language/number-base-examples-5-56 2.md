Example of Number expressed in Base $5$ Notation
The integer expressed in decimal as $56$ is expressed in base $5$ as $211_5$.


Proof













\(\ds 56\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 5^2 + 1 \times 5 + 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 50 + 1 + 2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 211_5\)









$\blacksquare$
