Example of Addition of Fractions
$\dfrac 1 2 + \dfrac 1 3 = \dfrac 5 6$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac 1 2 + \dfrac 1 3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {3 \times 1} {3 \times 2} + \dfrac {2 \times 1} {2 \times 3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 3 6 + \dfrac 2 6\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {3 + 2} 6\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 5 6\)









$\blacksquare$
