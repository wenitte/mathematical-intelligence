Theorem
$\sec 330 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {11 \pi} 6 = 2 \dfrac {\sqrt 3} 3$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 330 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 30 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 30 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \frac {\sqrt 3} 3\)





Secant of $30 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
