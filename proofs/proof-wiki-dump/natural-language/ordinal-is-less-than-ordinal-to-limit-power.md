Theorem
Let $x$, $y$, and $z$ be ordinals.
Let $z$ be a limit ordinal.

Then:

$x < y^z \iff \exists w \in z: x < y^w$


Proof













\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds x < y^z\)














\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds x < \bigcup_{w \mathop \in z} y^w\)





Definition of Ordinal Exponentiation








\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds \exists w \in z: x < y^w\)





Definition of Union of Family



$\blacksquare$
