Theorem
$\cot 225^\circ = \cot \dfrac {5 \pi} 4 = 1$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 225^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot \left({360^\circ - 135^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 135^\circ\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)





Cotangent of 135 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
