Example of Complex Modulus
$\cmod {-4 + 2 i} = 2 \sqrt 5$


Proof













\(\ds \cmod {-4 + 2 i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {\paren {-4}^2 + 2^2}\)





Definition of Complex Modulus














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {20}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \sqrt 5\)









$\blacksquare$
