Examples of Complex Cross Product
Let:

$z_1 = 3 - 4 i$
$z_2 = -4 + 3 i$
Then:

$z_1 \times z_2 = -7$
where $\times$ denotes (complex) cross product.


Proof













\(\ds z_1 \circ z_2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 - 4 i} \times \paren {-4 + 3 i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 3 - \paren {-4} \times \paren {-4}\)





Definition 1 of Complex Cross Product














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 9 - 16\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -7\)









$\blacksquare$
