Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $527$ and $765$ is:

$\gcd \set {527, 765} = 17$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 765\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 527 + 238\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 527\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 238 + 51\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 238\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 \times 51 + 34\)










\(\text {(4)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 51\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 34 + 17\)










\(\text {(5)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 34\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 17\)









Thus:

$\gcd \set {527, 765} = 17$
$\blacksquare$
