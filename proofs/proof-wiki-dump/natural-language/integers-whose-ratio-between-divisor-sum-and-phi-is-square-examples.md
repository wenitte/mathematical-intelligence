Examples of Integers whose Ratio between Divisor Sum and Phi is Square
Example: $714$













\(\ds \map \phi {714}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 192\)





$\phi$ of $714$














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {714}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1728\)





$\sigma_1$ of $714$














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {714} / \map \phi {714}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1728 / 192\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 9\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^2\)









$\blacksquare$
