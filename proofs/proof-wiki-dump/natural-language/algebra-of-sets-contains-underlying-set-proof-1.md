Theorem
Let $S$ be a set.
Let $\RR$ be an algebra of sets on $S$.
Then:

$S \in \RR$


Proof
By definition $1$ of algebra of sets, we have that:




\((\text {AS} 1)\)  

$:$  



Unit:   



\(\ds S \in \RR \)   







  

The result is hence immediate.
$\blacksquare$
