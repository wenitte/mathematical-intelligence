Example of Möbius Function
For $n \in \Z_{>0}$, let $\map \mu n$ denote the Möbius function of $n$.
Then:

$\map \mu 4 = 0$


Proof













\(\ds 2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^2\)





Prime Decomposition of $4$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \map \mu 4\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





as $4$ is not square-free



$\blacksquare$
