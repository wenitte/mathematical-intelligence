Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $34$ and $102$ is found to be:

$\gcd \set {34, 102} = 34$


Proof













\(\ds 102\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 34\)










Thus:

$\gcd \set {34, 102} = 34$
$\blacksquare$
