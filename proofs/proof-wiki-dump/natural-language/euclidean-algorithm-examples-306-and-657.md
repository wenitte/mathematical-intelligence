Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $306$ and $657$ is:

$\gcd \set {306, 657} = 9$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 657\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 306 + 45\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 306\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6 \times 45 + 36\)










\(\text {(3)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 45\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 36 + 9\)










\(\text {(4)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 36\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 \times 9\)









Thus:

$\gcd \set {306, 657} = 9$
$\blacksquare$
