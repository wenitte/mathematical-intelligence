Theorem
$\cot 345 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {23 \pi} {12} = -\paren {2 + \sqrt 3}$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 345 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cot {360 \degrees - 15 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 15 \degrees\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {2 + \sqrt 3}\)





Cotangent of $15 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
