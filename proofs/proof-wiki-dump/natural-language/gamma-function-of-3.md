Theorem
Let $\Gamma$ denote the Gamma function.
Then:

$\map \Gamma 3 = 2$


Proof













\(\ds \map \Gamma 3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \Gamma {2 + 1}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \map \Gamma 2\)





Gamma Difference Equation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 1\)





Gamma Function of 2



$\blacksquare$
