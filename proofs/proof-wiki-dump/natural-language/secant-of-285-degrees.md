Theorem
$\sec 285 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {19 \pi} {12} = \sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 285 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 75 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 75 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2\)





Secant of $75 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
