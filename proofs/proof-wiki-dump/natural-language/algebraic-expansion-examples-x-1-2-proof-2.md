Example of Algebraic Expansion
$\paren {x + 1}^2 = x^2 + 2 x + 1$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {x + 1}^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {x + 1} \paren {x + 1}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \paren {x + 1} + 1 \paren {x + 1}\)





Distributive Property














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x^2 + 2 x + 1\)





Distributive Property



$\blacksquare$
