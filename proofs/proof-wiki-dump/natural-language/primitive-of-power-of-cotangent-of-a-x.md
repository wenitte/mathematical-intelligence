Theorem
$\ds \int \cot^n a x \rd x = \frac {-\cot^{n - 1} a x} {\paren {n - 1} a} - \int \cot^{n - 2} a x \rd x$
for $n \ne 1$.


Proof













\(\ds \int \cot^n a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \cot^{n - 2} a x \cot^2 a x \rd x\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \cot^{n - 2} a x \paren {\csc^2 a x - 1} \rd x\)





Difference of Squares of Cosecant and Cotangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \cot^{n - 2} a x \csc^2 a x \rd x - \int \cot^{n - 2} \rd x\)





Linear Combination of Primitives














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {-\cot^{n - 1} a x} {\paren {n - 1} a} - \int \cot^{n - 2} \rd x\)





Primitive of $\cot^n a x \csc^2 a x$



$\blacksquare$
