Theorem
Let $X$ be a set.
Let $S \subseteq X$, $Z \subseteq S$.

Then $i_S \sqbrk Z = Z$
where

$i_S$ denotes the inclusion mapping of $S$
$i_S \sqbrk Z$ denotes the image of $Z$ under $i_S$.


Proof













\(\ds i_S \sqbrk Z\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \set {\map {i_S} z: z \in Z}\)





Definition of Image of Subset under Mapping














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \set {z: z \in Z}\)





Definition of Inclusion Mapping














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds Z\)





Definition of Set Equality



$\blacksquare$
