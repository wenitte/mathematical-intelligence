Example of Euler $\phi$ Function of Non-Square Semiprime
$\map \phi {91} = 72$
where $\phi$ denotes the Euler $\phi$ Function.


Proof
We have that:

$91 = 7 \times 13$

Thus:














\(\ds \map \phi {91}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {7 - 1} \paren {13 - 1}\)





Euler $\phi$ Function of Non-Square Semiprime














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 6 \times 12\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 72\)









$\blacksquare$
