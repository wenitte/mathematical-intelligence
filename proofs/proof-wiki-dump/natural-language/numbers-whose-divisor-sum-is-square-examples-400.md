Example of Number whose Divisor Sum is Square
$\map {\sigma_1} {400} = 961 = 31^2$


Proof
From $\sigma_1$ of $400$:

$\map {\sigma_1} {400} = 961$
which equals $31^2$.
$\blacksquare$
