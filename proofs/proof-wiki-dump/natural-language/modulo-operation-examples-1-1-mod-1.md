Theorem
$1 \cdotp 1 \bmod 1 = 0 \cdotp 1$
where $\bmod$ denotes the modulo operation.


Proof
By definition of modulo $1$:

$x \bmod 1 = x - \floor x$

Thus:














\(\ds 1 \cdotp 1 \bmod 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \cdotp 1 - \floor {1 \cdotp 1}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \cdotp 1 - 1\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0 \cdotp 1\)









$\blacksquare$
