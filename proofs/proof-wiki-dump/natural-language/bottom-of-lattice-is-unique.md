Theorem
Let $\struct {S, \vee, \wedge, \preceq}$ be a lattice.

Then $S$ has at most one bottom.


Proof
By definition, a bottom for $S$ is a smallest element.
The result follows from Smallest Element is Unique.
$\blacksquare$
