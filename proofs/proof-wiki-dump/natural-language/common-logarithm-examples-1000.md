Example of Common Logarithm
The common logarithm of $1000$ is:

$\log_{10} 1000 = 3$


Proof













\(\ds 1000\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10 \times 10 \times 10\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 10^3\)





Definition of Integer Power








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \log_{10} 1000\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3\)









$\blacksquare$
