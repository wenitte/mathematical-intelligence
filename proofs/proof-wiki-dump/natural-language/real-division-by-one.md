Theorem
$\forall x \in \R: \dfrac x 1 = x$


Proof













\(\ds \frac x 1\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \times \frac 1 1\)





Definition of Real Division














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \times 1\)





Real Number Divided by Itself














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x\)





Real Number Axiom $\R \text M4$: Inverses for Multiplication



$\blacksquare$
