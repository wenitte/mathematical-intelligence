Example of Definite Integral
$\ds \int_0^4 \sqrt x \rd x = \dfrac {16} 3$


Proof













\(\ds \int_0^4 \sqrt x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \intlimits {\dfrac {2 x^{3/2} } 3} 0 4\)





Primitive of Square Root














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {2 \times \sqrt 4^3} 3 - \dfrac {2 \times \sqrt 0^3} 3\)





Definition of Definite Integral














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {16} 3\)





evaluating



$\blacksquare$
