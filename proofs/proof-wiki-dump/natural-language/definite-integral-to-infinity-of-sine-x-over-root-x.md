Theorem
$\ds \int_0^\infty \frac {\sin x} {\sqrt x} \rd x = \sqrt {\frac \pi 2}$


Proof













\(\ds \int_0^\infty \frac {\sin x} {\sqrt x} \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int_0^\infty 2 u \frac {\sin u^2} {\sqrt {u^2} } \rd u\)





substituting $x = u^2$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \int_0^\infty \sin u^2 \rd u\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {\frac \pi 2}\)





Definite Integral to Infinity of $\sin a x^2$



$\blacksquare$
