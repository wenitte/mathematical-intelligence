Theorem
The integers $\struct {\Z, +, \times}$ form a commutative ring with unity under addition and multiplication.


Proof
We have that:

$\struct {\Z, +, \times}$ form a commutative ring.
$\struct {\Z, +, \times}$ has a unity, and the unity is $1$.
$\blacksquare$
