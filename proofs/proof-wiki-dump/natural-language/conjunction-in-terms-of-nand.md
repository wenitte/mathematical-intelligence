Theorem
$p \land q \dashv \vdash \paren {p \uparrow q} \uparrow \paren {p \uparrow q}$
where $\land$ denotes logical conjunction and $\uparrow$ denotes logical NAND.


Proof













\(\ds p \land q\)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds \neg \neg \paren {p \land q}\)





Double Negation














\(\ds \)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds \neg \paren {p \uparrow q}\)





Definition of Logical NAND














\(\ds \)

\(\dashv \vdash\)







\(\ds \paren {p \uparrow q} \uparrow \paren {p \uparrow q}\)





NAND with Equal Arguments



$\blacksquare$
