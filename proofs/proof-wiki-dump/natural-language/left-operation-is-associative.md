Theorem
The left operation is associative:

$\forall x, y, z: \paren {x \gets y} \gets z = x \gets \paren {y \gets z}$


Proof













\(\ds x \gets \paren {y \gets z}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \gets y\)





Definition of Left Operation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x\)





Definition of Left Operation


















\(\ds \paren {x \gets y} \gets z\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x \gets z\)





Definition of Left Operation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x\)





Definition of Left Operation



$\blacksquare$
