Example of Divisor Sum of Integer
$\map {\sigma_1} {63} = 104$
where $\sigma_1$ denotes the divisor sum function.


Proof
We have that:

$63 = 3^2 \times 7$

Hence:














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} {63}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {3^3 - 1} {3 - 1} \times \frac {7^2 - 1} {7 - 1}\)





Divisor Sum of Integer














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {27 - 1} 2 \times \frac {49 - 1} 6\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 13 \times 8\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 104\)









$\blacksquare$
