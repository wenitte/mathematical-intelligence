Theorem
Let Hausdorff's Maximal Principle be accepted as true.
Then Kuratowski's Lemma holds.


Proof
We have:

Hausdorff's Maximal Principle implies Axiom of Choice
Axiom of Choice implies Kuratowski's Lemma
$\blacksquare$
