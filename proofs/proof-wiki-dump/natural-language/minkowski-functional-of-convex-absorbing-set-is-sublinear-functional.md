Theorem
Let $\GF \in \set {\R, \C}$. 
Let $X$ be a vector space over $\GF$.
Let $A$ be a convex absorbing set. 
Let $\mu_A$ be the Minkowski functional of $A$. 

Then $\mu_A$ is a sublinear functional.


Proof
This follows from Minkowski Functional of Convex Absorbing Set is Positive Homogeneous and Minkowski Functional of Convex Absorbing Set is Sublinear.
$\blacksquare$
