Example of Greatest Common Divisor of Integers
The greatest common divisor of $-5$ and $5$ is:

$\gcd \set {-5, 5} = 5$


Proof
From GCD of Integer and its Negative:

$\gcd \set {-5, 5} = 5$
$\blacksquare$
