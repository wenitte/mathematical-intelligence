Example of Complex Division
$\dfrac {1 - i} {1 + i} = -i$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac {1 - i} {1 + i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {\paren {1 - i} \paren {1 - i} } {\paren {1 + i} \paren {1 - i} }\)





multiplying top and bottom by $1 - i$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {1 - 2 i + i^2} {1^2 + 1^2}\)





simplifying














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {0 - 2 i} 2\)





simplifying














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -i\)









$\blacksquare$
