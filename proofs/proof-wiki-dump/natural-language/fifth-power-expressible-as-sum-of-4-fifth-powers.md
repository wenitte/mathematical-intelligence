Theorem
$61 \, 917 \, 364 \, 224$ can be expressed as the sum of $4$ fifth powers:

$61 \, 917 \, 364 \, 224 = 144^5 = 27^5 + 84^5 + 110^5 + 133^5$


Proof













\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 27^5 + 84^5 + 110^5 + 133^5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 14 \, 348 \, 907 + 4 \, 182 \, 119 \, 424 + 16 \, 105 \, 100 \, 000 + 41 \, 615 \, 795 \, 893\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 61 \, 917 \, 364 \, 224\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 144^5\)









$\blacksquare$
