Corollary to Range of Idempotent is Kernel of Complementary Idempotent
Let $\struct {\HH, \innerprod \cdot \cdot}$ be a Hilbert space.
Let $A$ be an idempotent operator.

Then:

$\ker A = \Rng {I - A}$


Proof
From Complementary Idempotent is Idempotent, we have that:

$I - A$ is an idempotent operator.
Hence by Range of Idempotent is Kernel of Complementary Idempotent, we have:

$\Rng {I - A} = \map \ker {I - \paren {I - A} } = \ker A$
$\blacksquare$
