Theorem
$\paren {1 + \cot x} \paren {1 + \tan x} = 2 + \csc x \sec x$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {1 + \cot x} \paren {1 + \tan x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + \cot x + \tan x + \cot x \tan x\)





multiplying out














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + \cot x + \tan x + \dfrac 1 {\tan x} \tan x\)





Cotangent is Reciprocal of Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 + \cot x + \tan x\)





simplifying














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 + \csc x \sec x\)





Sum of Tangent and Cotangent



$\blacksquare$
