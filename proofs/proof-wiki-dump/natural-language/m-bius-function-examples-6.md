Example of Möbius Function
For $n \in \Z_{>0}$, let $\map \mu n$ denote the Möbius function of $n$.
Then:

$\map \mu 6 = 1$


Proof













\(\ds 6\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 3\)





Prime Decomposition of $6$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \map \mu 6\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-1}^2\)





as $6$ has $2$ distinct prime divisors














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
