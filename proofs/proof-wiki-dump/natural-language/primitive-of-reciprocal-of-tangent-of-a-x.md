Theorem
$\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\tan a x} = \frac 1 a \ln \size {\sin a x} + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \frac {\d x} {\tan a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \cot a x \rd x\)





Cotangent is Reciprocal of Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 a \ln \size {\sin a x} + C\)





Primitive of $\cot a x$



$\blacksquare$
