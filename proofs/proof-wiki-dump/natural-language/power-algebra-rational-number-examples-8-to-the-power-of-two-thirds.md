Example of Rational Power
$8^{2/3} = 4$


Proof













\(\ds 8^{2/3}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren {2^3}^2}^{1/3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {4^3}^{1/3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4\)









$\blacksquare$
