Corollary to Double Angle Formula for Hyperbolic Sine
$\sinh 2 x = \dfrac {2 \tanh x} {1 - \tanh^2 x}$
where $\sin$ and $\tan$ denote hyperbolic sine and hyperbolic tangent respectively.


Proof













\(\ds \sinh 2 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \sinh x \cosh x\)





Double Angle Formula for Hyperbolic Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \sinh x \cosh x \frac {\cosh x} {\cosh x}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \tanh x \cosh^2 x\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2 \tanh x} {\sech^2 x}\)





Definition 2 of Hyperbolic Secant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2 \tanh x} {1 - \tanh^2 x}\)





Sum of Squares of Hyperbolic Secant and Tangent



$\blacksquare$
