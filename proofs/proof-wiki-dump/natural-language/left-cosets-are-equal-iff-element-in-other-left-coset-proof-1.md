Theorem
Let $x H$ denote the left coset of $H$ by $x$.

Then:

$x H = y H \iff x \in y H$


Proof













\(\ds x H\)

\(=\)







\(\ds y H\)














\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds x^{-1} y\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds H\)





Left Cosets are Equal iff Product with Inverse in Subgroup








\(\ds \leadstoandfrom \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(\in\)







\(\ds y H\)





Element in Left Coset iff Product with Inverse in Subgroup



$\blacksquare$
