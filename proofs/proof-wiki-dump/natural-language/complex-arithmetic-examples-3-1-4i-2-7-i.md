Example of Complex Arithmetic
$3 \paren {-1 + 4 i} - 2 \paren {7 - i} = -17 + 14 i$


Proof













\(\ds 3 \paren {-1 + 4 i} - 2 \paren {7 - i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {3 \times -1 + 3 \times 4 i} - \paren {2 \times 7 - 2 \times i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-3 + 12 i} - \paren {14 - 2 i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-3 - 14} + \paren {12 - \paren {-2} } i\)





Definition of Complex Subtraction














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -17 + 14 i\)









$\blacksquare$
