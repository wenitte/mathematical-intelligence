Theorem
Let $\struct {S, \vee, \wedge, \neg}$ be a Boolean algebra.

Then for all $a \in S$:

$\map \neg {\neg a} = a$


Proof
Follows directly from Complement in Boolean Algebra is Unique.
$\blacksquare$
