Theorem
$\csc 360 \degrees = \csc 2 \pi$ is undefined
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof
From Cosecant is Reciprocal of Sine:

$\csc \theta = \dfrac 1 {\sin \theta}$
From Sine of Full Angle:

$\sin 360 \degrees = 0$
Thus $\csc \theta$ is undefined at this value.
$\blacksquare$
