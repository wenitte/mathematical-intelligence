Example of Rational Power
$27^{-4/3} = \dfrac 1 {81}$


Proof













\(\ds 27^{-4/3}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\dfrac 1 {27^4} }^{1/3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\dfrac 1 {\paren {3^3}^4} }^{1/3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren {\dfrac 1 {3^4} }^3}^{1/3}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 {81}\)









$\blacksquare$
