Example of Quadratic Equation
The quadratic equation:

$3 x^2 + 24 x + 9 = 0$
has the roots:

$x = -4 \pm \sqrt {13}$


Proof













\(\ds 3 x^2 + 24 x + 9\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x^2 + 8 x + 3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





dividing both sides by $3$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \paren {x + 4}^2 - 16 + 3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0\)





Completing the Square








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \paren {x + 4}^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 13\)





simplifying








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x + 4\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \pm \sqrt {13}\)





simplifying



The result follows.
$\blacksquare$
