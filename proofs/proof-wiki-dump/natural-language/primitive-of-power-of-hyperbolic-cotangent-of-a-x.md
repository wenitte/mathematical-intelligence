Theorem
$\ds \int \coth^n a x \rd x = \frac {-\coth^{n - 1} a x} {a \paren {n - 1} } + \int \coth^{n - 2} a x \rd x + C$


Proof













\(\ds \int \coth^n a x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \coth^{n - 2} a x \coth^2 a x \rd x\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \coth^{n - 2} a x \paren {1 + \csch^2 a x} \rd x\)





Difference of Squares of Hyperbolic Cotangent and Cosecant














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \coth^{n - 2} a x \csch^2 a x \rd x + \int \coth^{n - 2} \rd x\)





Linear Combination of Primitives














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {-\coth^{n - 1} a x} {a \paren {n - 1} } + \int \coth^{n - 2} a x \rd x + C\)





Primitive of $\coth^n a x \csch^2 a x$



$\blacksquare$
