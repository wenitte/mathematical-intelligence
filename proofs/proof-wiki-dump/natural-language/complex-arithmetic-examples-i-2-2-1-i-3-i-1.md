Example of Complex Arithmetic
$\paren {i - 2} \paren {2 \paren {1 + i} - 3 \paren {1 - i} } = -9 + 7 i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {i - 2} \paren {2 \paren {1 + i} - 3 \paren {i - 1} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {i - 2} \paren {\paren {2 + 2 i} - \paren {-3 + 3 i} }\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-2 + i} \paren {5 - i}\)





Definition of Complex Subtraction














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren {-2} \times 5 - 1 \times \paren {-1} } + \paren {\paren {-2} \times \paren {-1} + 1 \times 5} i\)





Definition of Complex Multiplication














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-10 + 1} + \paren {2 + 7} i\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -9 + 7 i\)









$\blacksquare$
