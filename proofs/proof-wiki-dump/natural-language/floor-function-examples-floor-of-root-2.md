Theorem
$\floor {\sqrt 2} = 1$
where $\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof
The decimal expansion of $\sqrt 2$ is:

$\sqrt 2 \approx 1.41421 \ 35623 \ 73095 \ 0488 \ldots$
Thus:

$1 \le \sqrt 2 < 2$
Hence $1$ is the floor of $\sqrt 2$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$
