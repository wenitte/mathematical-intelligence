Theorem
Let $A$ be a class.

Then:

$A \ne \O \iff \exists x: x \in A$


Proof

\(\ds A \ne \O\)

\(\leadstoandfrom\)







\(\ds \neg \forall x: \paren {x \in A \iff x \in \O}\)





Definition of Class Equality














\(\ds \)

\(\leadstoandfrom\)







\(\ds \neg \forall x: \neg x \in A\)





Definition of Empty Set














\(\ds \)

\(\leadstoandfrom\)







\(\ds \exists x: x \in A\)
