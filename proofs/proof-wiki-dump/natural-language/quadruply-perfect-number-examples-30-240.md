Example of Quadruply Perfect Number
$30 \, 240$ is quadruply perfect:

$\map {\sigma_1} {30 \, 240} = 120 \, 960 = 4 \times 30 \, 240$


Proof
From $\sigma_1$ of $30 \, 240$:

$\map {\sigma_1} {30 \, 240} = 120 \, 960$
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
