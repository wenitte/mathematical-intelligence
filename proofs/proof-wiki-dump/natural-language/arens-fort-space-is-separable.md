Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau}$ be the Arens-Fort space.

Then $T$ is a separable space.


Proof
We have that the Arens-Fort space is an expansion of a countable Fort space.
So $S$ is countable.
The result follows from Countable Space is Separable.
$\blacksquare$
