Theorem
Let $M$ be a class.
Let $g: M \to M$ be a progressing mapping on $M$.
Let $M$ be a $g$-tower.

$\O$ is the smallest element of $M$.


Proof
Follows directly from $g$-Tower is Well-Ordered under Subset Relation.
$\blacksquare$
