Corollary to Cancellation Laws
$g h = g \implies h = e$


Proof













\(\ds g h\)

\(=\)







\(\ds g\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds g^{-1} \paren {g h}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds g^{-1} g\)





Group Axiom $\text G 2$: Existence of Identity Element








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \paren {g^{-1} g} h\)

\(=\)







\(\ds g^{-1} g\)





Group Axiom $\text G 1$: Associativity








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds e h\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e\)





Group Axiom $\text G 3$: Existence of Inverse Element








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds h\)

\(=\)







\(\ds e\)





Group Axiom $\text G 2$: Existence of Identity Element



$\blacksquare$
