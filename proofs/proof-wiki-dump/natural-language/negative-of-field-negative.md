Theorem
Let $\struct {F, +, \times}$ be a field whose zero is $0_F$.
Let $a \in F$ and let $-a$ be the field negative of $a$.

Then:

$-\paren {-a} = a$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {-a} + a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a + \paren {-a}\)





Field Axiom $\text A2$: Commutativity of Addition














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0_F\)





Field Axiom $\text A4$: Inverses for Addition








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {-a}\)





Definition of Field Negative



$\blacksquare$
