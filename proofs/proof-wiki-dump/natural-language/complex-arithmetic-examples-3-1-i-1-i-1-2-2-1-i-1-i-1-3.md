Example of Complex Arithmetic
$3 \paren {\dfrac {1 + i} {1 - i} }^2 - 2 \paren {\dfrac {1 - i} {1 + i} }^3 = -3 - 2 i$


Proof













\(\ds 3 \paren {\dfrac {1 + i} {1 - i} }^2 - 2 \paren {\dfrac {1 - i} {1 + i} }^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \paren {\dfrac {\paren {1 + i}^2} {\paren {1 - i} \paren {1 + i} } }^2 - 2 \paren {\dfrac {\paren {1 - i}^2} {\paren {1 + i} \paren {1 - i} } }^3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \paren {\dfrac {1 + 2 i + i^2} {1^2 + 1^2} }^2 - 2 \paren {\dfrac {1 - 2 i + i^2} {1^2 + 1^2} }^3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \paren {\dfrac {2 i} 2}^2 - 2 \paren {\dfrac {-2 i} 2}^3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 i^2 - 2 \paren {-i}^3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -3 - 2 \paren {-1}^3 i^3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -3 - 2 \paren {-1} \times \paren {-i}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -3 - 2 i\)









$\blacksquare$
