Theorem
Let $G$ be a group.
Then:

$G = \gen G$
where $\gen G$ denotes the group generated by $G$.


Proof
By definition of generated subgroup, $\gen G$ is the smallest subgroup of $G$ containing $G$.
Hence the result by Group is Subgroup of Itself.
$\blacksquare$
