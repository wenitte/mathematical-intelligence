Theorem
$\left({\dfrac 1 2}\right)! = \dfrac {\sqrt \pi} 2$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {\dfrac 1 2}!\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \Gamma {1 + \dfrac 1 2}\)





Gamma Function Extends Factorial














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 2 \map \Gamma {\dfrac 1 2}\)





Gamma Difference Equation














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac 1 2 \sqrt {\pi}\)





Gamma Function of One Half



$\blacksquare$
