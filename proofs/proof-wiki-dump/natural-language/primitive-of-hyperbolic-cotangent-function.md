Theorem
$\ds \int \coth x \rd x = \ln \size {\sinh x} + C$
where $\sinh x \ne 0$.


Proof













\(\ds \int \coth x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \frac {\cosh x} {\sinh x} \rd x\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Cotangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \frac {\paren {\sinh x}'} {\sinh x} \rd x\)





Derivative of Hyperbolic Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \size {\sinh x} + C\)





Primitive of Function under its Derivative



$\blacksquare$
