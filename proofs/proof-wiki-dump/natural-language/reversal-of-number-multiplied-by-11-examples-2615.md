Example of Reversal of Number Multiplied by 11
$11$ multiplied by the reversal of $2615$ equals the reversal of the product of $2615$ and $11$.


Proof
  2615     5162
 x  11    x  11
 -----    -----
  2615     5162
 26150    51620
 -----    -----
 28765    56782
 -----    -----

$\blacksquare$
