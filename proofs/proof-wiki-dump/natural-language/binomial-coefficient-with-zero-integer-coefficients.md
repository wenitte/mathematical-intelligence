Theorem
$\forall n \in \N: \dbinom n 0 = 1$
where $\dbinom n 0$ denotes a binomial coefficient.


Proof
From the definition:














\(\ds \binom n 0\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {n!} {0! \ n!}\)





Definition of Binomial Coefficient














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {n!} {1 \cdot n!}\)





Definition of Factorial of $0$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1\)









$\blacksquare$
