Example of Half-Open Real Interval
Let $I$ be the unbounded closed real interval defined as:

$I := \hointr 1 2$
Then $1 \in I$.


Proof
By definition of open real interval:

$I = \set {x \in \R: 1 \le x < 2}$
As $1 \le 1$ it follows that $1 \in I$.
$\blacksquare$
