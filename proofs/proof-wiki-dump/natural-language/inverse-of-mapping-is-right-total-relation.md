Theorem
Let $f$ be a mapping.

Then its inverse $f^{-1}$ is a right-total relation.


Proof
We have that $f$ is a mapping.
Hence $f$ is a fortiori a left-total relation.
Then from Inverse of Left-Total Relation is Right-Total, $f^{-1}$ is right-total.
$\blacksquare$
