Theorem
$\csc 195^\circ = \csc \dfrac {13 \pi} {12} = - \left({\sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2}\right)$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 195^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \csc \left({360^\circ - 165^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 165^\circ\)





Cosecant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds - \left({\sqrt 6 + \sqrt 2}\right)\)





Cosecant of 165 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
