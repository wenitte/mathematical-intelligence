Example of Common Logarithm
The common logarithm of $0 \cdotp 236$ is:

$\log_{10} 0 \cdotp 236 = \overline 1 \cdotp 3729 = -0.6271$


Proof













\(\ds 0 \cdotp 236\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \cdotp 36 \times 10^{-1}\)





using scientific notation








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \log_{10} 0 \cdotp 236\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map {\log_{10} } {2 \cdotp 36 \times 10^{-1} }\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \log_{10} 2 \cdotp 36 + \log_{10} 10^{-1}\)





Logarithm of Product














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 0 \cdotp 3729 + \paren {-1}\)





Common Logarithm of $2 \cdotp 36$, Definition of Common Logarithm














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \overline 1 \cdotp 3729\)





Notation for Negative Logarithm














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -0.6271\)









$\blacksquare$
