Theorem
In every group, the identity, and only the identity, has order $1$.


Proof
Let $G$ be a group with identity $e$.
Then:

$e^1 = e$
and:

$\forall a \in G: a \ne e: a^1 = a \ne e$.
Hence the result.
$\blacksquare$
