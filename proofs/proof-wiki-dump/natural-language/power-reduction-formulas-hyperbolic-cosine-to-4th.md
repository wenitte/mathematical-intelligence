Theorem
$\cosh^4 x = \dfrac {3 + 4 \cosh 2 x + \cosh 4 x} 8$
where $\cos$ denotes hyperbolic cosine.


Proof 1













\(\ds \cosh 4 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\cosh^2 x}^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\frac {\cosh 2 x + 1} 2}^2\)





Square of Hyperbolic Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cosh^2 2 x + 2 \cosh 2 x + 1} 4\)





multiplying out














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\frac {\cosh 4 x + 1} 2 + 2 \cosh 2 x + 1} 4\)





Square of Hyperbolic Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\cosh 4 x + 1 + 4 \cosh 2 x + 2} 8\)





multiplying top and bottom by $2$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {3 + 4 \cosh 2 x + \cosh 4 x} 8\)





rearrangement



$\blacksquare$


Proof 2













\(\ds \cosh^4 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {2^4}\left(e^{x} + e^{-x}\right)^4\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 {16} \left({e^{4 x} + 4 e^{2 x} + 6 e^{0 x} + 4 e^{-2 x} + e^{-4 x} }\right)\)





Binomial Theorem














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 8 \left({\frac{e^{4 x} + e^{-4 x} } 2}\right) + \frac 4 8 \left({\frac{e^{2 x} + e^{-2 x} } 2 }\right) + \frac 6 {16}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {3 + 4 \cosh 2 x + \cosh 4 x} 8\)





Definition of Hyperbolic Cosine



$\blacksquare$


Proof 3













\(\ds \cosh^4 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos^4 i x\)





Hyperbolic Cosine in terms of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {3 + 4 \cos \paren {2 i x} + \cos \paren {4 i x} } 8\)





Fourth Power of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {3 + 4 \cosh 2 x + \cosh 4 x} 8\)





Hyperbolic Cosine in terms of Cosine



$\blacksquare$
