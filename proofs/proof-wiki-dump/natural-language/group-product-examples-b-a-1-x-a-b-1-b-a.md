Examples of Operations on Product Elements
Solve for $x$ in:

$b a^{-1} x a b^{-1} = b a$


Solution













\(\ds b a^{-1} x a b^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds b a\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds b^{-1} b a^{-1} x a b^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds b^{-1} b a\)





Product of both sides with $b^{-1}$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds b^{-1} b a^{-1} x a b^{-1} b\)

\(=\)







\(\ds b^{-1} b a b\)





Product of both sides with $b$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds a^{-1} x a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a b\)





Group Axiom $\text G 3$: Existence of Inverse Element








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds a a^{-1} x a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^2 b\)





Product of both sides with $a$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds a a^{-1} x a a^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^2 b a^{-1}\)





Product of both sides with $a^{-1}$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^2 b a^{-1}\)





Group Axiom $\text G 3$: Existence of Inverse Element



$\blacksquare$
