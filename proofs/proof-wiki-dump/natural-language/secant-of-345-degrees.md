Theorem
$\sec 345 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {23 \pi} {12} = \sqrt 6 - \sqrt 2$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 345 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 15 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 15 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt 6 - \sqrt 2\)





Secant of $15 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
