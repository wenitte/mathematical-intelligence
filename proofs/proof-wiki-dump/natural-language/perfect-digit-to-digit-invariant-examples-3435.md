Example of Perfect Digit-to-Digit Invariant
$3435$ is a perfect digit-to-digit invariant:

$3435 = 3^3 + 4^4 + 3^3 + 5^5$


Proof













\(\ds \)

\(\)







\(\ds 3^3 + 4^4 + 3^3 + 5^5\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 27 + 256 + 27 + 3125\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3435\)









$\blacksquare$
