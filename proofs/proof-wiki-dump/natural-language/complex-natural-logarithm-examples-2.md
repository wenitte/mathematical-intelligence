Examples of Complex Natural Logarithm
$\ln \paren {-2} = \ln 2 + \paren {2 k + 1} \pi i$
for all $k \in \Z$.


Proof













\(\ds -2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 e^{i \pi}\)





Euler's Identity








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \ln \paren {-2}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln \paren {2 e^{i \pi + 2 k \pi i} }\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln 2 + i \pi + 2 k \pi i\)





Definition of Complex Natural Logarithm














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \ln 2 + \paren {2 k + 1} \pi i\)









$\blacksquare$
