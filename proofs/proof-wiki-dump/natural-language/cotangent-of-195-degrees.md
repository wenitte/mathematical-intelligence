Theorem
$\cot 195 \degrees = \cot \dfrac {13 \pi} {12} = 2 + \sqrt 3$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 195 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cot {360 \degrees - 165 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 165 \degrees\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 + \sqrt 3\)





Cotangent of $165 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
