Theorem
$\cot 330^\circ = \cot \dfrac {11 \pi} 6 = -\sqrt 3$
where $\cot$ denotes cotangent.


Proof













\(\ds \cot 330^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cot \left({360^\circ - 30^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\cot 30^\circ\)





Cotangent of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sqrt 3\)





Cotangent of 30 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
