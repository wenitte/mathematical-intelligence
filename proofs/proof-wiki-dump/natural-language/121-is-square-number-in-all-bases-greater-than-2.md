Theorem
Let $b \in \Z$ be an integer such that $b \ge 3$.
Let $n$ be a positive integer which can be expressed in base $b$ as $121_b$.

Then $n$ is a square number.


Proof
Consider $11_b$.
By the Basis Representation Theorem:

$11_b = b + 1$
Thus:














\(\ds {11_b}^2\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {b + 1}^2\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds b^2 + 2 b + 1\)





Square of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 121_b\)









Thus:

$121_b = {11_b}^2$
and so is a square number.
$\blacksquare$
