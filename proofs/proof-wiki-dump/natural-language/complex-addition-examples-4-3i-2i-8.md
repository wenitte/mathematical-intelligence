Example of Complex Addition
$\paren {4 - 3 i} + \paren {2 i - 8} = -4 - i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {4 - 3 i} + \paren {2 i - 8}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {4 - 8} + \paren {-3 + 2} i\)





Definition of Complex Addition














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -4 - i\)









$\blacksquare$
