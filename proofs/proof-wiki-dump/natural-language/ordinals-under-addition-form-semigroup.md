Theorem
$\struct {\On, +}$ forms an semigroup, where:

$\On$ denotes the class of all ordinals
$+$ denotes ordinal addition.


Proof
The result follows from Ordinal Addition is Closed and Ordinal Addition is Associative.
$\blacksquare$
