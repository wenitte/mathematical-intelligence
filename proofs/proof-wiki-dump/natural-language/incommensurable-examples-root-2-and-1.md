Example of Incommensurability
$\sqrt 2$ and $1$ are incommensurable.


Proof
From Square Root of 2 is Irrational, the ratio $\sqrt 2$ to $1$ is irrational.
Hence the result by definition of incommensurable.
$\blacksquare$
