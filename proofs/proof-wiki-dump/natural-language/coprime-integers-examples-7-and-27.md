Example of Coprime Integers
$7$ and $27$ are coprime.


Proof
The divisors of $7$ are $1$ and $7$.
The divisors of $27$ are $1$, $3$, $9$ and $27$.
The only integer in common between them is $1$.
Hence the result by definition of coprime.
$\blacksquare$
