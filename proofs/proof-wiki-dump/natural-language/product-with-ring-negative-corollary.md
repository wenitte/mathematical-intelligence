Corollary to Product with Ring Negative
Let $\struct {R, +, \circ}$ be a ring with unity $1_R$.
Then:

$\forall x \in R: \paren {-1_R} \circ x = -x$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {-1_R} \circ x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\paren {1_R \circ x}\)





Product with Ring Negative














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -x\)





Definition of Unity of Ring



$\blacksquare$
