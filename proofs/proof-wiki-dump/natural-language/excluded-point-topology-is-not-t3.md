Theorem
Let $T = \struct {S, \tau_{\bar p} }$ be a excluded point space.
Then $T$ is not a $T_3$ space.


Proof
Excluded Point Topology is Open Extension Topology of Discrete Topology
Open Extension Topology is not T3
$\blacksquare$
