Theorem
Let $a$ be a set.

Then the singleton set $\set a$ may be constructed such that:

$a \in \set a$


Proof
Let $a$ be a set.
From the Axiom of Pairing the set $\set {a, a}$ may be formed.
From the Axiom of Extension it follows that:

$\set {a, a} = \set a$
$\blacksquare$
