Theorem
$\cosh^4 x = \dfrac {3 + 4 \cosh 2 x + \cosh 4 x} 8$


Proof













\(\ds \cosh^4 x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos^4 i x\)





Hyperbolic Cosine in terms of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {3 + 4 \cos \paren {2 i x} + \cos \paren {4 i x} } 8\)





Fourth Power of Cosine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {3 + 4 \cosh 2 x + \cosh 4 x} 8\)





Hyperbolic Cosine in terms of Cosine



$\blacksquare$
