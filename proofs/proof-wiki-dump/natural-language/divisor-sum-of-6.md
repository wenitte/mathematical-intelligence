Example of Divisor Sum of Integer
$\map {\sigma_1} 6 = 12$
where $\sigma_1$ denotes the divisor sum function.


Proof
We have that:

$6 = 2 \times 3$
Hence:














\(\ds \map {\sigma_1} 6\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2 + 1} \paren {3 + 1}\)





Divisor Sum of Non-Square Semiprime














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3 \times 4\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^2 \times 3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 12\)









$\blacksquare$
