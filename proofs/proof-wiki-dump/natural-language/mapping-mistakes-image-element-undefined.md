Example of Mistake in Definition of Mapping
This example of an attempted definition of a mapping contains a mistake.

$g: \R \to \R$ defined as: $\forall x \in \R: x \mapsto \dfrac 1 x$


Explanation
The image of the element $0 \in \R$ is undefined:

$g \paren 0 = \dfrac 1 0$
$\blacksquare$
