Theorem
$\ds \int_0^\infty \frac x {\sinh a x} \rd x = \frac {\pi^2} {4 a^2}$
where $a$ is a positive real number.


Proof













\(\ds \int_0^\infty \frac x {\sinh a x} \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2^2 - 1} {2 a^2} \map \Gamma 2 \map \zeta 2\)





Definite Integral to Infinity of $\dfrac {x^n} {\sinh a x}$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 3 {2 a^2} \times 1! \times \frac {\pi^2} 6\)





Gamma Function Extends Factorial, Basel Problem














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac \pi {4 a^2}\)









$\blacksquare$
