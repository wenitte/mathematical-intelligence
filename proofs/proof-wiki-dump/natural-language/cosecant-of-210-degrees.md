Theorem
$\csc 210 \degrees = \csc \dfrac {7 \pi} 6 = -2$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 210 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \csc {360 \degrees - 150 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 150 \degrees\)





Cosecant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -2\)





Cosecant of $150 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
