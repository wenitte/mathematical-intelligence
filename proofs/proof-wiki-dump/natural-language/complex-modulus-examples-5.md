Example of Complex Modulus
$\left\vert{-5}\right\vert = 5$


Proof













\(\ds \left\vert{-5}\right\vert\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \left\vert{-5 + 0 i}\right\vert\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {\left({-5}\right)^2 + 0^2}\)





Definition of Complex Modulus














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {25}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5\)









$\blacksquare$
