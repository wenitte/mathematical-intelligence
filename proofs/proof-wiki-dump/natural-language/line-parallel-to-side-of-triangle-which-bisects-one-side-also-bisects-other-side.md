Theorem
Let $ABC$ be a triangle.
Let $DE$ be a straight line parallel to $BC$.
Let $DE$ bisect $AB$.
Then $DE$ also bisects $AC$.

That is, $DE$ is a midline of $\triangle ABC$.



Proof
This is a direct application of the Parallel Transversal Theorem.
$\blacksquare$
