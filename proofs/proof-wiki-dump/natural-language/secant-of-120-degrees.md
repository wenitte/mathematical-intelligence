Theorem
$\sec 120 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {2 \pi} 3 = -2$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 120 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {90 \degrees + 30 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 30 \degrees\)





Secant of Angle plus Right Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -2\)





Cosecant of $30 \degrees$



$\blacksquare$
