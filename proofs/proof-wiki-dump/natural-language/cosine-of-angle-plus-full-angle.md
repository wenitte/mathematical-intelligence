Theorem
$\map \cos {x + 2 \pi} = \cos x$


Corollary
Let $n \in \Z$ be an integer.
Then:

$\map \cos {x + 2 n \pi} = \cos x$


Proof













\(\ds \map \cos {x + 2 \pi}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos x \cos 2 \pi - \sin x \sin 2 \pi\)





Cosine of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos x \cdot 1 - \sin x \cdot 0\)





Cosine of Full Angle and Sine of Full Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos x\)









$\blacksquare$
