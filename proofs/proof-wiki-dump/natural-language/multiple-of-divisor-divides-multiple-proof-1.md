Theorem
Let $a, b, c \in \Z$.
Let:

$a \divides b$
where $\divides$ denotes divisibility.

Then:

$a c \divides b c$


Proof
We have that Integers form Integral Domain.
The result then follows from Multiple of Divisor in Integral Domain Divides Multiple.
$\blacksquare$
