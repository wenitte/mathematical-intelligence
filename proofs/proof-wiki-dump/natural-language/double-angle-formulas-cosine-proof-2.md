Theorem
$\cos 2 \theta = \cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta$


Proof













\(\ds \cos 2 \theta\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \cos {\theta + \theta}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos \theta \cos \theta - \sin \theta \sin \theta\)





Cosine of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta\)









$\blacksquare$
