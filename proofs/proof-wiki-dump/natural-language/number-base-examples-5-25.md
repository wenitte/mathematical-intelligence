Example of Number expressed in Base $5$ Notation
The integer expressed in decimal as $25$ is expressed in base $5$ as $100_5$.


Proof













\(\ds 25\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 \times 5^2 + 0 \times 5 + 0\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 25 + 0 + 0\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 100_5\)









$\blacksquare$
