Examples of Use of Euclidean Algorithm
The GCD of $52$ and $273$ is found to be:

$\gcd \set {52, 273} = 13$


Proof



\(\text {(1)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 273\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5 \times 52 + 13\)










\(\text {(2)}: \quad\)









\(\ds 52\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 4 \times 13\)










Thus:

$\gcd \set {52, 273} = 13$
$\blacksquare$
