Theorem
$\floor {-\dfrac 1 2} = -1$
where $\floor x$ denotes the floor of $x$.


Proof
We have that:

$-1 = -\dfrac 2 2 \le -\dfrac 1 2$
and:

$0 > -\dfrac 1 2$
Hence $-1$ is the floor of $-\dfrac 1 2$ by definition.
$\blacksquare$
