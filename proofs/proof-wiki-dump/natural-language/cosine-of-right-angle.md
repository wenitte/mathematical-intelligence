Theorem
$\cos 90 \degrees = \cos \dfrac \pi 2 = 0$
where $\cos$ denotes cosine.


Proof
A direct implementation of Cosine of Half-Integer Multiple of Pi:

$\forall n \in \Z: \map \cos {n + \dfrac 1 2} \pi = 0$
In this case, $n = 0$ and so:

$\cos \dfrac 1 2 \pi = 0$
$\blacksquare$
