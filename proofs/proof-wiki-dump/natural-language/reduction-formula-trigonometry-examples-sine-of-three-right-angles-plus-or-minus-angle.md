Example of Reduction Formula in context of Trigonometry
$\map \sin {270 \degrees \pm \theta} = -\cos \theta$


Proof
From Sine of Angle plus Three Right Angles:

$\map \sin {270 \degrees + \theta} = -\cos \theta$
From Sine of Three Right Angles minus Angle:

$\map \sin {270 \degrees - \theta} = -\cos \theta$
$\blacksquare$
