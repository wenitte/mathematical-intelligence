Theorem
Let $\struct {S, \preceq}$ be a totally ordered set. 
Then the max semigroup $\struct {S, \max}$ is a semilattice.


Proof
The Max Semigroup is Commutative and idempotent. 
Hence the result, by definition of a semilattice.
$\blacksquare$
