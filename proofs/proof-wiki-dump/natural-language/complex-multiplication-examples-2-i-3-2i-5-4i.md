Example of Complex Multiplication
$\paren {2 - i} \paren {\paren {-3 + 2 i} \paren {5 - 4 i} } = 8 + 51 i$


Proof













\(\ds \paren {2 - i} \paren {\paren {-3 + 2 i} \paren {5 - 4 i} }\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2 - i} \paren {\paren {\paren {-3} \times 5 - 2 \times \paren {-4} } + \paren {2 \times 5 + \paren {-3} \times \paren {-4} } i}\)





Definition of Complex Multiplication














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2 - i} \paren {\paren {-15 + 8} + \paren {10 + 12} i}\)





simplification














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2 - i} \paren {-7 + 22 i}\)





simplification














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {2 \times \paren {-7} - \paren {-1} \times 22} + \paren {\paren {-1} \times \paren {-7} + 2 \times 22} i\)





Definition of Complex Multiplication














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {-14 + 22} + \paren {7 + 44} i\)





simplification














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 8 + 51 i\)









$\blacksquare$
