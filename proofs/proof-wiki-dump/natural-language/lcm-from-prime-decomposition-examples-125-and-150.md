Example of Use of LCM from Prime Decomposition
The lowest common multiple of $125$ and $150$ is:

$\lcm \set {125, 150} = 750$


Proof













\(\ds 125\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5^3\)




















\(\ds 150\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \times 3 \times 5^2\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 125\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^0 \times 3^0 \times 5^3\)




















\(\ds 150\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^1 \times 3^1 \times 5^2\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \lcm \set {125, 150}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^1 \times 3^1 \times 5^3\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 750\)









$\blacksquare$
