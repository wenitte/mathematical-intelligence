Theorem
$\ds \int \tanh^2 x \rd x = x - \tanh x + C$
where $C$ is an arbitrary constant.


Proof













\(\ds \int \tanh^2 x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int \paren {1 - \sech^2 x} \rd x\)





Sum of Squares of Hyperbolic Secant and Tangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int 1 \rd x - \int \sech^2 x \rd x\)





Linear Combination of Primitives














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \int 1 \rd x - \tanh x + C\)





Primitive of Square of Hyperbolic Secant Function














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds x - \tanh x + C\)





Primitive of Constant



$\blacksquare$
