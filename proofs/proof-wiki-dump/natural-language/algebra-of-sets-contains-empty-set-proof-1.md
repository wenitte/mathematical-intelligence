Theorem
Let $S$ be a set.
Let $\RR$ be an algebra of sets on $S$.
Then:

$\O \in \RR$
where $\O$ denotes the empty set.


Proof
By definition $2$ of Algebra of Sets:

$\RR$ is a ring of sets with a unit.
By definition $2$ of Ring of Sets:




\((\text {RS} 1_2)\)  

$:$  



Empty Set:   



\(\ds \O \in \RR \)   







  

$\blacksquare$
