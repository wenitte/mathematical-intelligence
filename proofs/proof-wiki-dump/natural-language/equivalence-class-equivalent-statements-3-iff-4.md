Theorem
Let $\RR$ be an equivalence relation on $S$.
Let $x, y \in S$.
The following statements are equivalent:

$x \mathrel \RR y$
$x \in \eqclass y \RR$


Proof
This follows directly by the definition of equivalence class.
$\blacksquare$
