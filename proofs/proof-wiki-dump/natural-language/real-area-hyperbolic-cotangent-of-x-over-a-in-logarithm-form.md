Theorem
$\arcoth \dfrac x a = \dfrac 1 2 \map \ln {\dfrac {x + a} {x - a} }$


Proof













\(\ds \arcoth \frac x a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 2 \map \ln {\frac {\frac x a + 1} {\frac x a - 1} }\)





Definition of Real Area Hyperbolic Cotangent














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac 1 2 \map \ln {\frac {x + a} {x - a} }\)





multiplying top and bottom by $a$



$\blacksquare$
