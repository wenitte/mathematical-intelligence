Corollary to Cube of Sum
$\paren {x + 1}^3 = x^3 + 3 x^2 + 3 x + 1$


Proof
Follows directly from the Cube of Sum:

$\paren {x + y}^3 = x^3 + 3 x^2 y + 3 x y^2 + y^3$
setting $y = 1$.
$\blacksquare$
