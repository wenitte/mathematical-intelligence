Theorem
Let $M = \struct {A, d}$ be a metric space.
Then $A$ is both open and closed in $M$.


Proof
From Metric Space is Open in Itself, $A$ is open in $M$.
From Metric Space is Closed in Itself, $A$ is closed in $M$.
$\blacksquare$
