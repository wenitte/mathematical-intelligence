Theorem
$\tan 2 \theta = \dfrac {2 \tan \theta} {1 - \tan^2 \theta}$


Proof













\(\ds \tan 2 \theta\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \tan {\theta + \theta}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {\tan \theta + \tan \theta} {1 - \tan \theta \tan \theta}\)





Tangent of Sum














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \frac {2 \tan \theta} {1 - \tan^2 \theta}\)









$\blacksquare$
