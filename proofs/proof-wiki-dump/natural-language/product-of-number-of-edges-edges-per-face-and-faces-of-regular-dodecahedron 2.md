Theorem
The product of the number of edges, edges per face and faces of a regular dodecahedron is $1800$.


Proof
A regular dodecahedron has:

$30$ edges
and:

$12$ faces.
Each face is a pentagon, and so has $5$ edges.
Hence:

$30 \times 12 \times 5 = 1800$
$\blacksquare$
