Example of Rational Power
$32^{6/5} = 64$


Proof













\(\ds 32^{6/5}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {\paren {2^5}^6}^{1/5}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2^6\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 64\)









$\blacksquare$
