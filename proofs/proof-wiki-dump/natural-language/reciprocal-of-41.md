Theorem
The decimal expansion of the reciprocal of $41$ is as follows:

$\dfrac 1 {41} = 0 \cdotp \dot 0243 \dot 9$
This sequence is A021045 in the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (N. J. A. Sloane (Ed.), 2008).
