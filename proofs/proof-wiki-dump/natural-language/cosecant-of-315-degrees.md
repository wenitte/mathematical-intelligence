Theorem
$\csc 315^\circ = \csc \dfrac {7 \pi} 4 = -\sqrt 2$
where $\csc$ denotes cosecant.


Proof













\(\ds \csc 315^\circ\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \csc \left({360^\circ - 45^\circ}\right)\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csc 45^\circ\)





Cosecant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sqrt 2\)





Cosecant of 45 Degrees



$\blacksquare$
