Theorem
$\forall a, x \in \R, y \in \R_{\ne 0}: \dfrac {a \times x} y = a \times \dfrac x y$


Proof













\(\ds \frac {a \times x} y\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \paren {a \times x} \times \frac 1 y\)





Definition of Real Division














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds a \times \paren {x \times \frac 1 y}\)





Real Number Axiom $\R \text M1$: Associativity of Multiplication














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds a \times \frac x y\)





Definition of Real Division



$\blacksquare$
