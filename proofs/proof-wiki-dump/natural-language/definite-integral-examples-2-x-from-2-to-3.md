Example of Definite Integral
$\ds \int_2^3 2 x \rd x = 5$


Proof













\(\ds \int_2^3 2 x \rd x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \bigintlimits {x^2} 2 3\)





Primitive of Power














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^2 - 2^2\)





Definition of Definite Integral














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5\)





evaluating



$\blacksquare$
