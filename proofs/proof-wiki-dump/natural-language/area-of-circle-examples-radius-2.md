Example of Area of Circle
Let $C$ be a circle whose radius is $2$.
Then the area of $C$ is $4 \pi$.


Proof
From Area of Circle:

$A = \pi r^2$
Hence when $r = 2$ we have:

$A = \pi \times 2^2 = 4 \pi$
$\blacksquare$
