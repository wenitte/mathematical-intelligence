Theorem
$\sec 225 \degrees = \sec \dfrac {5 \pi} 4 = -\sqrt 2$
where $\sec$ denotes secant.


Proof













\(\ds \sec 225 \degrees\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \map \sec {360 \degrees - 135 \degrees}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sec 135 \degrees\)





Secant of Conjugate Angle














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\sqrt 2\)





Secant of $135 degrees$



$\blacksquare$
