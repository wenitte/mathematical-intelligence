Example of Complex Modulus
$\cmod {4 + 3 i} = 5$


Proof













\(\ds \cmod {4 + 3 i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {4^2 + 3^2}\)





Definition of Complex Modulus














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {25}\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 5\)









$\blacksquare$
