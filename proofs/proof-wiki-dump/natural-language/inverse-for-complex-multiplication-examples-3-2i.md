Example of Inverse for Complex Multiplication
$\dfrac 1 {3 + 2 i} = \dfrac 3 {13} + \dfrac {2 i} {13}$


Proof













\(\ds \dfrac 1 {3 + 2 i}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {3 - 2 i} {\paren {3 + 2 i} \paren {3 - 2 i} }\)





multiplying top and bottom by $3 - 2 i$














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {3 - 2 i} {3^2 + 2^2}\)





simplifying














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds \dfrac {3 - 2 i} {13}\)





simplifying



$\blacksquare$
