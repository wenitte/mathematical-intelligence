Theorem
$\map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\csch a x} = -a \csch a x \coth a x$


Proof













\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\csch x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -\csch x \coth x\)





Derivative of $\csch x$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \map {\dfrac \d {\d x} } {\csch a x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds -a \csch a x \coth a x\)





Derivative of Function of Constant Multiple



$\blacksquare$
