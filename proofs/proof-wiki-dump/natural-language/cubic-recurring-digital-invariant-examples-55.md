Example of Cubic Recurring Digital Invariant
$55$ is a cubic recurring digital invariant:








\(\ds 55: \ \ \)





\(\ds 5^3 + 5^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 125 + 125\)

\(\ds = 250\)












\(\ds 250: \ \ \)





\(\ds 2^3 + 5^3 + 0^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 8 + 125 + 0\)

\(\ds = 133\)












\(\ds 133: \ \ \)





\(\ds 1^3 + 3^3 + 3^3\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 1 + 27 + 27\)

\(\ds = 55\)







$\blacksquare$
