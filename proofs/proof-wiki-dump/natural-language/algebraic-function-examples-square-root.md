Examples of Algebraic Functions
Let $f: \C \to \C$ be the complex function:

$\forall z \in \C: \map f z = z^{1/2}$
Then $f$ is an algebraic function.


Proof
We have that $w = z^{1/2}$ is a solution to the polynomial equation:

$w^2 - z = 0$
The result follows.
$\blacksquare$
