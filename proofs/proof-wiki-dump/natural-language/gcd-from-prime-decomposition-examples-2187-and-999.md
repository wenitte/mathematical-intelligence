Example of Use of GCD from Prime Decomposition
The greatest common divisor of $2187$ and $999$ is:

$\gcd \set {2187, 999} = 27$


Proof













\(\ds 2187\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^7\)




















\(\ds 999\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^3 \times 37\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 2187\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^7 \times 37^0\)




















\(\ds 999\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^3 \times 37^1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \gcd \set {2187, 999}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 3^3 \times 37^0\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 27\)









$\blacksquare$
