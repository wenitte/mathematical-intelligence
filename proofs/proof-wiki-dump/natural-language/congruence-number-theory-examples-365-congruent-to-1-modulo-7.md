Example of Congruence Modulo an Integer
$365 \equiv 1 \pmod 7$


Proof













\(\ds 365\)

\(=\)







\(\ds 52 \times 7 + 1\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds 365\)

\(\equiv\)







\(\ds 1\)

\(\ds \pmod 7\)







$\blacksquare$
