Example of Use of Real Arccosine
$\map \sin {2 \arccos x}$
can be simplified to:

$2 x \sqrt {1 - x^2}$


Proof
Let $\arccos x = \theta$.
Then:














\(\ds \cos \theta\)

\(=\)







\(\ds x\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds \sin \theta\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sqrt {1 - x^2}\)





Sum of Squares of Sine and Cosine




Then:














\(\ds \map \sin {2 \arccos x}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds \sin 2 \theta\)




















\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 \sin \theta \cos \theta\)





Double Angle Formula for Sine














\(\ds \)

\(=\)







\(\ds 2 x \sqrt {1 - x^2}\)









$\blacksquare$
