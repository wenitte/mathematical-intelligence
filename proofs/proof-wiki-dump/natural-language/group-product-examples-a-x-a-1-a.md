Examples of Operations on Product Elements
Solve for $x$ in:

$a x a^{-1} = a$


Solution













\(\ds a x a^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a\)














\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds a^{-1} a x a^{-1}\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^{-1} a\)





Product of both sides with $a^{-1}$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds a^{-1} a x a^{-1} a\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^{-1} a a\)





Product of both sides with $a$








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a^{-1} a a\)





Group Axiom $\text G 3$: Existence of Inverse Element








\(\ds \leadsto \ \ \)





\(\ds x\)

\(=\)







\(\ds a\)





Group Axiom $\text G 3$: Existence of Inverse Element



$\blacksquare$
